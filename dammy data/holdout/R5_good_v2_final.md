## 1. The problem we are solving

Tsumiki's downloads plateaued at 90k, but download is not where the funnel breaks.
The client's own analytics show 61% of downloaders abandon at KYC identity
verification, and only 33% of accounts ever get funded — against a 48% neobroker
benchmark. Buying more download ads pours water into a leaking bucket: at current
KYC completion, doubling downloads adds installs, not investors.

**Problem statement: Tsumiki doesn't need more people to download it — it needs the
people who download it to survive identity verification and fund an account.**

## 2. Target: one segment, deliberately narrow

**Primary: salaried employees 25–29 who searched "NISA" or a NISA-comparison term
in the last 6 months but never opened any investment account** (est. 2.1M people,
reachable via search-intent and finance-content audiences).

**Who we are NOT targeting (and why):**
- Active traders / existing NISA holders (wrong product — Tsumiki's ¥100 micro-lots
  underserve them; they churn within 60 days per client cohort data)
- Students under 22 (funded-account value averages ¥3,100 — economics never clear a
  ¥100M budget)

## 3. Strategy

Fix the leak before filling the bucket: **"From download to first investment in
one lunch break."** Lead with KYC-completion proof (median 9 minutes, client-verified),
then scale acquisition only into a funnel that converts.

## 4. Executions

- KYC flow messaging rework + "what you'll need" pre-checklist ad units (cuts
  abandonment drivers identified in the client's drop-off analysis)
- 15s proof video: real-time screen capture, download → funded in 9 minutes
- Search + finance-content ads against NISA-intent audiences with ¥100-start offer
- Creative batched into 3 waves of 4 assets each, submitted 10 business days ahead
  of flight to absorb the compliance review without pausing media

## 5. Media plan: sequenced, gated

1. Weeks 1–4: KYC completion UX/messaging fixes + proof content to intent audiences only
2. Weeks 5–8: scale acquisition spend ONLY if funded-account rate clears 40% in cohort
3. Weeks 9–24: broaden to adjacent 30–34 lookalikes if CPA holds under ¥550/download

## 6. KPIs

- Cumulative downloads: 90k → 180k by March 2026
- Funded-account rate: 33% → 40%+ (the gate for scaling spend)
- KYC completion: 39% → 60% by week 8
