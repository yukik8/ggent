# Client Brief — R4

- Client: Sakura Mobility Inc. (mobility)
- Received: 2025-10-01
- Contact: CMO office

## The ask

We launch **Hana EV**, a compact electric vehicle (¥2.1M after subsidies, 250km range,
designed for daily errands) in April 2026. We need a pre-launch awareness campaign.
EV consideration in our category is low; people think EVs are expensive and impractical.

- Product: Hana EV (compact EV)
- Target (client's words): "families thinking about their next car"
- Budget: ¥200,000,000
- Timing: January–April 2026
- Success: aided awareness 35%+ in launch prefectures; 8,000 test-drive bookings

## Notes from first meeting

Dealer network is thin outside metro areas — test drives concentrate in 6 prefectures.
Charging anxiety is the #1 stated objection in the client's own clinic surveys (62% of respondents).
