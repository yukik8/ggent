# Client Brief — R2

- Client: Hoshino Beverage Co. (beverage / FMCG)
- Received: 2025-09-12
- Contact: Marketing Director, Dairy Alternatives Div.

## The ask

Our oat-based yogurt **OatVita** launched two years ago and has stalled at 1.2% category
share. We want a relaunch campaign to make OatVita the default plant-based yogurt choice.

- Product: OatVita (oat-milk yogurt, 3 flavors, ¥198)
- Target (client's words): "health-conscious consumers of all ages"
- Budget: ¥50,000,000
- Timing: relaunch January 2026 (new packaging ships then)
- Success: category share to 3% within 6 months; repeat purchase rate

## Notes from first meeting

Shopper data shows trial is fine but repeat is weak (28% vs category average 41%).
Client suspects taste perception ("oat = watery") is the barrier but has no research to confirm.
