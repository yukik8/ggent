# Client Brief — R1

- Client: Hoshino Beverage Co. (beverage / FMCG)
- Received: 2025-08-04
- Contact: Brand Manager, RTD Coffee Div.

## The ask

We are launching **KURO Latte**, a premium canned craft latte (single-origin beans,
oat-milk option, ¥248 retail vs ¥140 for standard canned coffee). We want a launch
campaign that establishes KURO as "the craft coffee you can grab at a convenience store."

- Product: KURO Latte (canned craft latte, 2 SKUs)
- Target (client's words): "young adults who like nice coffee"
- Budget: ¥80,000,000
- Timing: launch mid-October 2025, campaign through December
- Success: sell-through at convenience stores; brand recall among target

## Notes from first meeting

Client worries the ¥248 price point will be seen as overpriced for a can.
Competitor "Craft Boss" owns the mainstream shelf; client wants to avoid a head-on price war.
