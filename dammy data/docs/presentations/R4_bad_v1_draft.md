# Hana EV Pre-Launch Awareness Proposal — Alternative Route (v1, shipped as-is)

- Requirement: R4 / Client: Sakura Mobility Inc.
- Author: Riku Tanaka (Planner)
- Date: 2025-10-20
- Status: presented to client — REJECTED
- Note: Sato-san was on the Hoshino Beverage account; this route went out without her review.

## 1. The Big Idea

**"For the Earth, For Tomorrow."**

Hana EV is more than a car — it's a promise to the planet. Every drive is a small
act of love for the future. We make choosing Hana EV feel like choosing hope itself.

## 2. Campaign concept

An emotional 60s film: a little girl plants a seedling; years pass in a single shot;
she grows up, and a Hana EV pulls up beside the now-towering tree. She smiles at
her own child in the back seat. Voiceover: "What we choose today grows tomorrow."
Tagline: "For the Earth, For Tomorrow."

## 3. Target

All families thinking about their next car, nationwide. Parents, grandparents,
newlyweds, commuters, weekend drivers. Everyone cares about the planet and everyone
needs a car — so Hana EV speaks to everyone.

## 4. Executions

- Emotional film (60s) in heavy national TV rotation
- Green-themed OOH domination in major stations across all 47 prefectures
- Newspaper full-page ads on environmental anniversaries
- Family-influencer program (100 parenting and lifestyle creators)
- "Tomorrow Forest" tree-planting PR event with celebrity guests

## 5. Media plan

National TV blitz from January, all channels, all dayparts, supported by OOH and
digital everywhere at once. Four months of total presence will make Hana EV a
household name before launch.

## 6. Why this wins

The environment is the issue of our time, and families feel it deeply. Our film
will bring tears to people's eyes, the tree-planting event will win the news cycle,
and Hana EV will be loved across Japan before a single car is delivered.
