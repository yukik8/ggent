## 1. The Big Idea

**"Banking That Works as Hard as You."**

Small business owners never stop, and neither should their bank. Aozora Biz is the
tireless partner behind every hustle — always on, always fast, always on their side.
We make Aozora Biz the badge of the hardest workers in Japan.

## 2. Campaign concept

Cinematic portraits of grit: a baker at 4 a.m., a delivery-company founder in the
rain, a freelance designer working late. Powerful music builds as their Aozora Biz
app quietly handles the money side. Respectful, emotional, premium — a bank that
truly gets them.

## 3. Target

All small business owners and freelancers nationwide — from solo creators to
50-person companies, every industry, every region. Hard work is universal, and so
is the need for a better bank.

## 4. Executions

- 60s and 30s hero films on TV and YouTube
- National newspaper and business-magazine placements
- Taxi and airport media for decision-makers
- SNS distribution across Instagram, X, LINE, and Facebook
- Launch PR event with well-known entrepreneur guests

## 5. Media plan

Big-bang national launch in January — every channel live simultaneously to dominate
the fiscal-year-end switching season. Maximum reach in the first month builds the
trust a bank needs, then sustained presence carries us to March.

## 6. Why this wins

No bank has ever truly celebrated the small business owner. An emotional, premium
campaign at national scale will make Aozora Biz the most talked-about bank of the
season, and openings will surge with the brand.
