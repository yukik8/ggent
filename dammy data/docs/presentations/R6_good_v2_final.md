# Aozora Biz Account Opening Proposal (final v2)

- Requirement: R6 / Client: Aozora Financial Group
- Author: Hana Yamamoto (Planner) / Revised: Ayaka Sato (Executive Strategy Director)
- Date: 2025-11-27
- Status: presented to client — SELECTED

## 1. The problem we are solving

Aozora Biz opens 800 accounts/month against a 2,000 target, but the barrier is not
awareness — aided awareness among SME owners is already 54%. The client's churned-lead
survey shows 71% of owners who considered switching stopped at one fear: payroll and
supplier payments breaking mid-migration. Meanwhile tax-accountant referrals already
drive 30% of openings at near-zero acquisition cost — the highest-leverage channel is
B2B2B, not broad advertising.

**Problem statement: Aozora Biz doesn't need more owners to know it exists — it needs
to remove the fear of switching and multiply the referral channel that already works.**

## 2. Target: one segment, deliberately narrow

**Primary: companies of 2–20 employees within 3 years of founding** (est. 310k firms;
banking habits not yet entrenched, no procurement committee, reachable via tax-accountant
networks and incorporation-service partnerships).

**Who we are NOT targeting (and why):**
- Freelancers / solo operators (average revenue per account ¥4,800/year — LTV never
  clears the ¥15,000 CAC cap)
- Larger SMEs, 50+ employees (bank switching runs through 6–12 month procurement
  cycles — decisions land outside the January–March window)

## 3. Strategy

Sell the safe migration, not the shiny bank: **"Switch without missing a single
payroll."** Lead with a migration-concierge promise (guided 15-minute setup, parallel-run
period, payroll dry-run checklist), amplified through the advisors owners already trust.

## 4. Executions

- Migration-concierge proof content: step-by-step switching guide + 3 documented case
  studies with payroll dates shown intact
- Tax-accountant partner program: co-branded switching kit, ¥8,000 referral fee, target
  400 active referring offices (from ~120 today)
- Incorporation-service integrations (2 platforms) placing Aozora Biz at company setup
- Retargeting only: search + LinkedIn/Facebook against switching-intent audiences

## 5. Media plan: sequenced, gated

1. Weeks 1–4: migration-concierge content live + partner program rollout — no paid media
2. Weeks 5–8: paid retargeting ONLY if blended CAC from partner channel holds under ¥11,000
3. Weeks 9–12: scale paid to broader SME-intent audiences if blended CAC stays under ¥15,000

## 6. KPIs

- Account openings: 800 → 2,000/month by March 2026
- Blended CAC: under ¥15,000 (paid-media gate at ¥11,000 partner-channel benchmark)
- Active referring tax-accountant offices: 120 → 400 by March

- Aozora acquisition-channel analysis, Sep 2025 (30% referral share, channel CAC)
