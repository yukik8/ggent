# OatVita Relaunch Proposal (draft v1)

- Requirement: R2 / Client: Hoshino Beverage Co.
- Author: Hana Yamamoto (Planner)
- Date: 2025-09-24
- Status: internal draft

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Kind to You, Kind to Earth."**

OatVita is more than yogurt — it's a lifestyle statement. Plant-based living is
booming, and OatVita becomes the badge of a kinder, healthier, more sustainable life.

## 2. Campaign concept

A warm, gentle brand film: morning light, a family sharing breakfast, oats swaying
in a field. Soft narration about choosing kindness. New packaging shown as the
centerpiece of a beautiful healthy table.

## 3. Target

Health-conscious consumers of all ages — young women into wellness, families wanting
healthier breakfasts, seniors watching cholesterol, flexitarians, vegans. Health is
for everyone.

## 4. Executions

- Brand film (30s) TV + YouTube
- Supermarket in-store displays with the new packaging
- Wellness influencer gifting (30 accounts)
- Recipe content series: "OatVita mornings"

## 5. Media plan

Launch all channels at once in January to ride New Year health resolutions.
Broad TV reach builds trust, digital adds engagement.

## 6. Why this wins

Sustainability and wellness are the biggest trends of the decade. The campaign feels
good, looks beautiful, and everyone will relate to it.
