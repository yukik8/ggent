# Tsumiki Download Campaign Proposal (draft v1)

- Requirement: R5 / Client: Aozora Financial Group
- Author: Riku Tanaka (Planner)
- Date: 2025-09-17
- Status: internal draft

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Small Steps, Big Dreams."**

Tsumiki is more than an app — it's the beginning of a better life. Investing feels
scary and complicated, but Tsumiki makes it friendly, warm, and effortless. From
just ¥100, anyone can start building their dream future.

## 2. Campaign concept

An uplifting brand film: a young person drops a coin into a jar, the jar becomes a
tree, the tree becomes a forest. Gentle piano, hopeful narration about how great
things grow from tiny beginnings. Bright, optimistic, approachable design language
everywhere — investing has never looked this friendly.

## 3. Target

Young people 20–39 who should invest but haven't started yet — new graduates,
young professionals, newlyweds, side-hustlers, anyone curious about money. The
future belongs to everyone, so the campaign should speak to everyone.

## 4. Executions

- Brand film (30s) TV + YouTube
- App-install ads across Instagram, TikTok, LINE, and X
- Finance influencer collabs (25 accounts) sharing their "first ¥100" moment
- Station domination in Shibuya and Shinjuku for launch buzz
- "Dream calculator" web content: see what your dreams could grow into

## 5. Media plan

Launch everything at once in October for maximum impact. Big simultaneous reach
creates conversation, and conversation creates downloads. Keep all channels running
through March so the momentum never drops.

## 6. Why this wins

Investing is booming among young people and Tsumiki is perfectly positioned. The
campaign is emotional, shareable, and feel-good — everyone who sees it will want
to take their first small step.
