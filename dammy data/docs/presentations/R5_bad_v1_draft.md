## 1. The Big Idea

**"Your Future Starts Today."**

Tsumiki turns everyday people into investors. We make investing exciting, modern,
and cool — the app that finally makes your money work for you, starting today.

## 2. Campaign concept

Energetic, aspirational visuals: young people in motion — commuting, working out,
grabbing coffee — while their Tsumiki balance quietly grows on screen. Bold
typography, upbeat music, a confident voice telling viewers the best time to start
was yesterday, and the second best time is today.

## 3. Target

Everyone 20–39 who hasn't started investing — students, first-jobbers, young
couples, freelancers, career-changers. Money anxiety is universal in this
generation, so the message will resonate across the board.

## 4. Executions

- 30s hero film on TV and YouTube
- Massive app-install blitz across TikTok, Instagram, LINE, and X
- Celebrity ambassador launch event with press coverage
- Out-of-home takeover in five major city stations
- Hashtag challenge: #MyFutureStartsToday

## 5. Media plan

Big-bang national launch in October — all channels live on day one. First-mover
noise wins the season; we dominate share of voice before competitors react and
keep spend heavy through December.

## 6. Why this wins

Young Japan is waking up to investing and Tsumiki has the friendliest product on
the market. Bold reach plus an inspiring message will make Tsumiki the app everyone
is talking about — and downloads will follow.
