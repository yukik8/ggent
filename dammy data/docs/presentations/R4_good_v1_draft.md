# Hana EV Pre-Launch Awareness Proposal (draft v1)

- Requirement: R4 / Client: Sakura Mobility Inc.
- Author: Hana Yamamoto (Planner)
- Date: 2025-10-14
- Status: internal draft

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Drive the Bloom."**

Hana EV isn't just a car — it's a flower blooming on four wheels. Fresh, joyful,
beautifully compact. We make electric driving feel like spring arriving early.

## 2. Campaign concept

A heartwarming brand film: cherry blossom petals swirl behind a Hana EV as it
glides silently through town — past a bakery, a school, a grandmother waving.
Everywhere it goes, color returns to the streets. The car that makes the whole
neighborhood smile.

## 3. Target

City people 20–49 and families thinking about their next car. Eco-conscious
millennials, young parents, design lovers, commuters, retirees downsizing —
anyone ready for a kinder, cleaner way to drive.

## 4. Executions

- Brand film (60s) for national TV and YouTube
- Beautiful floral OOH in major stations and shopping malls nationwide
- Instagram/TikTok: #DriveTheBloom photo contest
- Celebrity ambassador who loves nature (TBD)
- "Hana Garden" pop-up showcase in Roppongi with a real car covered in flowers

## 5. Media plan

Launch everything simultaneously in January for maximum impact. TV + OOH +
digital + the pop-up all at once makes Hana EV feel like a national moment
and gets the whole country talking before April.

## 6. Why this wins

EVs are the future and everyone knows it. Our campaign is emotional, gorgeous
and full of hope — people will fall in love with Hana EV at first sight, and
awareness will bloom naturally across Japan.
