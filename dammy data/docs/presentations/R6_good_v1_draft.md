# Aozora Biz Account Opening Proposal (draft v1)

- Requirement: R6 / Client: Aozora Financial Group
- Author: Hana Yamamoto (Planner)
- Date: 2025-11-20
- Status: internal draft

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Business, Unchained."**

Aozora Biz frees small business owners from old banking. No branches, no paperwork,
no waiting — just a bank that moves as fast as they do. We make Aozora Biz the
symbol of a new, liberated way of doing business.

## 2. Campaign concept

A stylish brand film: a founder closes her laptop at a beach café, an artisan sends
an invoice from his workshop, a food-truck owner gets paid on the spot. Freedom,
speed, independence. The tagline lands over a sunrise: business, unchained.

## 3. Target

Small business owners and freelancers everywhere — startup founders, shop owners,
craftspeople, consultants, creators, side-business salarymen. Every small business
deserves better banking, so we speak to all of them.

## 4. Executions

- Brand film (30s) on TV and YouTube
- Business-magazine and taxi-media placements for credibility
- SNS ads across Instagram, X, and LINE with founder stories
- Freelancer influencer collabs (20 accounts)
- "Freedom stories" owned-content series

## 5. Media plan

Launch all channels together in January to own the fiscal-year-end conversation.
Broad reach builds trust in a bank brand, and constant presence across every
touchpoint keeps Aozora Biz top of mind through March.

## 6. Why this wins

The way people work has changed forever, and banking must catch up. This campaign
is inspiring, modern, and speaks to the entrepreneurial spirit of the era — small
business owners will feel seen and switch.
