## 1. The Big Idea

**"The Future of Breakfast."**

OatVita leads the plant-based revolution. We position OatVita as the modern,
forward-thinking choice — the yogurt of tomorrow, available today.

## 2. Campaign concept

Sleek, futuristic visuals: minimalist kitchens, morning routines of stylish people,
drone shots of oat fields transitioning to city skylines. OatVita as the smart
choice of people who live ahead of the curve.

## 3. Target

Health-conscious consumers 20–60, urban and suburban. Anyone open to plant-based
eating: wellness enthusiasts, busy professionals, young families, active seniors.

## 4. Executions

- 30s TV spot in morning-show slots
- Transit ads in major stations
- Paid social (Instagram, LINE) with lifestyle creators
- New-packaging launch PR event with a celebrity ambassador

## 5. Media plan

National simultaneous launch in January. Health-resolution season means broad reach
converts best — maximize GRP in the first two weeks.

## 6. Why this wins

Plant-based is the future and consumers know it. Strong reach plus premium imagery
will lift the whole brand and drive share growth toward the 3% goal.
