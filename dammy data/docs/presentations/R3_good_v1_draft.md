# Glide Subscription Growth Proposal (draft v1)

- Requirement: R3 / Client: Sakura Mobility Inc.
- Author: Riku Tanaka (Planner)
- Date: 2025-09-02
- Status: internal draft

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Own the Streets."**

Glide isn't just a scooter — it's freedom on two wheels. We turn the daily commute
into an adventure. Fast, effortless, undeniably cool. The city becomes yours.

## 2. Campaign concept

An epic brand movie: a rider glides past a packed, sweaty train platform at golden
hour, wind in their hair, headphones on, weaving through the waking city. The train
crowd watches through the window. Tagline lands: "Own the Streets." The CEO wants a
cool brand movie, and this delivers exactly that — cinematic, stylish, shareable.

## 3. Target

City people 20–49 who hate crowded trains. Young professionals, students, creatives,
delivery workers, weekend explorers — anyone tired of the commute grind and hungry
for a smarter, cooler way to move around the city.

## 4. Executions

- Brand film (90s) for TV, YouTube and cinema pre-roll
- Massive OOH takeover in Shibuya, Shinjuku and Umeda stations
- Instagram/TikTok: #OwnTheStreets rider UGC challenge
- Influencer rides with popular lifestyle creators (TBD)
- Pop-up "Glide Garage" experience space in Harajuku

## 5. Media plan

Launch everything simultaneously in mid-September for maximum impact. TV + OOH +
digital + the pop-up all at once makes Glide feel like a citywide movement that
everyone is talking about.

## 6. Why this wins

Micro-mobility is the hottest trend in urban life right now. The film will be
beautiful and emotional, riders will feel like heroes, and the campaign is so
cool that new subscribers will flood in. Glide becomes the icon of city freedom.
