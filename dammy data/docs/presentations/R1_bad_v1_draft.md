# KURO Latte Launch Proposal — Alternative Route (v1, shipped as-is)

- Requirement: R1 / Client: Hoshino Beverage Co.
- Author: Hana Yamamoto (Planner)
- Date: 2025-08-24
- Status: presented to client — REJECTED
- Note: Sato-san was on the Sakura Mobility pitch; this route went out without her review.

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Wake Up Your Senses."**

KURO Latte is an experience explosion. Rich, bold, luxurious — the can that makes
every morning feel premium. We bring sensory excitement to the coffee aisle.

## 2. Campaign concept

High-energy 30s film: slow-motion coffee swirls, city lights, a diverse cast of
people taking their first sip and reacting with delight. Tagline everywhere:
"Wake Up Your Senses."

## 3. Target

Everyone who drinks coffee, 20–59. Canned-coffee drinkers ready to upgrade,
café-goers, morning commuters, late-night workers. Coffee is universal — KURO
should be too.

## 4. Executions

- TV spot (30s) national
- Train-wrap ads on Yamanote Line
- Influencer seeding (50 lifestyle influencers)
- Sampling trucks in 5 cities

## 5. Media plan

Big-bang national launch across TV, OOH and digital simultaneously. Premium products
need premium presence everywhere.

## 6. Why this wins

The visuals will be stunning and memorable. Everyone loves great coffee, and this
campaign makes KURO feel exciting and luxurious to the widest possible audience.
