# Hana EV Pre-Launch Awareness Proposal (final v2)

- Requirement: R4 / Client: Sakura Mobility Inc.
- Author: Hana Yamamoto (Planner) / Revised: Ayaka Sato (Executive Strategy Director)
- Date: 2025-10-21
- Status: presented to client — SELECTED

## 1. The problem we are solving

Awareness is not what blocks Hana EV — anxiety is. In the client's own clinic surveys,
62% of respondents name charging as their #1 objection, and the dealer network is thin
outside metro areas: test drives are realistically bookable in only 6 prefectures.
Category data shows EV consideration collapses at the "where do I charge?" question,
not at "have I heard of it?". Broad awareness spend before we have anxiety-proof
assets buys recognition of a car people have already decided is impractical.

**Problem statement: Hana EV's barrier is charging anxiety plus a thin dealer network —
awareness money spent before the anxiety is answered is wasted money.**

## 2. Target: one segment, deliberately narrow

**Primary: two-car suburban households in the 6 launch prefectures replacing the second
car** (est. 410,000 households; second-car daily trips average 18km — well inside the
250km range, and 78% of these homes can install a home charger). For them the anxiety
is answerable with facts, and a dealer is close enough to actually book a test drive.

**Who we are NOT targeting (and why):**
- EV enthusiasts (already converted; they buy without our help and skew the read)
- Single-car urban households (no home charging, one car must do everything — range
  and charging are structurally incompatible, no message fixes that)

## 3. Strategy

Answer the anxiety before asking for attention. Message: **"250km. Your second car
drives 18km a day. Charged at home overnight for about ¥190."** Proof first, poetry later.

## 4. Executions

- Real-owner documentation series: 3 households, 90 days, actual charging costs and
  range logs published unedited — the anxiety-proof asset library
- Home-charging install content: cost, wall-box process, subsidy walkthrough per prefecture
- Dealer-radius digital and local OOH only within the 6 launch prefectures
- "Drive the Bloom" film retained from draft — cut to 30s, launched in phase 3 as the
  emotional layer only after proof assets have run and the test-drive gate is passed

## 5. Media plan: sequenced, gated

1. Weeks 1–4: proof assets only — owner cost/range documentation, home-charging content
2. Weeks 5–10: consideration layer — test-drive booking push, geo-targeted to dealer radius
3. Broad awareness (TV, national OOH) only if test-drive bookings exceed 3,000 by week 8

## 6. KPIs

- Aided awareness in 6 launch prefectures: 35% by end of March
- Test-drive bookings: 8,000 by launch (April 2026)
- Charging-anxiety objection in tracking survey: 62% → 40%

## 7. Sources

- Client clinic surveys, Sep 2025 (charging-anxiety objection rate)
- Passenger EV Consideration Tracker 2025 (consideration funnel drop-off points)
- Household Mobility Census 2024 (two-car household counts, second-car trip distances)
