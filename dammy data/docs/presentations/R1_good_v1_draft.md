# KURO Latte Launch Proposal (draft v1)

- Requirement: R1 / Client: Hoshino Beverage Co.
- Author: Riku Tanaka (Planner)
- Date: 2025-08-18
- Status: internal draft

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Your Daily Masterpiece."**

KURO Latte isn't just coffee — it's a small work of art you can hold. We turn every
can into a moment of craft appreciation. Beautiful, premium, unmistakably special.

## 2. Campaign concept

A cinematic brand film showing an artisan roaster at dawn, hands cupping beans,
steam rising — cut to a young professional opening a KURO can at their desk and
smiling. The craft travels from the roastery to your hand.

## 3. Target

Young adults 20–49 who appreciate quality. Coffee lovers, design-conscious people,
busy professionals, students who want a treat — anyone who wants something better
than ordinary canned coffee.

## 4. Executions

- Brand film (60s) for TV and YouTube
- Stylish OOH posters in Shibuya, Omotesando, Shinjuku
- Instagram/TikTok: #DailyMasterpiece UGC campaign
- Collaboration with a famous barista (TBD)
- Launch event: pop-up "KURO Gallery" café in Aoyama

## 5. Media plan

Launch everything simultaneously in mid-October for maximum impact.
TV + OOH + digital all at once creates a huge splash and makes KURO
feel like a big cultural moment.

## 6. Why this wins

The craft coffee trend is huge right now. Our campaign is beautiful and emotional,
and people will definitely want to share it. KURO will become the iconic premium can.
