# Glide Subscription Growth Proposal — Alternative Route (v1, shipped as-is)

- Author: Kenji Mori (Senior Planner)

- Status: presented to client — REJECTED

## 1. The Big Idea

**"Freedom of the City."**

Glide is liberation. No timetables, no ticket gates, no shoulder-to-shoulder carriages —
just you and the open street. We make Glide the symbol of a freer urban life.

## 2. Campaign concept

A sweeping anthem film: dozens of riders from every walk of life converge from
different neighborhoods and glide together across a bridge at sunrise, the city
skyline behind them. Voiceover about choosing your own path. Tagline: "Freedom
of the City."

## 3. Target

Everyone who moves around the city, 18–59. Commuters, students, parents on errands,
night-shift workers, weekend wanderers. Everybody hates crowded trains — so Glide
is for everybody.

## 4. Executions

- Anthem film (60s) national TV and YouTube masthead
- Station-domination OOH across all major Tokyo and Osaka hubs
- Radio spots during morning rush
- Mass influencer program across lifestyle, fitness and travel genres
- "Freedom Festival" launch event in Odaiba

## 5. Media plan

Big-bang national launch across TV, OOH, radio and digital simultaneously.
A subscription this exciting deserves to be everywhere at once — total presence
creates total desire.

## 6. Why this wins

The emotion of freedom is universal and powerful. The film will move people, the
festival will make headlines, and the sheer scale of the campaign will make Glide
the most talked-about brand in urban mobility this autumn.
