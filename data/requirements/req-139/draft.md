## Campaign Title

**"The Weekend Kitchen Glow-Up"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Hartline Weekend Kitchen Glow-Up, a program of project bundles priced $149-$499 that refresh a kitchen without a remodel — cabinet painting, hardware swaps, peel-and-stick backsplash, faucet upgrades, and under-cabinet lighting. Kitchen content is absolutely everywhere right now. #KitchenTok and "renter-friendly kitchen hacks" are among the biggest home trends on TikTok and Instagram, Pinterest searches for budget kitchen refreshes keep climbing, and before-and-after reveals are the most shareable format in home content. This campaign rides that wave and puts Hartline at the center of it.

## Tactics

1. **Short-form video (Reels & TikTok):** A stream of 30-second before-and-after Glow-Up transformations, one per bundle, cut for trending audio.
2. **Creator partnerships:** Regional home and DIY creators each complete one bundle in their own kitchen and post the journey.
3. **Pinterest & Instagram inspiration ads:** Idea-pin and carousel formats targeting kitchen refresh searches and saves.
4. **In-store weekend workshop series:** "Glow-Up Saturdays" where shoppers practice cabinet painting and backsplash installation on demo rigs.
5. **Email & SMS:** Bundle spotlights, workshop invites, and a countdown series timed to spring.
6. **Reveal-content engine:** Customers who finish a bundle are invited to share their own before-and-after for a feature on Hartline's channels.

## Target Audience

The program targets homeowners aged 28-50 who want a kitchen update without committing to a remodel. They are active on social platforms, save lots of inspiration content, and respond to realistic, budget-friendly transformations more than showroom perfection.

## More Tactics & Amplification

We will boost the best-performing creator posts, retarget workshop attendees with bundle offers, and run a spring-finale push collecting the season's best customer reveals into a hero montage.
