# Client Request — Shoulder-Season Weekend Getaway Campaign

**To:** Meridian & Craft
**From:** Tom Ashford, Executive Director, Cascade Valley Tourism Board
**Date received:** 2025-07-03
**Subject:** Request for proposal — Fall 2025 weekend getaway campaign

---

Folks,

Hope summer is treating you well down in Halverton. Board approved the fall budget last night, so here's the official ask. Seven years in, you know how we do this, but our procurement rules mean I still have to write it all down.

## The situation up the valley

The Valley has never looked better — the ridge trails, the farm markets, the tasting rooms, the new inn openings along the river road. Summer is strong as always. What we want this year is a proper fall campaign that shows the region at its most beautiful and gets a younger crowd of weekenders up here for the autumn colors.

We've packaged things up nicely this year: getaway bundles positioned around **$400 per visitor** covering lodging, food, and activities in one booking.

I'll mention one thing our lodging partners keep bending my ear about — once the summer crowd goes home, a lot of them are sitting close to half-empty through September and October, even in peak foliage weeks. It's a sore point at the association meetings. But the heart of this brief is simpler: make Cascade Valley's fall look absolutely irresistible to the next generation of visitors.

## What we want

A campaign that showcases the Valley's fall experiences — beautiful video, creator content, an easy path to booking. We want reach, we want the Valley all over the feeds of every young traveler within driving distance, and we want the packages easy to buy.

## Target

Drive-market travelers under 35 within about 4 hours of Cascade Valley. The weekend-escape crowd that plans trips off their phones. Couples and young families in the same radius are welcome spillover.

## Budget & timeline

- **Budget:** $310,000 (fully approved, public funds — reporting requirements apply)
- **Campaign window:** 2025-08-15 to 2025-10-31

## Success measure

The board wants a hard number: **2,500 getaway packages booked through the campaign by October 31**. We'll also survey for awareness lift, but bookings are what I stand up and present in November.

## Deliverables

- Campaign concept and creative approach
- Media/channel plan with full budget breakdown
- Production plan (we'd love assets we can keep using after the window)
- Measurement plan suitable for a public board report

## Process

Because we're a public body, this goes out as an open RFP — four agencies are bidding, yourselves included. You've won it every year since 2018 and I see no reason to break the streak, but the scoring rubric doesn't know that. Proposals due July 25.

See you up the valley road,
Tom
