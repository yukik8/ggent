# Pawstead Grocery In-Store Sampling Weekend Series — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Pawstead Fresh
**Campaign window:** June 1 – September 15, 2028
**Budget:** $220,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Try Fresh Weekends"**

---

## Opening

Introducing Try Fresh Weekends, a summer-long series of in-store sampling events at Pawstead's fridge-aisle grocery partners. Trained brand ambassadors run chilled demo stations next to the Pawstead fridge, offering single-meal samples, answering formulation questions, and handing out a starter coupon. The product sells itself once shoppers see and smell real food, and sampling puts that moment right where the purchase happens, at an average feeding cost of $3.20/day.

---

## Program Details

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| Format | Chilled demo station beside the Pawstead fridge placement |
| Cadence | Friday-Sunday events, rotating across partner banners |
| Coverage | 60 high-traffic stores across partner grocery chains |
| Offer | Single-meal sample plus starter coupon at the station |

---

## Target Audience

Premium dog-food shoppers already in the fridge-aisle grocery partners. They are in-store, they already spend on quality food, and many have seen Pawstead in the fridge but have not yet picked it up. A guided taste-and-talk moment is often all it takes.

---

## The Big Idea

**"See it. Smell it. Feed it tonight."** Try Fresh Weekends turns the fridge placement from something shoppers walk past into something they experience, with the demo station making the freshness argument no package can.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| In-store sampling & demo stations | The core of the program; staffed weekend events at 60 stores |
| Retail media (grocery partners) | Sponsored placements and banner support in the weeks around each store's events |
| Local paid social (geo-targeted) | Short videos announcing each weekend's stores to nearby dog owners |
| POS materials | Fridge decals, shelf talkers, and coupon takeaways that persist after events |

Sampling works very well for food products, and pairing it with retail media keeps Pawstead visible before and after each event weekend.

## KPIs

- Strong sample volume and station conversations per event
- Healthy coupon redemption from event weekends
- Sales lift at event stores versus non-event control stores
- Improved velocity for Pawstead SKUs at partner banners

---

## Why This Will Work

Fresh food wins on taste and visibility, and sampling delivers both at the exact moment of purchase. With retail media and geo-targeted social wrapped around each weekend, Try Fresh Weekends turns the fridge aisle into Pawstead's best-performing channel of the summer.
