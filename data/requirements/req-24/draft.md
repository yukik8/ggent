## Campaign Title

**"The Glow Window" — Velura's TikTok Shop Flash-Drop Program**

---

## Opening: Velura Goes Live

The Velura $25 flash bundle is built for the way people shop right now. Each bundle pairs the hero fragrance-free moisturizer with a rotating mini (cleanser one week, lip balm the next), wrapped in a limited-run sleeve, and it only exists inside a countdown window on TikTok Shop. When the timer hits zero, that bundle is gone and the next one gets teased.

TikTok Shop is the fastest-moving thing in beauty commerce right now, and flash mechanics are absolutely everywhere on the platform. Live shopping is blowing up, creator affiliate storefronts are blowing up, and beauty is consistently one of the top-performing categories on the whole app. Brands that lean into the native mechanics — countdowns, live drops, creator carts — are seeing huge results, and brands that just run normal ads are getting scrolled past. Velura has clean formulas, a cult moisturizer, and a $25 price that sits right in the impulse sweet spot. The opportunity is to plug all of that into every trending Shop mechanic at once and let the platform's momentum do the heavy lifting.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The Glow Window"** turns scarcity into a ritual. Every Friday at 7pm, a new $25 bundle drop opens on Velura's TikTok Shop for a limited window — sometimes 24 hours, sometimes just the length of a livestream. The countdown clock is the brand asset. Creators tease what's in the next window, live hosts open the window on stream, and the comment section becomes a "did you catch it or miss it" conversation that fuels the next drop. Miss a window and you wait a week, which is exactly the kind of light FOMO that TikTok rewards.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. TikTok Shop LIVE Flash Events
Live shopping is the single hottest format on the platform, and flash sales perform best when they happen on camera. We will produce weekly Friday-night live events with a rotating cast of hosts — a brand host plus a guest creator — running demos, ingredient talk, and the on-stream window opening with a visible countdown and live stock ticker. Live events will also test flash-within-flash moments, like a bonus mini unlocked if the stream hits a viewer milestone, which is a mechanic doing really well for beauty brands lately.

### 2. Creator Affiliate Blitz
TikTok Shop's affiliate ecosystem is exploding, and niche skincare creators drive a ton of conversion on the platform. We will recruit a wide roster of affiliates — big, mid, and micro — arm them with bundle samples and drop calendars, and let them push the Glow Window through their own storefronts and shoppable videos. Volume matters here, so we go broad rather than picky, plus a smaller paid-partnership tier for tentpole drops.

### 3. Paid TikTok Media (Spark Ads + Video Shopping Ads)
Organic momentum needs paid fuel. We will boost the best-performing creator and live clips as Spark Ads, run Video Shopping Ads directly against the active bundle, and retarget viewers who watched a live or visited the Shop page but did not buy before the window closed. "You missed the window" retargeting creative is a fun angle that should perform.

### 4. Trend-Response Content Studio
TikTok moves fast, so the brand account has to move faster. We will stand up a lightweight always-on content operation producing 4-6 posts a week that jump on trending sounds, formats, and skincare memes, always tying back to the countdown. GRWM formats, "restock day" vlogs, packing-orders ASMR, ingredient duets with derm creators — everything trending in beauty content gets a Glow Window spin.

### 5. Unboxing-Ready Bundle Kits
Each drop ships in a limited sleeve with a collectible window-number card, designed to be filmed. The packaging is the UGC engine: buyers post their haul, which becomes next week's social proof, which we then boost. Shareable unboxings are a huge driver of TikTok Shop purchases, so the kit design is genuinely a media channel.

---

## Target Audience

Primary audience is impulse beauty buyers under 30 on TikTok — people who discover products on their For You page and are comfortable checking out inside the app without ever visiting a brand site. They love a deal, they love a limited drop, and a $25 price point is low enough that a good video plus a ticking clock is all the convincing they need. Many of them are already deep in skincare content and follow multiple beauty creators, so they will encounter the Glow Window from several directions in the same week.

Secondary audience is existing Velura customers who follow the brand and will treat the weekly bundle as a fun way to restock the moisturizer they already use.
