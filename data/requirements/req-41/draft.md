# Pawstead Fresh Grocery Fridge-Aisle National Launch Proposal — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Pawstead Fresh
**Campaign window:** April 1 – August 31, 2025
**Budget:** $680,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Fridge-Worthy" — Pawstead Fresh Comes to the Fresh Aisle**

---

## Opening: Real Food for Dogs, Finally Where Real Food Lives

Introducing Pawstead Fresh in the grocery fridge aisle — the most exciting step yet for a brand that has already changed how thousands of dogs eat. Pawstead's vet-formulated refrigerated meals are gently cooked, made with recognizable whole ingredients, and portioned into sleek, stackable fridge packs that look right at home next to the salad kits and cold-pressed juices. Now, for the first time, shoppers will be able to grab that same beautiful fresh food off the shelf at their own grocery store instead of waiting for a delivery box.

And the timing could not be better. Fresh dog food is booming, and more and more dog owners are treating their pets like true family members. People are reading labels, asking questions, and looking for food that actually looks like food. At an average feeding cost of just $3.20/day, Pawstead makes the fresh upgrade feel totally doable — about the price of a coffee. This launch is Pawstead's chance to leap out of the delivery box and into millions of daily grocery trips, and we could not be more thrilled to bring it to life.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is dog owners under 40 who already buy premium dog food but have not yet tried fresh. These are devoted, detail-oriented pet parents — the kind who screenshot ingredient panels, follow adorable rescue-dog accounts, and genuinely want the best for their pups. They are curious about fresh but a little hesitant, whether it is the subscription commitment, the fridge space, or just not being sure their dog will love it. They spend tons of time on social media, and they really trust vets and real dog owners way more than they trust ads.

Secondary audiences include current Pawstead subscribers who will love the convenience of grabbing a top-up pack in store, and gift-y shoppers buying for the dog people in their lives.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Fridge-Worthy"** is a launch platform built around one warm, simple idea: if it is not good enough for your fridge, it is not good enough for your dog. We celebrate the moment Pawstead earns a spot on the actual family fridge shelf — right between the meal-prep containers and the oat milk. The creative centers on real dogs and real fridges: owners filming the "first bowl" moment, dogs waiting at the fridge door instead of the pantry, and the little shelf reorganization that says this dog is family. It is charming, endlessly memeable, and it turns the fridge aisle itself into the hero of the campaign.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts)
Short-form video is where our audience lives, and dog content is basically the internet's favorite genre. We will run a sustained flight of Reels and Shorts featuring first-bowl reactions, fridge-door "waiting" moments, and gorgeous product footage of the fresh packs. Social video will do the heavy lifting on awareness and will make the fridge-aisle arrival feel like a genuine cultural moment for dog people.

### 2. Vet & Pet Creator Partnerships
Trust is everything in pet food, so we will partner with veterinarian creators and beloved real-dog accounts to tell the Pawstead story authentically. Vets can walk through the formulation on camera, while dog creators document their pup's switch to fresh over a few weeks. This kind of content feels honest and personal, and it performs really well with pet parents who are skeptical of polished brand ads.

### 3. In-Store Fridge-Aisle Sampling & Shopper Activation
Nothing convinces a dog owner like their own dog cleaning the bowl. We will run "First Bowl on Us" weekend sampling activations at high-traffic grocery locations in priority launch markets, with branded fridge-aisle displays, take-home trial packs, and team members who can talk through the $3.20/day math. The fridge placement itself is a statement — this is real food, kept cold like real food.

### 4. Grocery Retail Media
We will support the launch with sponsored product placements and banners across our grocery partners' retail media networks, so Pawstead stays visible when shoppers are building baskets online and planning their trips. Retail media is growing incredibly fast and it is a natural fit for capturing shoppers who saw our social content earlier in the week.

### 5. Lifecycle Email & CRM
Every trial pack and sampling interaction feeds a welcome journey. We will build a warm post-trial email and SMS flow with feeding tips, transition guides, and a gentle nudge toward the second purchase — because the second bowl matters just as much as the first.
