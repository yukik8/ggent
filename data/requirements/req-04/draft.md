# Ledgerline AP Automation Suite — Demand-Generation Program

**Campaign title:** "Month-End, Handled"
**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Ledgerline
**Budget:** $540,000
**Flight:** June 1, 2025 – November 30, 2025

---

## 1. Opening

Introducing the Ledgerline AP Automation Suite, a cloud platform that takes the pain out of accounts payable. The suite offers automated invoice capture, configurable approval workflows, real-time spend visibility, and native integrations with the major mid-market ERPs and accounting systems. It installs quickly, requires minimal IT lift, and is priced accessibly at a $24,000 average annual contract, which makes it one of the most complete AP automation offerings available to growing companies today.

Ledgerline has built a genuinely impressive product, and finance software is a hot category right now. Companies everywhere are looking to modernize their back office, and automation is on nearly every finance leader's roadmap. This program is designed to put Ledgerline's product in front of the right buyers at scale and turn that interest into a steady flow of demo requests and qualified pipeline through the second half of 2025.

## 2. Target Audience

Our target is finance directors and controllers at US companies with 50–500 employees. These are busy, detail-oriented professionals who care about accuracy, efficiency, and control. They are active on professional networks, they read industry content, and they talk to peers before making software decisions. Many of them are frustrated with manual processes and are open to change if the case is made clearly. We will also reach adjacent influencers in the buying process, such as CFOs, VPs of finance, and senior AP managers, since B2B purchases usually involve several stakeholders.

## 3. The Big Idea — "Month-End, Handled"

Every controller knows the feeling of month-end close, the late nights, the invoice piles, the chasing of approvals. "Month-End, Handled" is a creative platform that dramatizes the before-and-after of AP automation. The hero creative shows the calendar flipping to the last week of the month while our protagonist finance director calmly leaves the office on time, because Ledgerline is handling the invoices in the background. It is a simple, relatable promise expressed with warmth and a little humor, and it gives us a flexible container for testimonial content, product demos, and thought-leadership pieces across the flight.

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

We recommend an integrated B2B mix built around channels that are known to work well for this kind of audience.

- **LinkedIn (paid social & sponsored content).** LinkedIn is very effective for reaching finance decision-makers, and sponsored content performs well for B2B software. We will run always-on awareness and retargeting flights featuring the hero creative, customer stories, and gated content.
- **Peer review platforms (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius).** Peer reviews matter a lot these days in software buying, so we will invest in premium profiles, category placements, and review-generation programs to strengthen Ledgerline's presence where buyers compare vendors.
- **Content & webinar series.** A monthly webinar series ("The Modern Close") plus downloadable guides and benchmark checklists. Educational content is a proven way to build trust with finance audiences and capture leads.
- **Paid search.** Always-on coverage of high-intent AP automation and invoice-processing keywords, capturing buyers who are actively researching solutions.
- **Landing pages & creative production.** A refreshed campaign landing environment showcasing the suite's features, integrations, and pricing, plus the full creative toolkit for all channels.
