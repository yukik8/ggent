# Campaign Proposal: "The New Standard of Care for AP"

**Client:** Ledgerline
**Product:** Ledgerline AP Automation Suite — Healthcare Tier
**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Budget:** $460,000
**Timeline:** March 1, 2026 – August 31, 2026
**Version:** Draft v1

---

## 1. Opening: Why Ledgerline Healthcare Is Exciting

Introducing the Ledgerline Healthcare Tier, the most capable version of the Ledgerline AP Automation Suite the company has ever shipped. Built on the same cloud platform that mid-market finance teams already trust, the healthcare tier adds vendor-compliance tracking, department-level cost-center routing, multi-entity approval chains, and audit-ready documentation trails designed for provider organizations. At a $30,000 average annual contract value, it is a premium offering, and it deserves a premium launch.

Healthcare finance teams have a lot on their plates, and modern AP automation is becoming more and more relevant to them. Finance software is a growing space, and buyers everywhere are looking for tools that save time. Ledgerline's feature set is genuinely best-in-class, and we believe that once healthcare finance leaders see what the product can do, adoption will follow. This campaign is about getting the product in front of them with confidence and polish.

## 2. Target Audience

Our primary audience is finance leads at 100–500 employee healthcare providers across the US — controllers, finance directors, and VPs of finance at physician groups, outpatient networks, behavioral health organizations, and regional clinic chains. Secondarily, we will reach the office managers and revenue-cycle staff who influence software decisions, plus CFOs at the larger end of the range. Broadly speaking, anyone in a healthcare organization who touches invoices or vendor payments is a potential champion for Ledgerline.

Many finance professionals in healthcare are frustrated with manual processes, and lots of them are actively researching automation tools. Peer recommendations matter a great deal in this space, and professional social channels are very effective for reaching finance decision-makers.

## 3. Big Idea: "The New Standard of Care"

Healthcare organizations hold themselves to a standard of care for patients. Our campaign extends that language to the back office: **Ledgerline is the new standard of care for accounts payable.** The creative system pairs clinical visual language (charts, vitals, checklists) with finance imagery, positioning manual AP work as a condition that can be treated, and Ledgerline as the proven treatment protocol.

The idea flexes across every channel: hero video ("Diagnose your AP process"), an interactive self-assessment tool ("Get your AP health check"), case-study content ("Recovery stories"), and webinar programming ("Grand rounds for finance teams").

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

1. **LinkedIn advertising** — Sponsored content and conversation ads targeting healthcare finance titles. Professional social is where these buyers live, and LinkedIn's targeting is strong for finance roles.
2. **Peer review site presence** — Sponsored placement and profile enhancement on major B2B software review platforms. Buyers in this category rely heavily on peer reviews, so a strong presence there is essential.
3. **Content & case studies** — A library of healthcare-specific case studies, an "AP Health Check" interactive assessment, and vertical landing pages. Content marketing is one of the most effective tools in B2B.
4. **Webinar series** — A monthly "Grand Rounds for Finance" webinar featuring customer speakers and healthcare finance thought leaders. Webinars are great for generating qualified pipeline.
5. **Industry conference presence** — Booth and speaking presence at two healthcare finance conferences during the campaign window, where face-to-face conversations can move deals along.
6. **Email nurture** — A six-touch nurture program for leads generated across all channels, keeping Ledgerline top of mind through what can be a lengthy consideration process.
