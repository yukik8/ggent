# Cascade Valley Harvest Festival Weekend Push — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Cascade Valley Tourism Board
**Campaign window:** August 15 – October 31, 2028
**Budget:** $250,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Harvest Days in the Valley"**

---

## Opening: The Valley's Most Delicious Season

Introducing the Cascade Valley Harvest Festival Weekend push, a celebration of the most mouthwatering, golden, and downright joyful time of year in the valley. The $39 harvest festival pass unlocks an incredible lineup: orchard picking, a cider barn crawl, a farmers' feast market with dozens of local producers, pumpkin fields, hayrides, and a glowing lantern-lit evening market that has to be seen to be believed. Autumn in Cascade Valley is absolutely stunning, and the harvest festival wraps all of that seasonal magic into one easy, affordable pass.

This is the valley at its warmest and most welcoming, and our campaign will bring that irresistible harvest energy to families and food-lovers across the entire drive market.

---

## Target Audience

Drive-market families and food-motivated travelers aged 25-50. These are people who adore seasonal traditions, love discovering local food, and are always looking for that perfect fall weekend. Autumn content is enormously popular with this group, and a festival pass gives them a wonderfully simple way to say yes.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Harvest Days in the Valley"** turns the festival into the region's signature fall tradition. The creative is warm, golden, and abundant — overflowing market tables, kids in orchards, steaming cider — all leading to one inviting call to action: grab your $39 pass and taste the season.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Gorgeous, cozy fall video is some of the most engaging content on social media, and it will carry our awareness push across Reels and TikTok.

### 2. Creator Partnerships
Regional food and family creators will experience the festival and share their favorite moments, bringing authentic charm that polished ads simply can't match.

### 3. Drive-Market Out-of-Home
Beautiful harvest billboards along the key routes into the valley will keep the festival top of mind all season.

### 4. Regional Search & Display
We will capture everyone searching for fall festivals, apple picking, and autumn weekend ideas.

### 5. Email & Landing Page
A festive pass-purchase page and warm, inviting emails to the board's list.
