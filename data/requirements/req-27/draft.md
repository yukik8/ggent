## Opening: Introducing the Northtrail Corporate Commuter-Benefit Program

The Northtrail Corporate Commuter-Benefit Program packages everything Northtrail does best into a single turnkey employee benefit. Participating employers offer their teams a Northtrail commuter e-bike plus unlimited access to the battery-swap network at a subsidized rate of $75/month per employee. Northtrail handles delivery, maintenance, insurance, and swaps; the employer simply adds the benefit to its package and watches the bikes roll in.

The program's core components are summarized below.

| Component | What It Includes |
|---|---|
| Subsidized subscription | $75/month per employee, employer co-funded |
| Battery-swap access | Unlimited swaps at all Northtrail network stations |
| Fleet dashboard | HR admin portal for enrollment, usage, and billing |
| Full-service coverage | Maintenance, theft insurance, and seasonal tune-ups |
| Turnkey rollout | On-site launch day, sizing/fitting, and safety session |

Commuter benefits are a growing conversation in HR right now, and companies are always looking for perks that employees actually use. Many employers want to offer something fresh that supports wellness and sustainability goals at the same time, and an e-bike benefit checks all of those boxes. This campaign will introduce the program to benefits decision-makers and build a strong pipeline of corporate partnerships through the first half of 2027.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is HR and benefits leads at 200-2,000 employee companies in cities with good bike-lane infrastructure. These are practical, ROI-minded professionals who are constantly evaluating new benefits vendors and juggling renewal cycles. They read industry newsletters, attend benefits conferences, and rely heavily on peer recommendations before shortlisting a new vendor.

| Segment | Role | What They Care About |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | HR / Total Rewards leads | Utilization, employee satisfaction, easy admin |
| Influencer | Finance / procurement | Cost per employee, contract flexibility |
| Champion | Sustainability officers | Emissions reporting, ESG storytelling |

Employees themselves are a secondary audience — internal enthusiasm makes the HR buying decision much easier, so the campaign includes materials employers can share internally.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Commute Forward"** positions the Northtrail program as the one benefit employees can literally see arriving every morning. Gym stipends go unused and wellness apps get deleted, but a bike rack that fills up outside the office is proof the benefit is working. The centerpiece activation is **Fleet Week**: a two-week on-site trial where Northtrail parks a ten-bike demo fleet at a prospect company, lets employees ride to work on real commutes, and hands HR a utilization report at the end. Fleet Week turns an abstract benefits pitch into something the whole company talks about in the break room.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. LinkedIn Paid & Account-Based Marketing
LinkedIn is the most direct way to reach HR and benefits professionals, and it is very effective for B2B campaigns like this one. We will run sponsored content and InMail targeted at benefits titles at companies matching our size and geography profile, supported by an ABM tier for a named list of high-priority accounts with personalized landing pages.

### 2. HR Trade Media & Newsletter Sponsorships
Benefits leads read a lot of industry content, so we will place sponsored articles and newsletter takeovers in the HR and total-rewards trade press. Thought-leadership pieces on commuter benefits will build credibility and keep Northtrail top of mind through renewal season.

### 3. Fleet Week On-Site Pilot Activations
The heart of the campaign. We will run Fleet Week pilots at qualified prospect companies across our priority cities. Each pilot includes bike delivery, a launch-day event with fitting and safety orientation, swap-station orientation, and a closing utilization report for HR. Pilots create internal champions and give the sales team a warm, evidence-backed close.

### 4. Webinars & Benefits Conference Presence
We will host a quarterly webinar series featuring HR leaders from early partner companies, and take a demo booth to two major benefits conferences during the window. Live rides at conference venues tend to draw a crowd and generate strong lead volume.

### 5. Email Nurture & CRM
All leads flow into a lifecycle email program that moves prospects from awareness content to Fleet Week booking. Nurture tracks are segmented by role so finance influencers see cost content and HR sees utilization content.
