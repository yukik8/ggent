## Campaign Title

**"The First 30 Club"**

---

## Opening

Introducing The First 30 Club, Kinetiq's new member activation program built from the mechanics people are actually obsessed with right now. Streaks are having a massive cultural moment — Duolingo turned the streak into an identity, run clubs are the biggest story in fitness, Strava kudos are a social currency, and every app that wins retention has some form of daily momentum loop. The First 30 Club stacks all of that energy into a new Kinetiq member's first month: a streak tracker, milestone badges, a live class series, swag drops, creator-hosted livestreams, and a community challenge, all wrapped into one 30-day arc at the $39/month membership.

---

## The Big Idea

Your first month on the rower shouldn't feel like homework, it should feel like joining something. The First 30 Club gives every new member a visible 30-day journey with moments of celebration built in. Day 1 gets a welcome video and a starter class plan. Day 10 unlocks a badge. Day 20 unlocks a swag drop entry. Day 30 makes you a Club alum with a shareable milestone card, and the sharing is the flywheel — milestone cards are exactly the kind of low-effort, high-identity content people post.

---

## Tactics

### 1. In-App Streak Challenge & Badges
A dedicated First 30 track inside the Kinetiq app: streak counter, workout milestones at 4, 8, 12, and 20 sessions, and a finisher badge. Badges are designed to be screenshot-worthy.

### 2. Live "First 30" Class Series
A beginner-friendly live class series programmed specifically for the first month — short sessions, form-first coaching, and shout-outs for members hitting milestones. Replays join the on-demand library.

### 3. Lifecycle Email & Push
A day-by-day message arc: welcome, first-class nudge, week-two slump rescue, milestone celebrations, and a day-30 congratulations that rolls into month-two programming. Push notifications mirror the email arc.

### 4. SMS Nudges
Short, timely texts for scheduled-workout reminders and milestone moments. SMS is direct and immediate, which fits the habit-building use case.

### 5. Creator-Hosted Onboarding Livestreams
Monthly livestreams where a fitness creator and a Kinetiq coach row the beginner series together and answer new-member questions live. Co-watching content is huge right now and it makes week one feel social instead of solitary.

### 6. Milestone Rewards
Physical swag drops (water bottle at 8 workouts, hoodie lottery for finishers) plus partner perks. Unboxing moments feed the community challenge hashtag.

---

## Target Audience

New Kinetiq members in their first month of ownership — the household that just unboxed a rower and is deciding, in real time, whether it becomes a habit or furniture. They skew 28-45, many are first-time connected-fitness owners, and their motivation is highest in week one and most fragile in weeks two and three.
