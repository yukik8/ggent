Hi both,

Following up on what we sketched at the QBR — here's the formal brief.

## Context

The moisturizer franchise is still our engine, and we're finally launching the subscription refill offer: $29 per refill against $32 for a one-off jar, flexible delivery windows, pause-and-skip, the whole modern setup. Ops and fulfillment are ready; what we need now is the marketing program that makes existing buyers actually adopt it.

I'll share the uncomfortable internal number so you know what's underneath this: nearly half of our first-time moisturizer buyers never come back for a second jar. They love the product when we survey them, and then they just... drift. The subscription is our answer, but honestly I don't think anyone here fully understands why they lapse in the first place.

## What we want

A loyalty and replenishment campaign that puts the refill offer in front of everyone who's bought the moisturizer at least once, makes subscribing feel like an obvious upgrade, and gives the program some warmth and brand feel — I don't want this to read like a discount scheme. Show off the offer: the $3 savings, the convenience, maybe a rewards layer.

## Audience

Existing Velura first-time buyers at risk of lapsing, plus the broader customer file of anyone who has purchased the moisturizer.

## Budget and timeline

- **Budget:** $180,000.
- **Timeline:** In market 2026-05-01 through 2026-09-30.

## Success measure

The target I've agreed with our CEO: **8,000 active refill subscribers by September 30.** Secondary, I'll be watching second-purchase rate against our current baseline.

## Deliverables

1. Program strategy and creative platform
2. CRM/lifecycle plan plus any paid support you recommend
3. Budget allocation
4. Measurement framework

## Process

You're the incumbent on the moisturizer franchise and our data integration is already in place, which matters for something this CRM-heavy. That said, our new CFO has asked me to look at one other agency's proposal alongside yours for good governance. Proposals by April 17; I expect to decide by mid-May.

Looking forward to it.
