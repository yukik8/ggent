## Campaign Title

**"The Invisible Step" — the Daily Defense Launch**

---

## 1. Opening: Introducing Velura Daily Defense

Velura Daily Defense is the newest extension of the Velura moisturizer franchise: a fragrance-free daily moisturizer with built-in broad-spectrum SPF, priced at $36 per 50ml. It carries everything the core Velura jar is known for — the short ingredient list, the fragrance-free formula, the sensitive-skin positioning — and adds sun protection in a texture that behaves like a moisturizer, not like a sunscreen.

Product highlights at a glance:

| Attribute | Daily Defense |
|---|---|
| Format | 50ml jar, matches core Velura franchise |
| Protection | Broad-spectrum SPF, daily-wear level |
| Texture | Lightweight cream, no white cast, no sunscreen smell |
| Ingredient list | Short, pronounceable, fragrance-free |
| Price | $36 per 50ml |
| Routine fit | Replaces the morning moisturizer step, adds nothing |

Sun protection is one of the most talked-about topics in skincare right now, and clean beauty as a whole keeps growing. Many skincare shoppers say they know they should wear SPF every day, but a lot of them skip it because typical sunscreens feel heavy, smell like the beach, or leave a cast. Daily Defense solves all of that in one step, which makes this a very exciting launch for Velura and a natural next purchase for the brand's loyal moisturizer buyers.

---

## 2. Target Audience

| Segment | Who they are | What we want them to do |
|---|---|---|
| Existing Velura buyers | Loyal core-moisturizer customers, already trust the formula | Add Daily Defense as their morning jar |
| SPF-skeptical skincare users under 35 | Ingredient-label readers who research before buying and avoid traditional sunscreen textures | Trial Daily Defense as their first daily SPF |

Both groups are digitally native and spend a lot of time researching skincare online. They follow skincare creators, read ingredient breakdowns, and increasingly browse specialty beauty retail in person. They tend to be skeptical of glossy brand advertising, so authentic, evidence-flavored content works better with them than polished claims.

---

## 3. The Big Idea

**"The Invisible Step."** Daily Defense is SPF that disappears into the routine you already have. The creative platform dramatizes the moment of *not noticing*: no cast, no smell, no extra step, no excuses. Every asset is built around a simple side-by-side ritual demo — same two-minute morning routine, one jar swapped — with the payoff line "You just wore SPF. Did you notice?" It is a demonstration-first idea that suits an audience that wants proof over promises, and it works identically for loyalists (upgrade your jar) and skeptics (this is not the sunscreen you hate).

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

| # | Channel | Role in the plan |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Paid social — short-form video | Awareness and demo delivery |
| 2 | Creator & derm partnerships | Credibility and proof |
| 3 | CRM — email & SMS lifecycle | Converting the existing buyer base |
| 4 | Specialty retail activation | Physical discovery and trial |

### 4.1 Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Instagram Reels & TikTok)
Short-form video is where skincare discovery happens today, and it is a very effective format for texture-demo content. We will run a sustained flight of Reels and TikTok placements built on the swap-one-jar demo, white-cast stress tests, and creator cut-downs. Social video performs especially well with under-35 skincare audiences, so it carries the top of the funnel.

### 4.2 Creator & Dermatologist Partnerships
We will partner with a roster of niche skincare creators and dermatologists to review the formula, decode the ingredient list on camera, and run multi-week wear tests. Smaller, trusted creators feel more credible to this audience than celebrity endorsements, and their content gives us a library of authentic proof material to amplify through paid.

### 4.3 CRM — Email & SMS Lifecycle
Velura's existing buyer file is the warmest audience for a line extension. We will build a launch lifecycle: an announcement flow explaining exactly what is in the new formula and why, a routine-upgrade sequence for active buyers, and a win-back flow for lapsed customers. Email and SMS are reliable, low-cost converters for a DTC brand like Velura.

### 4.4 Specialty Retail Activation
With Velura expanding into specialty beauty retail, we will support launch endcaps with "Invisible Step" shelf displays, tester units, and a mini-size sampling program at the chain's highest-traffic doors. In-person swatching matters a lot to skincare shoppers, and shelf presence in a beauty chain adds legitimacy for skeptics who have never bought from the brand's site.
