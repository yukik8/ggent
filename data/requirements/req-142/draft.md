# Clearbrook Pediatric Urgent-Care Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Clearbrook Health
**Campaign window:** August 1 – December 15, 2027
**Budget:** $380,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"No Pediatrician? No Panic."**

---

## Opening

Introducing Clearbrook Pediatric Urgent Care, a new service line offering $149 flat-fee pediatric visits and $59 pediatric telehealth, bookable online with evening and weekend hours. Parenting content is having a massive moment across every platform — momfluencers and dad creators dominate short-form feeds, parent Facebook groups drive local recommendations, and "is this normal" search spikes every school year. This launch plugs Clearbrook straight into all of it.

## Tactics

1. **Short-form video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts):** Relatable 20-second scenarios — the Saturday-night fever, the playground wrist, the 9pm rash photo — each ending on the booking screen and the flat price.
2. **Local search & maps:** Own "pediatric urgent care near me" and keep the Google Business listings loaded with fresh reviews, hours, and book-now links.
3. **Parent-creator partnerships:** Local parenting creators film a walkthrough visit and post their honest experience.
4. **Out-of-home near schools and daycares:** Price-forward billboards and transit posters on school-run routes — "$149. Tonight. Booked from your phone."
5. **Email & SMS follow-up flows:** Post-visit check-ins that make the next step one tap — book the recheck, book the flu shot.
6. **Back-to-school surge plan:** A concentrated August-September burst timed to enrollment physicals and the first fever wave of the school year.

## Target Audience

Parents under 45 without an established pediatrician for every situation — often covered by high-deductible plans, allergic to phone-only scheduling, and deciding from a Google listing at 9pm. Many manage everything else in life from their phone and expect care to work the same way.

## Amplification

We boost the best creator posts, retarget site visitors who did not finish booking, and swap in seasonal creative for flu season.

## Why This Will Work

Parent content is the most engaged corner of local social, and no clinic network in the footprint is showing up there with a price and a booking link. With creators, search, out-of-home, and follow-up flows all pushing the same promise, Clearbrook becomes the default answer to the Saturday-night fever.
