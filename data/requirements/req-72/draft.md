# Skillharbor Brand Awareness Video Flight — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Skillharbor
**Campaign window:** April 1 – August 31, 2027
**Budget:** $350,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Harbor Your Ambition" — Skillharbor Brand Film Flight**

## Opening

Skillharbor has grown into one of the most complete upskilling platforms on the market: a deep course library, live cohort programs, career services, and an active alumni community, all under the $199/month all-access subscription. What the brand has not yet had is a sustained, top-of-funnel video presence that plants Skillharbor's name with people before they start comparing options. This flight fills that gap with a cinematic brand film and a five-month, always-on video presence across the channels where the audience watches.

The campaign centers on a sixty-second hero film, "Harbor Your Ambition," supported by thirty- and fifteen-second cutdowns. The film follows three working adults through a quiet, honest moment of deciding to change direction, and lands on the platform as the place where that decision becomes real.

## Target Audience

Adults aged 25-40 who are considering career moves. They are employed, busy, and quietly restless, streaming video in the evenings, watching YouTube on weekends, and scrolling short-form video daily. Most are not actively shopping for a learning platform yet, which is exactly why a memorable brand presence matters before the comparison moment arrives.

## The Big Idea

**"Harbor Your Ambition" — the place your next career docks.**

The harbor metaphor gives the brand a warm, ownable visual world: departures, arrivals, open water, and safe harbor. The tone is calm and confident rather than urgent, positioning Skillharbor as the trusted name that comes to mind when restlessness finally becomes a plan.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Streaming / CTV video | Hero film in premium evening streaming environments; primary reach builder |
| YouTube pre-roll | Sixty- and thirty-second cuts against career, finance, and self-improvement content |
| Paid social video | Fifteen-second cutdowns on Reels, TikTok, and Shorts for frequency and younger reach |
| Hero film production | One flagship shoot producing the full asset ladder for every placement |
| Brand-lift measurement | Pre/post brand tracking across the flight to quantify awareness gains |

Video is the strongest medium for brand storytelling, and a consistent five-month presence across CTV, YouTube, and social gives the campaign both reach and repetition. The asset ladder approach keeps production efficient while giving every channel a format built for how it is actually watched.

## KPIs

- Aided and unaided brand awareness lift among adults 25-40 (pre/post study)
- Video completion rates across CTV, YouTube, and social placements
- Effective reach and frequency against the target audience
- Branded search volume growth over the flight
- Efficient CPMs relative to plan across all three video channels

## Why This Will Work

Awareness is the foundation every later campaign builds on, and Skillharbor has earned the right to tell a bigger brand story. The harbor world is distinctive and warm, the film is built to be remembered rather than skipped, and the five-month always-on structure means the brand is present through an entire season of career restlessness. When these viewers eventually start comparing platforms, Skillharbor will already feel familiar.
