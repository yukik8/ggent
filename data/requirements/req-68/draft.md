## Campaign Title

**"Ahead of the Curve" — the Skillharbor AI-Skills Bootcamp Launch**

---

## Opening: The Boldest Launch in Skillharbor History

Introducing the Skillharbor AI-Skills Bootcamp, the most ambitious, most timely, most transformative program Skillharbor has ever created. This intensive $3,200 bootcamp teaches working professionals how to actually use AI on the job — prompt fluency, workflow automation, AI-assisted analysis, and the judgment to know what to delegate to the machines and what to keep. It is taught live by practitioners, built around real workplace projects, and finishes with a capstone portfolio that proves capability, not just attendance.

AI is everywhere right now, and everyone is talking about what it means for their job. That makes this the perfect moment for a big, confident, energetic launch. The bootcamp is a truly impressive product with a genuinely empowering promise, and "Ahead of the Curve" will make it the most visible AI upskilling offer of the year.

---

## Target Audience

Mid-career professionals worried about AI displacement. These are experienced, accomplished people in their late twenties through forties who read the headlines and feel a knot in their stomach. They are proud of their expertise and anxious about its shelf life. They spend time on LinkedIn, listen to news and business podcasts, and watch a lot of YouTube. They want to feel capable again, and they will lean toward whoever makes them feel that way.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Ahead of the Curve"** flips the AI conversation from fear to advantage. Instead of dwelling on what AI might take away, the creative shows professionals visibly in command of it — running the tools, making the calls, becoming the person their team turns to. Every asset lands the same feeling: you are not being replaced, you are being upgraded.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
Short-form video is a fantastic stage for before-and-after transformation content. We will run a dynamic flight showing real professionals doing their jobs with AI superpowers, plus quick wins and tool demos that make the value feel immediate and exciting.

### 2. YouTube & Podcast Sponsorships
Longer formats let us tell the fuller story. YouTube pre-roll carries polished bootcamp trailers, while host-read spots on business and news podcasts reach anxious professionals right in the middle of their daily AI headlines.

### 3. LinkedIn Paid
LinkedIn is where career anxiety lives, and it is the natural home for a professional-upgrade message. Sponsored content and lead-gen forms will drive directly to the free workshop.

### 4. Free AI Workshop Funnel & Webinars
A live, free "Your First AI Workflow" workshop gives prospects a real taste of the bootcamp experience. It is confidence-building, hands-on, and genuinely useful, and attendees flow into a nurture sequence toward bootcamp enrollment windows.

### 5. Creator Partnerships
We will partner with respected work-and-productivity creators who can demonstrate the skills in their own voice. Their credibility makes the promise believable and their content gives us an authentic drumbeat between our own flights.
