# Cascade Valley Girlfriend-Getaway Social Video Flight — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Cascade Valley Tourism Board
**Campaign window:** March 1 – May 31, 2028
**Budget:** $170,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Group Chat Getaway"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Cascade Valley Group Chat Getaway, a social-video-led flight built around a getaway package positioned at around $399 per person. The package bundles shared lodging, a spa or winery session, a group dinner reservation, and a ready-made 36-hour itinerary designed for groups of friends. The product is easy to book in one transaction for the whole group, which removes the coordination friction that usually kills group trips.

---

## Product Details

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| Price | Around $399 per person, group booking in one transaction |
| Includes | Shared lodging, spa or winery session, group dinner, 36-hour itinerary |
| Group size | 3-6 travelers, flexible room configurations |
| Booking | Single payment link shareable in a group chat |

---

## Target Audience

Groups of women aged 25-40 planning short-notice trips. They plan together on social platforms and in group chats, and they respond well to trips that look fun and are easy to organize. Spring is a popular time for friend trips, and the valley offers plenty for groups to do.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Group Chat Getaway"** leans into how these trips actually start: someone drops a video in the chat and says "we're doing this." Every asset is designed to be shared — short videos that end with "tag the group," plus a booking link built to be pasted straight into the conversation.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video (Reels, TikTok) | Group-trip videos engineered for shares and tags |
| Creator partnerships | Regional creators take their own friend group and document the weekend |
| Paid search | Capture group-trip and girls-weekend searches |
| Landing page & booking funnel | One-link group booking with a shareable itinerary preview |

Social video is very effective for travel inspiration, and share-to-chat behavior is strong for group trip content, so the flight is concentrated there.

## KPIs

- Strong share and tag rates on group-trip video
- Healthy traffic to the group booking funnel

- Solid volume of $399 package bookings across the flight

---

## Why This Will Work

The product removes the real blocker for group trips — coordination — and the creative meets the audience exactly where the trip decision happens. With shareable video and a one-link booking flow, "Group Chat Getaway" turns group chats into booked weekends.
