## Campaign Title

**"#BenefitDrop" — Unlock the Learning Budget You Already Have**

---

## Opening

Workplace-benefits content is having a serious moment. "Ask HR" explainers are blowing up on TikTok, benefits-hack threads go viral on LinkedIn every week, and new-year "money moves" content peaks exactly in our launch window. #BenefitDrop rides all of it at once. The play: turn the discovery that your employer will pay up to $2,000/year for Skillharbor into shareable, thumb-stopping content, and stack enough touchpoints around partner workplaces that the benefit becomes impossible to miss.

---

## The Tactics

### 1. LinkedIn Paid + Display
New-year career content peaks in January, and LinkedIn is the natural home for a "your employer will pay for this" message. Sponsored posts, conversation ads, and display retargeting keep #BenefitDrop in the feed all quarter.

### 2. Partner HR Email & Internal Comms Kits
We arm partner-company HR teams with ready-made internal announcements: email templates, Slack/Teams drop-in posts, intranet banners, and a printable one-pager. HR gets an easy win, we get distribution inside the building.

### 3. Short-Form Video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
"Wait, my job will PAY for this?" is a perfect short-form hook. We produce a run of benefits-hack style videos, faux "HR reaction" skits, and 30-second walkthroughs of claiming the benefit, formatted for trend-speed publishing so we can jump on whatever benefits meme is live that week.

### 4. Lunch-and-Learn Webinar Series
A monthly "Use It or Lose It" virtual lunch-and-learn, co-hosted with partner HR teams, walks employees through the reimbursement process live and ends with a QR code to start enrollment on the spot.

## Who We're Talking To

Employees at Skillharbor's partner companies who have unused tuition benefits — up to $2,000/year already sitting in their compensation package. Most of them either don't know the benefit exists or assume the paperwork is a nightmare. They skew 25-45, they're active on LinkedIn and short-form video, and they pay attention when money is being left on the table.

### 5. Landing Page + Reimbursement Concierge Funnel
A dedicated #BenefitDrop landing page checks eligibility by employer, shows exactly what's covered, and hands users to a "reimbursement concierge" flow where Skillharbor handles the approval paperwork. Removing the admin headache is the conversion unlock.
