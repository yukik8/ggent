## Campaign Title

**"Clearbrook Checks In" — the Follow-Up & Return-Visit Program**

---

## Opening: The Warmest Thing a Clinic Has Ever Done

Introducing Clearbrook Checks In, a genuinely lovely follow-up experience that turns a one-time urgent-care visit into the start of a real relationship. A few days after every visit, patients get a warm, personal check-in text — how's the wrist, how's the fever, did the antibiotics kick in — with one-tap options to book a recheck, message the care team, or explore the $79/month membership. It is thoughtful, human, and beautifully simple, and absolutely nobody else in the footprint is doing anything like it.

The follow-up flow is powered by Clearbrook's shiny new patient-messaging platform, which can trigger perfectly timed sequences off the visit record and personalize every message to the visit reason. It is the kind of small, delightful touch that people screenshot and tell their friends about — healthcare that finally texts you back.

---

## Target Audience

Recent Clearbrook urgent-care visitors. These are people who already chose Clearbrook once, had a good experience, and then mostly never heard from us again. Many of them don't have a regular doctor at all, so there is a huge warm audience sitting right in the patient records. They are busy, appreciate convenience, and respond well to friendly, low-pressure outreach that feels personal rather than promotional.

Secondary audience: household members of recent visitors, who often book on each other's behalf.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Clearbrook Checks In."** Healthcare usually goes silent the moment you walk out the door. We flip that: Clearbrook is the clinic that actually follows up, actually remembers, and actually makes the next step effortless. Every message in the program feels like it came from a caring human, and every message ends with exactly one tap-able next step. Over time, the check-ins gently introduce the membership — because the natural end state of a clinic that keeps checking in is having them as your doctor.

---

## Channel & Tactic Plan

### 1. Lifecycle Email & SMS Program
The heart of the program: a perfectly timed post-visit sequence (day 3 check-in, day 10 recovery note, day 30 "worth having a doctor" nudge) triggered off visit records, with warm copy and one-tap actions. Sequences branch by visit type for a personal feel.

### 2. Retargeting Paid Social
Gentle, warm retargeting to recent visitors and their lookalikes — recovery-tip content, "what happens after your visit" explainers, and membership stories. Nothing salesy; everything in the same caring voice.

### 3. Membership Upsell Landing Experience
A dedicated page that makes the jump from "that clinic I went to once" to "my $79/month doctor" feel obvious, with real member stories and a beautifully clear breakdown of what membership includes.

### 4. Direct Mail Touch
A single, lovely printed piece — a recovery card with the care team's signature — for visits where a physical touch will land hardest. People keep things like this on the fridge.

## Why Clearbrook Wins

Nobody expects a clinic to check in on them, which is exactly why it works. The program takes the huge, warm audience already sitting in Clearbrook's records and gives them the one thing healthcare never gives anyone — a thoughtful follow-up — then makes the next step effortless. It is inexpensive, deeply on-brand, and the kind of experience people genuinely talk about.
