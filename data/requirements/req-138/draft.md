# Hartline "Winter-Ready Weekend" Weatherization Campaign — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Hartline Home & Hardware
**Campaign window:** September 1 – November 30, 2027
**Budget:** $360,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Winter-Ready Weekend"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Hartline Winter-Ready Weekend program, a complete fall weatherization lineup organized into grab-and-go kits priced from $59 to $179. Each kit bundles everything for one job — door and window sealing, pipe insulation, gutter protection, or draft-proofing — so a customer can pick up one box and finish one project in one weekend. The kits are merchandised on a single end-cap with clear good/better/best tiers and a printed checklist inside every box.

Energy costs are a growing topic for homeowners, and interest in efficiency projects tends to rise every fall. Winter-Ready Weekend gives Hartline a structured, easy-to-shop way to capture that seasonal demand across all 40 stores.

## Kit Lineup

| Kit | Contents | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Draft Stopper Kit | Door sweeps, weatherstrip, outlet gaskets | $59 |
| Window Shield Kit | Film, caulk, caulk gun, glazing tape | $89 |
| Pipe & Faucet Kit | Pipe wrap, faucet covers, heat tape | $119 |
| Whole-House Kit | All of the above plus gutter guards | $179 |

## Target Audience

Homeowners aged 30-55 prepping their houses before winter. Many are busy professionals and families who care about comfort and utility bills, and industry reporting suggests a large share of them prefer to do smaller projects themselves when the steps are clear.

## The Big Idea

One weekend, one checklist, one trip. We position Hartline as the place where getting winter-ready is a finished project, not a shopping puzzle. Every asset points to the checklist and the end-cap.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social & YouTube video | Seasonal how-to videos and kit walk-throughs to build awareness |
| How-to content production | A four-part "Winter-Ready in a Weekend" video and article series |
| In-store workshops | Free Saturday weatherization workshops in priority stores |
| Email & CRM | Checklist reminders and kit offers to the Hartline customer file |
| Local digital display & search | Capture "how to winterize" and local hardware searches |

Social video is very effective for home projects, workshops build store traffic, and email keeps the checklist in front of existing customers through the season.
