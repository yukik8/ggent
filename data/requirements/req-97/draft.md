**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft

**Campaign window:** February 1 – May 31, 2028
**Budget:** $450,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Board Games" — Crisp Thins Belong on Every Snackboard**

---

## Opening

Introducing Sunbrook Crisp Thins, a crisp thin enough to act like a cracker and bold enough to stay a crisp. At $4.49 per box, Crisp Thins slide straight into the biggest food trend on the internet right now: the snackboard. Charcuterie boards, girl-dinner boards, butter boards, dessert boards — board content is everywhere, and Crisp Thins are the missing base layer. This is Sunbrook's moment to ride a trend wave that shows no sign of slowing.

---

## The Tactic Stack

We hit the trend from every angle at once:

1. **#BoardGames hashtag challenge** — creators and fans build boards where Crisp Thins are the foundation, escalating from simple to absurd.
2. **Snackboard ASMR content** — crunch-forward, oddly satisfying board-building videos for TikTok and Reels.
3. **Micro-influencer board drops** — we ship "board kits" to niche foodie creators and let them freestyle.
4. **Duet-bait formats** — a weekly "board or bored?" judging format built for stitches and duets.
5. **Trend-jacking calendar** — a standing content desk that reacts within 48 hours whenever a new board trend spikes.
6. **Retail media surge buys** — sponsored placements against cracker and entertaining searches during peak weekends.
7. **In-store sampling boards** — sampling stations serve mini boards, not naked chips, so the trend context travels to shelf.

---

## Target Audience

Primary audience is snack buyers aged 25-44 who trade between crackers and crisps depending on occasion — the exact shopper the snackboard trend activates. They entertain at home, browse food content daily, and buy both categories in the same basket. Secondary audience is existing Sunbrook loyalists curious about the new format.

---

## Channels

- Paid social — short-form video (TikTok, Reels)
- Creator partnerships (niche foodie and entertaining creators)
- Grocery retail media
- In-store sampling & displays

Social carries the trend energy, creators keep it authentic, retail media captures the demand we create, and sampling closes at shelf.
