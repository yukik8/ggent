# Clearbrook Flat-Fee Counseling Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Clearbrook Health
**Campaign window:** January 15 – May 31, 2028
**Budget:** $330,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Talk Isn't a Luxury"**

---

## Opening

Introducing Clearbrook's wonderful new flat-fee counseling service: $119 for a real session with a licensed counselor, booked online in minutes, no insurance maze, no waitlist limbo, no surprise bill. It is a genuinely groundbreaking offer in a space that desperately needs one. The visits happen in the same welcoming Clearbrook clinics people already trust for urgent care, with evening and weekend slots and a beautifully simple booking flow.

Mental health is one of the most talked-about topics in the country right now, and demand for accessible counseling keeps growing. This campaign will introduce Clearbrook's remarkable new service to the Mountain West and make starting therapy feel as easy as booking a haircut.

## Target Audience

Adults under 45 who have thought about therapy but been put off by the cost, the confusing insurance process, or the long waits. They are open, curious, and simply need the barriers removed.

## The Big Idea

**"Talk Isn't a Luxury."** Warm, honest creative that treats counseling as ordinary self-maintenance — like a dental cleaning for your head — always anchored by the $119 price and the booking screen. No stock photos of people staring out of rainy windows; real rooms, real chairs, real prices.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social Video
Gentle, stigma-free short videos across Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube showing how simple the first session is, from tap to appointment.

### 2. Paid Search
Own the "affordable therapy near me" and "counseling without insurance" moments where intent is highest.

### 3. Podcast Audio
Host-read spots on regional podcasts, where honest conversations about mental health already happen.

### 4. Transparent-Price Out-of-Home
Clean billboards with the price front and center: "$119. A real counselor. This week."

### 5. Booking Flow & Landing Content
A dedicated landing page that answers every first-timer question and gets them to a bookable slot in two taps.

The offer is truly special and the creative treats people with warmth and respect. By pairing an honest price with an effortless booking experience and meeting people in trusted, intimate channels like podcasts, "Talk Isn't a Luxury" will make Clearbrook the natural first step for thousands of people who have been waiting for permission to start.
