## Opening: The Problem We Are Actually Solving

The adults Clearbrook wants to reach are not resistant to counseling; they are priced and processed out of it. This is the same disconnected cohort Clearbrook already serves in urgent care: 41% of US adults under 45 have no primary-care physician, up from 33% in 2019 (ref-15), and their route into any care runs through a phone screen, not a referral. Two barriers keep them out of therapy specifically: an unknowable bill, and a front door that fails their booking test — 64% of patients under 40 choose a clinic based on online booking availability before considering reputation (ref-15). The system's waits do the rest: the average wait for a new-patient primary-care appointment in mid-size metros is 26 days (ref-15), and specialty mental-health access is no kinder.

Flat-fee counseling is our answer to that specific problem: $119, a licensed counselor, booked online with evening and weekend slots, in clinics people already trust. The price transparency is not a detail, it is the conversion engine — transparent cash-pay pricing pages increase new-patient bookings by 34% for urgent-care services (ref-15). Every tactic below exists to remove one named barrier: cost opacity or booking friction.

## Target Audience

Adults under 45 priced out of traditional therapy — high-deductible or uninsured, allergic to phone-only intake, deciding at 9pm from a search result. For them the booking test precedes reputation (64%, ref-15), so every asset in this plan ends on a bookable slot.

## The Big Idea

**"Talk Isn't a Luxury."** Warm, honest creative that treats counseling as ordinary self-maintenance — always anchored by the $119 price and the booking screen. Because the core problem is bill fear, the price is the hero of every asset; real rooms, real chairs, real prices.

## Channel & Media Plan

Each channel below removes one of the two named barriers.

### 1. Paid Social Video
Because the barrier is dread of process, the videos show the whole path from tap to appointment in under 30 seconds. Short-form video ads average 2.4x the completion rate of static feed ads (ref-06).

### 2. Paid Search
"Affordable therapy near me" and "counseling without insurance" are the highest-intent expressions of the cost barrier; search routes them straight to the priced booking page (34% booking lift from transparent pricing, ref-15).

### 3. Podcast Audio
Host-read spots where honest mental-health conversations already happen, reaching the cohort in a trusted, low-defense moment.

### 4. Transparent-Price Out-of-Home
"$119. A real counselor. This week." Because the core problem is an unknowable bill, the board is the price — the format proven to move bookings (ref-15).

### 5. Booking Flow & Landing Content
The landing page answers every first-timer question and reaches a bookable slot in two taps, because 64% of this audience screens on booking availability before anything else (ref-15).
