## Campaign Title

**"Faster Together: Ledgerline Connect 2026"**

## Opening

Ledgerline Connect 2026 is the company's flagship user conference, taking place June 9–10 in Nashville. The two-day program includes a product keynote with the year's roadmap reveal, hands-on labs where attendees configure real AP workflows, a new Ledgerline Certified Administrator track, customer-led breakout sessions, a partner expo, and an evening networking reception. Tickets are $399, and attendance is free for existing Ledgerline customers — an easy yes for current users and a low-risk step for prospects.

Connect showcases everything the Ledgerline platform can do, and it is the one moment each year when the entire Ledgerline community is in the same room. Our job is to fill that room. With sustained promotion across the channels finance professionals actually use, we will make Connect 2026 the best-attended edition yet and turn the event into a marketing asset for the rest of the year. It is worth the effort: industry benchmarks consistently suggest that customers who attend a vendor's user conference renew and expand at roughly 20% higher rates than those who don't.

## Target Audience

The primary audience is existing Ledgerline customers in finance roles — controllers, AP managers, and finance directors at 50–500 employee companies. These are busy, practical people who value peer learning and respond far better to other finance teams than to vendor pitches. Many have never met another Ledgerline user in person, so the community angle should resonate strongly.

The secondary audience is warm prospects already in Ledgerline's pipeline — finance leaders who have taken a demo, attended a webinar, or engaged with content but have not yet signed. For them, the conference is a low-pressure way to see the product in action and, more importantly, to talk to real customers. Peer proof carries weight in this category.

## The Big Idea

**"Faster Together"** frames Connect as the place where finance teams get faster by learning from each other. Every close-process improvement, automation trick, and reporting shortcut on the agenda comes from a peer, not a pitch. The creative leads with real attendee voices and session titles ("How we cut our close from ten days to four") rather than generic conference imagery. To customers: your peers will be there, and they're bringing their playbooks. To prospects: come see what a room full of finance teams that solved AP looks like.

## Channel & Media Plan

Finance audiences are professional and channel-selective, so the plan concentrates spend on a small set of channels where they already spend time. Email and LinkedIn are the workhorses of B2B event promotion, and Ledgerline's own customer-facing channels are high-trust reach we should use fully.

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Email & lifecycle program | Segmented invitation streams for customers vs. prospects, agenda reveals, speaker spotlights, deadline reminders |
| LinkedIn (paid + organic) | Sponsored content and speaker/session promos targeting finance titles; organic posts from Ledgerline leaders and confirmed speakers |
| Webinar teaser series | Three short preview webinars in April and May, each hosted by a Connect speaker and closing on a registration call to action |
| In-app & customer marketing | Banners inside the Ledgerline product, CSM outreach kits, and a "bring your team" offer for existing accounts |
| Paid search & retargeting | Capture branded conference searches and re-engage site visitors who viewed the agenda but did not register |

The channels reinforce one another: LinkedIn and email build awareness, the webinars give people a taste of the content, and retargeting closes the loop with anyone who hesitated.

## KPIs

- Best-attended Connect to date, measured against prior-year registration
- Healthy show rate among registered customers
- A meaningful share of registrations from warm prospects, not just existing users
- Above-benchmark engagement across the email program and LinkedIn content
- Positive post-event survey feedback from attendees

## Closing

Connect 2026 is a strong event with a strong offer behind it — free for customers, affordable for prospects, and built around peer-led content. This plan gives it a steady promotional drumbeat from announcement through the final push, concentrated on the channels finance professionals trust. "Faster Together" will fill the room in Nashville and send the Ledgerline community home energized.
