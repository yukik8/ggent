# Ledgerline Connect 2026 User Conference Promotion — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Ledgerline
**Campaign window:** April 1 – June 15, 2026
**Budget:** $260,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Faster Together: Ledgerline Connect 2026"**

## Opening

Ledgerline Connect 2026 is the company's flagship user conference, taking place June 9–10 in Nashville. The event packs a lot into two days: a product keynote with the year's roadmap reveal, hands-on labs where attendees configure real AP workflows, a new Ledgerline Certified Administrator track, customer-led breakout sessions, a partner expo, and an evening networking reception at the venue's rooftop terrace. Tickets are priced at $399, and attendance is free for existing Ledgerline customers — a generous offer that makes the conference an easy yes for current users and an attractive, low-risk step for prospects.

Connect is a fantastic showcase of everything the Ledgerline platform can do, and it is also the one moment each year when the whole Ledgerline community is in the same room. Our campaign will fill that room. With a clean, sustained promotion plan across the channels finance professionals actually use, we can make Connect 2026 the best-attended edition yet and turn the event itself into a marketing asset for the rest of the year.

## Target Audience

The primary audience is existing Ledgerline customers in finance roles — controllers, AP managers, and finance directors at 50–500 employee companies. These are busy, practical people who value peer learning and are far more receptive to hearing from other finance teams than from vendors. Many of them have never met another Ledgerline user in person, so the community angle of the conference should resonate strongly.

The secondary audience is warm prospects already in Ledgerline's pipeline — finance leaders who have taken a demo, attended a webinar, or engaged with content but have not yet signed. For this group, the conference is a low-pressure way to see the product in action and, more importantly, to talk to real customers. Peer proof is very persuasive in this category.

## The Big Idea

**"Faster Together"** frames Connect as the place where finance teams get faster by learning from each other. Every close-process improvement, every automation trick, every reporting shortcut on the agenda comes from a peer, not a pitch. The creative leans on real attendee voices and session titles ("How we cut our close from ten days to four") rather than generic conference imagery. The message to customers: your peers will be there, and they're bringing their playbooks. The message to prospects: come see what a room full of finance teams that solved AP looks like.

## Channel & Media Plan

Finance audiences are professional and channel-selective, so the plan concentrates on a small set of channels where they already spend time. Email and LinkedIn are known to work very well for B2B event promotion, and customer-facing channels are effectively free reach we should use fully.

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Email & lifecycle program | Segmented invitation streams for customers vs. prospects, agenda reveals, speaker spotlights, deadline reminders |
| LinkedIn (paid + organic) | Sponsored content and speaker/session promos targeting finance titles; organic posts from Ledgerline leaders and confirmed speakers |
| Webinar teaser series | Three short "preview" webinars in April and May, each hosted by a Connect speaker, ending with a registration call to action |
| In-app & customer marketing | Banners inside the Ledgerline product, CSM outreach kits, and a "bring your team" offer for existing accounts |
| Paid search & retargeting | Capture branded conference searches and re-engage site visitors who viewed the agenda but did not register |

The channels reinforce each other: LinkedIn and email build awareness, the webinars give people a taste of the content, and retargeting closes the loop with anyone who hesitated.

## KPIs

- Strong overall registration, with the goal of the best-attended Connect to date
- Healthy attendance rate among registered customers
- A solid share of registrations coming from warm prospects, not just existing users
- Good engagement across the email program and LinkedIn content
- Positive post-event survey feedback from attendees

## Closing

Connect 2026 is a great event with a great offer behind it — free for customers, affordable for prospects, and packed with peer-led content. This plan gives it a steady drumbeat of promotion across the channels finance professionals trust, building from announcement to a strong final push. We're confident "Faster Together" will fill the room in Nashville and send the Ledgerline community home energized.
