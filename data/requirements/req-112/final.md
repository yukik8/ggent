## Campaign Title

**"Connect On the Road" — Ledgerline Connect 2028**

## Opening

Introducing Connect On the Road, a five-city roadshow series that takes the energy of the Ledgerline Connect conference on tour. Each stop is a half-day event featuring a product keynote, customer panels, hands-on demo stations, and a networking happy hour. Tickets are $199, with customers attending free. Industry benchmarks consistently rank in-person events among the highest-converting B2B marketing channels, and a touring format brings that value to customers and prospects in their own metros rather than asking them to travel.

Having produced Ledgerline Connect for the past two years, our team can stage this series on proven event infrastructure: the same staging kit, capture crew, registration stack, and vendor relationships, shipped city to city.

## The Big Idea

**"Connect On the Road"** makes Ledgerline the finance event of the spring in each metro. Every stop is designed to travel beyond the room: keynote moments, a customer "close confessions" panel, demo-station challenges, and a per-city highlight reel that fuels promotion for the next stop.

## Tactic Stack

### 1. Venue & Event Production
Five half-day events in premium but cost-efficient venues, run on a consistent staging kit that ships between cities. One production build, five deployments — the touring model keeps per-event cost well below five standalone events.

### 2. Email & CRM Invitations
Segmented invitation waves to customers and warm prospects in each metro, with reminder sequences and post-event nurture flows handed to sales.

### 3. LinkedIn Promotion
Geo-targeted promotion per metro, speaker-card creative, event-page retargeting, and highlight-reel amplification between stops.

### 4. Speaker & Content Program
Customer speakers recruited per city, the "close confessions" panel format, and all session content packaged into clips and recaps for a season-long content drop.

### 5. On-Site Experience & Swag
Demo-station challenges with prizes, a photo moment, and quality swag. The happy hour gives sales unhurried face time with the accounts they most want to advance.

## Target Audience

Existing Ledgerline customers and warm mid-market finance prospects in the five metros. Customers attend for the roadmap and networking; prospects attend to see the product live and meet peers who already use it. The touring format meets both where they are — no flights, no hotel, a half-day commitment.

## Why This Wins

Finance buyers want rooms, not another webinar, and Connect On the Road delivers five of them in the metros where Ledgerline's customers and best prospects already work. With a proven production team, a repeatable staging model that stretches the budget across five cities, and a content engine that keeps each stop working after the chairs are folded, this series turns one event brand into a full spring of pipeline.
