## Campaign Title

**"Plug Into Easy" — the Hartline Smart-Home Starter Push**

## Opening

Introducing the Hartline Smart-Home Starter program: smart-home bundles priced $129-$399 with professional installation included. The bundles cover the categories shoppers ask about most — video doorbells, smart thermostats, leak sensors, and smart lighting kits — pre-configured and installed by Hartline's own team. No hubs to puzzle over, no apps that refuse to pair; Hartline handles everything from the box to the wall.

Smart-home is a strong and growing category — more than half of US households now own at least one smart device — and installation-included bundles give Hartline a distinctive offer within it. This campaign brings that offer to shoppers across the lower Midwest ahead of the holiday season.

## Target Audience

Tech-curious homeowners who like the idea of a smart home but are unsure about setting devices up themselves. They are interested and open to the category; the included installation is the reassurance that gets them to purchase.

## The Big Idea

**"Plug Into Easy."** Every asset shows the moment a smart device simply works — the doorbell chime on the phone, the thermostat warming the house before the alarm — with the Hartline installer waving goodbye. One message throughout: you pick the bundle, we make it work.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social Video
Short videos of real installations and first-use moments on Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube, weighted to evening viewing.

### 2. In-Store Demo Events
Live demo stations in the busiest stores where shoppers can ring the doorbell, dim the lights, and meet the installation team.

### 3. Digital Display & Search
Always-on search coverage for smart-home terms, plus display on regional news and home sites.

### 4. Creator Partnerships
Local home and tech creators film their own installation day and share the experience with their audiences.

- Awareness lift for the installation-included bundles in the trade area

## Why This Will Work

The product is strong and the service attached to it is genuinely differentiating. With clear creative, hands-on demos, and creators telling first-person stories, "Plug Into Easy" positions Hartline as the obvious place to start a smart home this holiday season.
