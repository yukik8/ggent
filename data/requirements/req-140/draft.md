# Hartline Smart-Home Starter Installation Push — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Hartline Home & Hardware
**Campaign window:** September 1 – December 15, 2028
**Budget:** $270,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Plug Into Easy" — the Hartline Smart-Home Starter Push**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Hartline Smart-Home Starter program, an exciting new lineup of smart-home bundles priced $129-$399 that come with professional installation included. These are genuinely impressive bundles: gorgeous video doorbells, brilliant smart thermostats, clever leak sensors, and dazzling smart lighting kits, all pre-configured and installed by Hartline's own friendly team. No hubs to puzzle over, no apps that refuse to pair, no weekend lost to firmware updates — Hartline handles everything from the box to the wall.

Smart-home technology is one of the most exciting categories in the store, and the installation-included angle makes these bundles a truly standout offer. This campaign will bring that magic to shoppers across the lower Midwest and show them how effortless a smarter home can be.

## Target Audience

Tech-curious homeowners who love the idea of a smart home but feel unsure about setting devices up themselves. They are interested, enthusiastic, and just need a nudge of reassurance to take the plunge.

## The Big Idea

**"Plug Into Easy."** Every asset shows the wonderful moment a smart device simply works — the doorbell chime on the phone, the thermostat warming the house before the alarm — with the Hartline installer waving goodbye. The message is simple and delightful: you pick the bundle, we make it work.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social Video
Charming short videos of real installations and happy first moments, running on Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube where our homeowners spend their evenings.

### 2. In-Store Demo Events
Live demo stations in the busiest stores where shoppers can ring the doorbell, dim the lights, and meet the installation team in person.

### 3. Digital Display & Search
Always-on search coverage for smart-home terms plus display placements on regional news and home sites.

### 4. Creator Partnerships
Local home and tech creators film their own installation day and share how easy the whole experience felt.
