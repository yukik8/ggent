# Proposal Draft — Northtrail Haulmate Cargo E-Bike Launch

**Client:** Northtrail Mobility
**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Campaign window:** 2026-05-15 to 2026-09-30
**Budget:** $560,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Room for Everything" — The Northtrail Haulmate Launch**

## Opening

Introducing the Northtrail Haulmate, the most capable cargo e-bike Northtrail has ever built. The Haulmate features an extended rear deck rated for two child seats, a low step-through frame for easy mounting even with a full load, integrated front and rear lighting, hydraulic disc brakes, and Northtrail's signature swappable battery system with an extended-range option. It comes in three colorways with a full accessory ecosystem, from weatherproof panniers to a lockable front cargo box. At $2,799 upfront or $119/month on the bundle plan, the Haulmate brings Northtrail's proven engineering to a whole new category.

This is a product families are going to fall in love with. Cargo bikes are having a real moment right now, and the Haulmate arrives with better build quality and a smarter battery story than anything else out there. Our campaign will introduce this exciting new bike to urban families everywhere and make "Room for Everything" the phrase parents associate with modern family mobility.

## Target Audience

Urban parents aged 30-45 who are looking at replacing second-car trips. More broadly, we should also reach young couples planning families, active empty-nesters who run errands by bike, and general cycling enthusiasts who follow new product launches, since word of mouth in the bike community is very influential. Parents today care about sustainability, health, and spending time with their kids, and the Haulmate speaks to all three.

## The Big Idea

**"Room for Everything"** — a campaign built around real family moments on the Haulmate: the school run, the farmers market haul, the beach day with two kids and a cooler on the back. Every asset shows the bike doing what a second car does, but with more fun and fresh air. The tagline works on two levels, cargo capacity and lifestyle: the Haulmate has room for the groceries, and room for the moments that matter.

The hero asset is a 60-second brand film following one family through a full Saturday on the Haulmate, cut down into 15- and 30-second versions for social. Supporting content shows loading configurations, the battery swap in action, and honest parent testimonials.

## Channel & Media Plan

1. **Short-form social video (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts).** Social is where parents spend their time and short video is extremely effective at driving discovery for products like this. We will run the cut-down hero film plus a stream of lifestyle clips throughout the flight.

2. **Parenting and cycling creator partnerships.** Family-lifestyle creators are very trusted by our audience, and their authentic day-in-the-life content performs really well. We will brief 10-14 creators to document their first month with a Haulmate.

3. **Local test-ride events ("Haulmate Saturdays").** Nothing sells a cargo bike like riding one with your kids on board. We will run weekend demo events in eight mid-size cities across the flight, partnering with local bike shops and family festivals.

4. **Paid search and retargeting.** Capture in-market demand for "cargo e-bike" and related queries, and retarget site visitors with configuration and financing messaging.

5. **Out-of-home in launch cities.** Placements near schools, parks, and grocery anchors to build neighborhood-level familiarity where the demo events happen.

## KPIs

- Video views and completion rate on hero film and cutdowns
- Engagement rate on creator content
- Test-ride sign-ups and event attendance across the 8 cities
- Website sessions to the Haulmate configurator
- Pre-orders and bundle sign-ups attributed to the campaign
- Brand lift in launch cities (survey pre/post)

## Why This Will Work

Families are increasingly open to car-replacement options, the cargo category is growing, and social content about family biking gets great engagement. The Haulmate is simply a better product than what is currently available, and once parents experience it at a demo event or see a creator they trust using one, the product sells itself. "Room for Everything" gives Northtrail an emotional, ownable platform to carry beyond this launch into the whole family line.
