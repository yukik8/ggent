# Northtrail Commuter Two Launch Proposal — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Northtrail Mobility
**Campaign window:** February 15 – June 15, 2028
**Budget:** $640,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Two Steps Ahead" — the Commuter Two Launch**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Northtrail Commuter Two, the next-generation commuter e-bike. The Commuter Two ships with a torque-sensing motor, a redesigned swappable battery with faster charge cycles, integrated smart lights, and an anti-theft GPS module, all at $2,099 upfront or $95/month on the subscription bundle. It is the most complete package Northtrail has ever built, and the launch should feel as next-gen as the bike itself.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Two Steps Ahead"** positions the Commuter Two as the upgrade that puts riders ahead of traffic, ahead of gas prices, and ahead of the curve. Everything in the plan is built to be clipped, stitched, and shared.

---

## Tactic Stack

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Short-form is where launches happen now. We ride current formats — POV commute edits, "car cost vs. bike cost" split screens, before/after upgrade cuts — and refresh creative every two weeks so we never go stale. Vertical-video launch content is dominating the mobility conversation right now and we should be all over it.

### 2. Creator & Influencer Partnerships
We activate commuter-lifestyle creators, personal-finance creators, and a few big cycling names for launch-day drops. Creators get early units and full creative freedom. Unboxings, 30-day commute diaries, duet chains — the works.

### 3. Test-Ride Pop-Up Series
City-center pop-ups with a "Two Minute Test Ride" hook. QR codes everywhere, same-day subscription signups on tablets, photo moments designed for sharing. Pop-ups travel across our launch cities through spring.

### 4. Upgrader CRM & Email
Current Commuter One owners get an early-access window, trade-in messaging, and a loyalty perk. Owned channels are free reach, so we max them.

### 5. City-Targeted Digital & OOH
Billboards and transit ads along commuter corridors plus geo-targeted digital in our launch cities, timed to spring riding season.

---

## Target Audience

Primary audience is urban commuters aged 25-40 in mid-size cities, plus current Commuter One owners who are natural upgraders. These riders are cost-conscious, online, and open to switching from cars. Early-access messaging speaks to owners; the price-and-lifestyle content speaks to new riders.
