---

## Campaign Title

**"The Golden Years Plan"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Pawstead Golden Years Plan, a vet-formulated senior-dog meal line at an average feeding cost of $3.40 per day. The recipes feature joint-support ingredients, controlled calories for weight management, and softer textures for older teeth. Senior pets are having a real moment online — senior-dog adoption content, "old man dog" accounts, and gray-muzzle glow-up videos are everywhere — and no fresh brand has planted a flag on the senior segment yet.

---

## Tactic Stack

We come at this with everything working in pet media right now:

1. **Short-form social video.** Emotional "13 years young" transformation content, gray-muzzle POVs, and before/after mobility clips. Senior-dog content over-performs on shares.
2. **Vet & pet creator partnerships.** Vet creators explain the senior formulation on camera; senior-dog accounts document month one on the plan.
3. **Podcast sponsorships.** Pet and health podcasts with host-read spots about aging dogs.
4. **Paid search & display.** Capture "senior dog food," "dog joint supplement," and related searches.
5. **CRM & email lifecycle.** Age-triggered upgrade offers to existing Pawstead subscribers whose dogs are approaching senior years.

---

## Target Audience

Owners of dogs aged 8+ managing weight and joint health. Many are long-time premium-food buyers who notice their dog slowing down and start researching. They are emotionally invested and willing to spend on more years of health.

---

## The Big Idea

**"More good mornings."** Every asset centers on the moment a senior dog gets up easier, walks farther, or plays again — the everyday wins owners actually watch for.

---

## More Ways to Ride the Trend

- **A "Senior Glow-Up" UGC series** with owner-shot month-one updates
- **Gotcha-day tie-ins** for senior rescue adoptions
- **A printable vet-visit checklist** as a lead magnet for the email program
