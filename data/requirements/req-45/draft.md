**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft

## Campaign Title

**"The Vet's Table: Pawstead in Every Exam Room"**

## Opening

The Pawstead Clinic Partnership Program is a new B2B offering that brings Pawstead's vet-formulated refrigerated meals directly into veterinary practices. The program is built around the Clinic Starter Bundle at $199, which includes a countertop mini-fridge display, a two-week supply of sample meal packs across all four recipes, waiting-room literature, a co-branded feeding transition guide, and a clinic referral code that tracks every subscription a practice generates. Participating clinics also get a dedicated portal where staff can order restocks, view nutrition documentation, and access the formulation notes written by Pawstead's own veterinary nutrition team.

The bundle is designed to be an easy yes for a busy practice. It ships in one box, sets up in under thirty minutes, and gives the front desk something concrete to hand to owners who ask what they should be feeding. Fresh dog food is a growing conversation in exam rooms, and many veterinarians want a brand they can point to with confidence. The Clinic Partnership Program makes Pawstead that brand.

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is veterinarians, practice managers, and vet techs at small-animal and mixed practices in the top 20 US metros. These are busy clinical professionals who are careful about what they recommend and skeptical of pet food marketing. They respond to credentials, documentation, and peer validation rather than consumer-style advertising. Practice managers are a key secondary decision-maker, since they control what takes up counter space and staff time.

Vet professionals spend time in trade publications, at regional and national conferences, in professional groups online, and in continuing-education settings. Reaching them means showing up in those professional spaces with substance, not slogans.

## The Big Idea

**"The Vet's Table" — the fresh food brand that earns its place in the exam room.**

Rather than asking clinics to endorse a consumer brand, we invite them to join a professional program with real materials, real formulation transparency, and a real revenue-sharing referral mechanic. The creative platform centers Pawstead's veterinary nutrition team talking peer-to-peer with practicing vets, with the message that Pawstead was built at the vet's table, not in a marketing meeting. Every touchpoint drives to one action: order the $199 Clinic Starter Bundle.

## Channel & Media Plan

Veterinary professionals are best reached through trade and professional channels, and peer-to-peer content is very effective with this audience, so the plan concentrates on the places where clinical trust is built.

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Veterinary conference & trade show presence | Booth activations at major spring/summer veterinary conferences with live starter-bundle demos and on-site sign-up incentives |
| Clinic sample kit direct mail | Targeted mailing of a mini sample kit to priority practices in the top 20 metros, with a personalized code for the full $199 bundle |
| Veterinary trade media & newsletters | Sponsored articles and newsletter placements in the trade outlets vets and practice managers already read |
| LinkedIn & professional digital | Sponsored content and retargeting aimed at veterinarians and practice managers by title and specialty |
| Lunch-and-learn & webinar program | Pawstead nutrition team hosts in-clinic lunch-and-learns and monthly webinars where staff can ask formulation questions directly |

Conferences give us high-intent, face-to-face moments. The direct mail kits put product physically in clinic hands, which matters for a refrigerated food. Trade media and LinkedIn keep the program visible between touchpoints, and the lunch-and-learn program converts interest into committed partner clinics.

## Why This Will Work

Vets are the most trusted voice in pet nutrition, and clinics are actively looking for fresh food answers they can stand behind. The Clinic Starter Bundle removes friction, the referral mechanic aligns incentives, and the peer-to-peer creative treats vet professionals like the experts they are. With conferences, direct mail, trade media, professional digital, and the lunch-and-learn program working together, "The Vet's Table" gives Pawstead a durable clinical channel that keeps paying off long after the campaign window closes.
