**Budget:** $170,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The Hartline Gift Shop" — Holiday Gift Guide Campaign**

---

## Opening: Introducing the Hartline Gift Guide

Introducing the Hartline Gift Guide: a curated holiday catalog of gift bundles priced $25 to $199, organized by the person you're shopping for rather than by aisle. The guide takes Hartline's holiday assortment and packages it into ready-to-give bundles, each one wrapped-counter ready with a printed gift card.

| Gift Bundle Tier | Price Range | Example Bundles |
|---|---|---|
| Stocking Tier | $25 – $49 | Tape-measure trio, headlamp kit, garage organizer set |
| Weekend DIYer | $50 – $99 | Cordless driver starter, precision screwdriver chest |
| Serious Hobbyist | $100 – $149 | Router + bit set, mechanic's tool chest starter |
| The Big Gift | $150 – $199 | Compound miter package, pressure washer bundle |

The guide ships as a printed piece, lives as a digital flipbook and landing page, and drives to both in-store gift displays and buy-online-pickup-in-store for last-minute shoppers.

---

## Target Audience

Gift shoppers buying for the DIYers in their lives — spouses, parents, and adult children who know their person loves projects but have no idea what a decent gift under the tree looks like. They want confidence that the gift will land, at a price tier they choose.

---

## The Big Idea

**"You know the person. We know the present."** The Gift Guide does the expertise for the shopper: pick the person, pick the tier, grab the bundle. No tool knowledge required.

## Channel Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Printed & digital gift guide | The campaign's core asset, mailed and distributed in store |
| Paid social & video | Gift-reveal creative driving to the guide and landing page |
| Email & CRM | Guide launch, tier spotlights, last-shipping-day and pickup reminders |
| In-store gift shop merchandising | Front-of-store gift displays matching the guide's tiers |
| Local search & display | Capture "gifts for dad who likes tools" style searches |

### 1. The Gift Guide
Printed guides mail to the customer list and stack at every register; the digital flipbook and landing page mirror the tiers with direct add-to-cart.

### 2. Paid Social & Video
Gift-reveal spots — the wrapped bundle, the reaction — run across Reels and TikTok through the season. Social is an effective channel for holiday gift discovery.

### 3. Email & CRM
A launch send, weekly tier spotlights, and a final week of last-minute messaging built around two-hour pickup for procrastinators.

### 4. In-Store Gift Shop Merchandising
A front-of-store gift zone stacks the bundles by tier with clear "for the person who..." signage, so a gift shopper never has to enter the tool aisles.

### 5. Local Search & Display
Search coverage on gift-intent queries in each store's radius, with display retargeting to guide visitors.

## Why Hartline Wins

The Gift Guide turns Hartline's biggest seasonal weakness — gift shoppers who don't know tools — into its easiest sale. The bundles remove the guesswork, the tiers remove the budget anxiety, and the guide gives the whole season one organizing asset that works in the mailbox, the feed, and the front of the store.
