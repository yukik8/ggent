## Campaign Title

**"Every Bowl Counts" — the Blue Fork Rewards Relaunch**

---

## Opening: A Gorgeous New App for a Beloved Brand

Introducing the all-new Blue Fork Rewards app — a complete, beautiful reimagining of Blue Fork Kitchen's loyalty experience. The relaunched app brings together everything guests could want in one sleek, joyful package: a simple points system where every dollar earns a point, a "My Usual" one-tap reorder button that remembers each guest's favorite build, a visual bowl-streak tracker that fills up with every visit, and surprise "Fork Drops" — spontaneous rewards like free toppings or a seasonal side that appear in the app when guests least expect them. The whole experience has been redesigned with warm colors, playful animations, and lightning-fast order-ahead that makes lunch feel effortless.

The app is completely free to download, and with an average check of $12.90, guests will rack up points quickly toward genuinely exciting rewards. Loyalty apps are incredibly powerful in the restaurant world right now, and everyone orders on their phones these days. This relaunch is Blue Fork's chance to turn its wonderful food into a wonderful habit, and to give the brand's 85 locations a digital experience as fresh as its grain bowls.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is Blue Fork's existing guests who currently visit once or twice per month. These are people who already know and enjoy the brand — busy professionals grabbing lunch near the office, students, and families who treat Blue Fork as a reliable, feel-good option. They love the build-your-own bowls, they appreciate fresh ingredients, and they're very comfortable ordering ahead on their phones. Many of them are the kind of guests who influence where their coworkers and friends eat, so winning them over more often has a lovely ripple effect.

The opportunity is simple and exciting: these guests already like Blue Fork, they just don't think of it quite often enough. A rewarding, delightful app experience gives them lots of little reasons to come back more frequently.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Every Bowl Counts"** turns loyalty from a punch card into a story. Every visit fills the guest's bowl-streak tracker, every streak unlocks something tasty, and every "Fork Drop" makes opening the app feel like a small gift. The campaign celebrates the ritual of the regular — the Tuesday bowl, the payday treat, the post-gym pickup — and tells guests that at Blue Fork, being a regular actually pays. It's warm, it's personal, and it makes frequency feel fun instead of transactional.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Lifecycle CRM — Email, Push & SMS
The relaunch will be powered by a rich program of lifecycle messages: a warm migration series for existing members, streak reminders, points-balance nudges when guests are close to a reward, and win-back messages for anyone who hasn't visited in a while. Email and push are great for keeping the app top of mind, and SMS is a really effective way to drive guests in for limited-time offers. Personalized messaging like this tends to perform much better than generic blasts.

### 2. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Instagram Reels & TikTok)
We'll run geo-targeted Reels and TikTok flights around all 85 locations showing the app's most delightful moments — the streak tracker filling up, a Fork Drop landing, the "My Usual" button in action. Short-form video is hugely popular with our audience and food content performs wonderfully in the format. Paid social will drive both app downloads and re-engagement from lapsed members.

### 3. Local Creator Partnerships
We'll partner with food and lifestyle creators in Blue Fork's core Southeastern markets to share their own bowl rituals and streaks. Creator content feels authentic and relatable, and local creators are especially trusted by the lunch crowds we want to reach. Their content also gives us a steady supply of assets to amplify through paid.

### 4. In-Store Merchandising & Team Activation
The relaunch has to live in the restaurants too. We'll deck out all 85 locations with counter cards, bag stuffers, and pickup-shelf signage, and equip team members with a simple script and incentive program for inviting every guest to join. Staff enthusiasm is one of the best drivers of app sign-ups, and the in-store moment is where the habit actually happens.
