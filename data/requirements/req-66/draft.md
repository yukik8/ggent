## Campaign Title

**"The Receipts" — Skillharbor Salary-Outcomes Campaign**

## Opening

Skillharbor's all-access subscription is one of the strongest products in online learning: a $199/month membership covering the full course library, live cohort upgrades, career services, and an alumni community that keeps growing. The platform now has years of graduate outcomes behind it, including salary increases, title changes, and successful career pivots, and this campaign is built to put those outcomes front and center.

"The Receipts" packages Skillharbor's graduate results into a proof-led campaign. Instead of describing what the platform teaches, we show what graduates earned. The creative system is built around real numbers from real alumni, presented cleanly and confidently across every channel.

## Target Audience

Prospective learners who are skeptical that online courses pay off. Many of them have tried a course before, or watched a colleague pay for one and get nothing from it, and they now treat education marketing with suspicion. They research carefully before spending, and they respond better to evidence than to enthusiasm.

## The Big Idea

**"The Receipts" — don't take our word for it, take theirs.**

Every asset is structured as a receipt: name, program, before-salary, after-salary. The visual system uses a clean receipt motif across video, static, and landing page. The message discipline is strict — no aspirational language, no stock imagery of laptops on beaches, just documented outcomes presented in a consistent format.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Receipt-format alumni videos; primary reach and traffic driver |
| YouTube pre-roll | Longer alumni stories with outcome figures; builds credibility with research-minded prospects |
| Alumni testimonial production | A library of documented outcome stories that feeds every other channel |
| Landing page + salary-outcomes report | A downloadable outcomes report and program-level results hub as the campaign's proof center |
| Retargeting & email nurture | Sequential outcome stories for visitors who did not convert on the first touch |
| Creator partnerships | Career creators review the outcomes report and pressure-test the numbers on their own channels |

Social video reaches this audience efficiently, and testimonial-style content tends to perform well. The landing page gives careful researchers a place to dig into the details, while retargeting and email keep the conversation going through their consideration window.

## KPIs

- Reach and video completion across the receipt-format flight
- Outcomes-report downloads and landing page engagement depth
- Free-trial starts attributable to the campaign
- Trial-to-paid conversion at the $199/month tier
- Brand-trust lift among skeptical prospects (pre/post survey)

## Why This Will Work

Skeptical audiences do not need more inspiration; they need proof, and Skillharbor is one of the few platforms that actually has it. The receipt format is distinctive, repeatable, and honest, and it turns the client's outcomes data into the campaign's creative engine. With video building reach, the report rewarding deeper research, and retargeting working the long consideration window, "The Receipts" meets skeptics on their own terms and converts them.
