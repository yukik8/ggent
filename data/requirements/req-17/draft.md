---

## Campaign Title

**"Crunch Time" — the Sunbrook Campus Takeover**

---

## Opening: Sunbrook Goes to College

Introducing the Sunbrook Crisps campus-town launch — the most energetic expansion in the brand's history. For the first time, Sunbrook is showing up exactly where college life happens: campus-adjacent grocery stores, corner marts, and the delivery apps students order from at midnight. The lineup is built for the way students actually snack, with the classic $3.99 bag for solo study sessions and an all-new $6.49 share size made for dorm hangouts, movie nights, and game-day watch parties.

The share size is honestly a game-changer. It's big enough to pass around a common room, the resealable bag keeps everything crunchy through a long week, and the vibrant Sunbrook packaging looks fantastic sitting on a dorm shelf or popping up in a delivery bag. Add in Sunbrook's genuinely craveable flavor range, and you have a product line that fits seamlessly into the rhythms of campus life. College towns are packed with hungry, adventurous snackers, and delivery culture on campus is bigger than ever. This is the perfect moment to make Sunbrook the go-to crisp in ten Midwest campus towns.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is college students aged 18-24 in ten Midwest campus towns. These students are social, spontaneous, and always snacking — between classes, during all-nighters, at intramural games, and while streaming with roommates. They live on their phones, they order a lot of delivery, and they love brands that feel fun and a little playful. Word travels incredibly fast on a campus, so if Sunbrook becomes the snack people bring to hangouts, momentum builds on its own.

Secondary audiences include campus-town residents and staff who shop the same stores, plus parents assembling care packages during exam season.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Crunch Time"** turns Sunbrook into the official fuel of the campus grind. Every semester has its crunch moments — midterms, finals week, the big game, the group project due at 9 a.m. — and Sunbrook shows up for all of them. The campaign celebrates the late-night study session as a shared ritual: you order the share size, you pass the bag, you get through it together. Creative leans into relatable campus humor (the library at 2 a.m., the roommate who "just wants a few"), and the share size becomes the hero of every crunch moment. It's warm, funny, and instantly recognizable to anyone who has ever crammed for an exam.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
Students basically live inside short-form video, so this is where the campaign makes its first impression. We'll run a steady flight of TikTok, Reels, and Shorts creative built around Crunch Time moments — study-session sketches, share-size reveals, and delivery unboxings — geo-targeted to our ten campus towns. Short video is a super effective way to build buzz fast with this crowd.

### 2. Campus Micro-Creator Partnerships
Every campus has its own creators — the meme account everyone follows, the day-in-my-life vlogger, the club sports personality. We'll recruit a roster of student creators in each town to weave Sunbrook into their real campus content. Students trust other students far more than they trust polished brand ads, and this content will feel native to each school's culture.

### 3. Delivery-App Placements & Late-Night Bundles
Delivery apps are huge with students, especially late at night. We'll run sponsored product placements on the major delivery platforms serving our campus towns and create a "Crunch Time Bundle" (share size + drink pairing) that surfaces during evening ordering windows. When the craving hits at midnight, Sunbrook will be the first crisp on the screen.

### 4. Campus Sampling & Event Activations
Tasting is believing. We'll set up Crunch Time sampling stations at campus-adjacent grocery stores, student unions, and big spring moments — basketball watch parties, spring fests, and finals-week "fuel stations" in library lobbies with free single-serve bags. Sampling gets the product into hands and gives people a reason to grab the share size on their next store run.

### 5. Crunch Time Club — SMS & Email
Students love a good deal drop. We'll build a simple opt-in SMS and email list — the Crunch Time Club — that sends limited-time offers around midterms and finals, share-size promos before game weekends, and delivery bundle codes. It keeps Sunbrook top of mind at exactly the moments students are snacking hardest.

## Why Sunbrook Wins
