## Campaign Title

**"Your First Fix" — the Hartline Starter Project Campaign**

---

## Opening: Introducing the First Fix Bundles

Introducing the Hartline First Fix Bundles: a curated line of starter project kits priced from $79 to $249 that give new homeowners everything they need for one complete project in one box. Each bundle contains the exact tools, hardware, and materials for a single project — Mount a Shelf, Hang It Right (curtains and towel bars), Fix That Faucet, and First Yard Kit — plus a printed step-by-step guide and a QR code linking to a companion video.

The bundles solve product selection for the customer: no comparing fourteen caulk guns, no guessing at anchor sizes. They are exclusive to Hartline's 40 stores and the Hartline website, and each one is backed by the "Finish It Guarantee" — if a customer gets stuck, any Hartline associate will walk them through the next step in store.

| Bundle | Price | Contents |
|---|---|---|
| Hang It Right | $79 | Level, stud finder, anchors, drill bits, guide |
| Mount a Shelf | $129 | Shelf hardware set, level, anchor kit, driver bits, guide |
| Fix That Faucet | $149 | Basin wrench, plumber's tape, washers, cartridge puller, guide |
| First Yard Kit | $249 | Spreader, seed, gloves, hose nozzle, soil test, guide |

---

## Target Audience

First-time homeowners within 12 months of purchase across Hartline's lower-Midwest trade areas. They are new to home ownership, motivated to improve their homes, and active on social platforms and YouTube. Many of them research projects extensively before buying anything.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Your First Fix"** turns the intimidating first project into a guided, guaranteed experience. Every touchpoint drives to one action: pick your first project, grab the bundle, finish it this weekend.

## Channel Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| How-to video series (YouTube + site) | Four full project walkthroughs matching the four bundles |
| Paid social (Reels, TikTok, YouTube) | Cut-downs of the how-to series plus bundle-focused creative |
| New-homeowner welcome program | Email and direct-mail sequence triggered by new-mover data |
| In-store "First Project" zone | Front-of-store bundle displays with associate training |
| Local community & geo-targeted digital | Neighborhood Facebook group presence and geo-targeted ads around all 40 stores |

### 1. How-To Video Series
We will produce four polished project walkthrough videos, one per bundle, hosted by Hartline associates. Video is a strong format for home improvement content and gives the campaign a durable content asset.

### 2. Paid Social
Short-form cut-downs will run on Reels, TikTok, and YouTube. Social video is very effective for reaching younger homeowners and should carry awareness for the campaign.

### 3. New-Homeowner Welcome Program
Using new-mover lists, we will send a welcome mailer with a bundle offer, followed by a four-email onboarding sequence that introduces one project at a time.

### 4. In-Store "First Project" Zone
Each store gets a front-of-store display with all four bundles, project boards, and trained associates who ask, "What are you working on?"

### 5. Local Community & Geo-Targeted Digital
We will maintain a presence in neighborhood Facebook groups and run geo-targeted ads in a radius around each store.
