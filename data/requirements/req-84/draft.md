## Campaign Title

**"Hartline Pro Crew" — the Contractor Loyalty Launch**

---

## Opening: Introducing Pro Crew

Introducing Hartline Pro Crew: a loyalty program built specifically for independent contractors and small crews. Members earn a 5% pro rebate on qualifying purchases, paid out quarterly, plus a set of working-day benefits designed around how pros actually buy.

| Program Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| 5% pro rebate tier | Quarterly payout on qualifying purchases |
| Dedicated pro desk | Priority service and phone-ahead order staging at all 40 stores |
| Early hours access | Doors open for members 30 minutes before public hours |
| Bulk pricing | Job-lot quantities on fasteners, lumber, and consumables |
| Crew accounts | One rebate account, multiple authorized buyers per crew |

The program is simple to join (sign up at the pro desk or online in under five minutes), simple to use (scan the Pro Crew card or phone number at checkout), and simple to value (5% back, no points math).

---

## Target Audience

Independent contractors and small crews — remodelers, handymen, painters, landscapers, and specialty trades — working in Hartline's lower-Midwest trade areas. They buy frequently, often daily, and value speed and reliability at the counter.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Your crew's got a crew."** Pro Crew positions Hartline as the supplier that treats a two-person operation like a valued account, not a walk-in. The launch campaign puts the 5% rebate front and center with proof of the working-day benefits behind it.

---

## Channel Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Field team & counter activation | Pro desk associates and a traveling field team sign crews up in person |
| Trade media (radio, jobsite OOH) | Drive-time radio and boards near supply routes and jobsite corridors |
| Email & SMS lifecycle program | Onboarding sequence, rebate statements, restock reminders |
| Paid search & social | Capture contractors searching for suppliers; retarget site visitors |
| Launch materials | Pro desk kits, crew welcome packs, vehicle decals |

### 1. Field Team & Counter Activation
A dedicated field team visits jobsites and supply stops in each market, while pro desk associates enroll walk-in contractors. In-person enrollment is the most reliable way to reach this audience.

### 2. Trade Media
Drive-time radio and out-of-home along supply routes reach contractors during their working day, when suppliers are top of mind.

### 3. Email & SMS
Members receive an onboarding sequence, quarterly rebate statements, and restock reminders. SMS is useful for time-sensitive messages like early-hours events and bulk deals.

### 4. Paid Search & Social
Search captures contractors actively looking for suppliers and rebate programs, and social retargeting keeps Pro Crew in front of site visitors who have not yet enrolled.

## Why Hartline Wins

Pro Crew is a clean, generous program with benefits contractors can feel on a working day: rebates without points math, a desk that knows their name, and doors that open early. The launch plan meets pros where they already are — the counter, the truck, the jobsite — and gives Hartline a durable loyalty asset in its most valuable customer segment.
