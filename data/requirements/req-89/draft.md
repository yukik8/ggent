---

## Campaign Title

**"Book It Like a Haircut" — the Clearbrook Online Booking Push**

---

## Opening: A Beautifully Simple New Way to See a Doctor

Introducing Clearbrook's gorgeous new online booking experience — a fast, frictionless, genuinely delightful scheduling flow that lets patients grab a real appointment in under a minute, from any device, at any hour. No hold music, no phone tag, no "our next opening is next month." The booking tool shows live availability across every Clearbrook location, confirms instantly, and sends a friendly reminder text. Booking is completely free, and for cash-pay patients the urgent-care visit itself is a refreshingly simple $149 flat — one clean number, no surprises.

This is honestly one of the slickest booking products we have seen from any regional health network, and it deserves a launch campaign with the same energy. Our job is to make sure every patient under 40 in the Clearbrook metro footprint knows they can now book a doctor the way they book everything else in their life — instantly, from their phone. Once people try it, we are confident they will never go back to calling.

---

## Target Audience

Patients under 40 in Clearbrook's metro footprint. These are busy, mobile-first people who run their entire lives from their phones — rides, food, banking, dating — and they find it baffling that healthcare still expects a phone call during business hours. Many of them put off care entirely rather than deal with the hassle. They are extremely receptive to anything that saves them time and feels modern, and word spreads fast in this group when something actually works.

Secondary audience: parents booking for kids after hours, and anyone who has ever given up on hold.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Book It Like a Haircut."** You book your barber, your nails, your oil change, and your dinner reservation online — why is a doctor's appointment the one thing stuck in 1995? The creative is playful and relatable: split-screen spots showing someone booking a haircut in twelve seconds next to someone trapped on hold with a clinic, ending on the Clearbrook booking flow doing its thing in real time. Every asset ends with the same effortless payoff: **Clearbrook. Book it like everything else.**

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
Short-form video is the perfect stage for the split-screen concept, and our audience practically lives there. We will run a strong flight of playful booking-demo videos and real-patient reaction content. Social will be the campaign's awareness engine.

### 2. Paid Search & Google Maps / Local Listings
When someone searches "urgent care near me" at 9pm, Clearbrook needs to be the listing with a big beautiful **Book online** button. We will run always-on search coverage and make sure every location listing is optimized so booking is one tap from the map.

### 3. Out-of-Home — Transit & Billboards
Bold, minimal boards on commuter routes: "Sore throat? Booked by the next stoplight." OOH gives the campaign real-world presence and makes the $149 flat price impossible to miss.

### 4. Landing Page & Booking-Flow Creative
A dedicated campaign landing page that drops visitors directly into the booking flow, plus polished demo videos and screenshots for use across every channel.

### 5. Email & SMS to Existing Patients
Clearbrook already has thousands of past patients who still call. A short, warm series shows them the new flow and invites them to book their next visit online.
