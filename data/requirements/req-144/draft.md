# Clearbrook Back-to-School Sports Physicals Campaign — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Clearbrook Health
**Campaign window:** July 1 – September 15, 2028
**Budget:** $180,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Game-Ready in 20 Minutes"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Clearbrook sports physical program for the 2028 back-to-school season: $39 flat-fee physicals, free for members, bookable online with evening and weekend slots across the clinic footprint. The visit is designed to be fast and complete — forms signed on the spot, records sent to the school, done in about 20 minutes. It is a simple product with a clear seasonal deadline, which makes it a strong recurring campaign for Clearbrook.

Every student athlete needs a signed physical before the season starts, and families tend to leave it late. The campaign concentrates media in the weeks when the deadline pressure peaks.

## Program Summary

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Price | $39 flat; free for members |
| Booking | Online, same-week slots, evening and weekend hours |
| Visit | About 20 minutes, school forms completed on site |
| Footprint | All Clearbrook clinics in the network |

## Target Audience

Parents of school athletes in the clinic footprint. Most are juggling registration deadlines across multiple kids and sports, and industry reporting suggests convenience and scheduling flexibility drive most of the clinic choice for this kind of visit.

## The Big Idea

Own the deadline. Position Clearbrook as the fastest way to check the physical off the back-to-school list — book tonight, done this week, forms in hand.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Local paid social | Deadline-countdown creative to parents in clinic zip codes |
| Search & maps | Own "sports physical near me" and school-form searches |
| School & league partnerships | Flyers, registration-packet inserts, coach emails through district and league partners |
| Out-of-home | Boards near schools and practice fields with the price and booking message |
| Email & SMS | Reminders to the existing patient file, plus a member call-out |

The product is simple, the deadline is real, and the price is easy to remember. Concentrated media plus school and league distribution puts Clearbrook in front of parents at exactly the moment the form is due.
