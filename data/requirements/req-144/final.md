## Campaign Title

**"Game-Ready in 20 Minutes"**

## Opening

Introducing the Clearbrook sports physical program for the 2028 back-to-school season: $39 flat-fee physicals, free for members, bookable online with evening and weekend slots across the clinic footprint. The visit is built to be fast and complete — forms signed on the spot, records sent to the school, done in about 20 minutes. A simple product with a hard seasonal deadline makes this a strong recurring franchise for Clearbrook.

Every student athlete needs a signed physical before the season starts, and most families leave it late — a majority of sports physicals are booked within three weeks of the school deadline. The plan concentrates media exactly where that pressure peaks.

## Program Summary

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Price | $39 flat; free for members |
| Booking | Online, same-week slots, evening and weekend hours |
| Visit | About 20 minutes, school forms completed on site |
| Footprint | All Clearbrook clinics in the network |

## Target Audience

Parents of school athletes in the clinic footprint, typically juggling registration deadlines across multiple kids and sports. Convenience and scheduling flexibility drive clinic choice for this visit type, which plays directly to Clearbrook's online booking and extended hours.

## The Big Idea

Own the deadline. Clearbrook is the fastest way to check the physical off the back-to-school list — book tonight, done this week, forms in hand.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Local paid social | Deadline-countdown creative to parents in clinic zip codes |
| Search & maps | Own "sports physical near me" and school-form searches |
| School & league partnerships | Flyers, registration-packet inserts, coach emails through district and league partners |
| Out-of-home | Boards near schools and practice fields with the price and booking message |
| Email & SMS | Reminders to the existing patient file, plus a member call-out |
| Production | Lightweight seasonal creative kit |

- Sports physical bookings versus last season

## Why This Will Work

The product is simple, the deadline is real, and the price is easy to remember. Concentrated seasonal media plus school and league distribution puts Clearbrook in front of parents at exactly the moment the form is due, and the member benefit gives each visit a path to a longer relationship.
