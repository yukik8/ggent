# Client Request — Specialty Retail Launch (National Beauty Chains)

**To:** Meridian & Craft
**From:** Nadia Chen, Head of Brand, Velura Skin
**Date received:** 2025-12-04
**Subject:** RFP — Velura's first physical retail launch, spring 2026

---

Hi team,

Big news first: we signed the deal. Velura is going into national beauty chains starting this winter — our first time on a physical shelf, ever. This is the largest investment in the company's history and I need the launch to land. Formal brief below.

## The situation

Five years as a DTC-only brand, and it's carried us a long way. But we've watched where new clean-beauty customers actually come from these days, and it isn't brand websites — it's the beauty chains, where we've simply been invisible until now. That said, this brief isn't about soul-searching: we're on the shelf in mid-January, hero SKUs priced **$28-$46**, and I want the loudest, most beautiful arrival the clean-beauty set has ever seen.

Our packaging is gorgeous, our ingredient story is best-in-class, and the community is passionate. Bring all of it into the aisle.

## What we want

A retail launch campaign: in-store presence that stops shoppers, launch buzz on social, creators talking about finding us in real life, events if they earn their keep. We want people who've only seen Velura on a screen to walk past a shelf and go "it's *here*."

One caution from our data, mentioned mostly so you know I know: shoppers in these chains browse skeptically — they stand there googling a brand before it goes in the basket, and our price points sit right in the zone where people double-check everything. Something to bear in mind around the edges.

## Target

In-store beauty shoppers aged 20-35 discovering clean-beauty brands. The label-readers, the skincare hobbyists, the gift buyers.

## Budget & timeline

- **Budget:** $720,000
- **Window:** 2026-01-15 to 2026-05-31

## Success metric

The retailer will judge us fast, so we will too: **average sell-through of 15 units per store per week across launch-wave stores by the end of the window**, and a retailer decision to expand our shelf placement. That second one is existential.

## Deliverables

- Launch strategy and creative platform
- In-store activation design direction
- Channel/media plan with budget breakdown
- Timeline and KPI framework

## Process

I have to be honest with you: our board has mandated a competitive review at this spend level — three agencies, including the retail-marketing shop the chains themselves recommended. You know our brand better than anyone, so show them why that matters. Proposals due December 19; decision in January.

Wish us luck,
Nadia
