## Campaign Title

**"Clean, In Person"**

---

## Opening

Introducing Velura to the beauty aisle. Velura Skin has built one of the most loved clean-beauty lines in the DTC space, anchored by its fragrance-free Barrier Repair Moisturizer and a hero SKU range priced from $28 to $46. The formulas are dermatologist-tested, fully fragrance-free, and built around short, readable ingredient lists that customers genuinely trust. Now, for the first time, shoppers will be able to pick Velura up off a real shelf in national beauty chains.

This is a huge moment for the brand. Velura's packaging is beautiful, its ingredient story is best-in-class, and its community is passionate. Our campaign will bring all of that energy into physical retail and make Velura the most exciting new arrival in the clean-beauty set. Young consumers love clean beauty, the category is growing fast, and specialty retail is where beauty discovery really happens. Velura deserves to be there, and this campaign will make sure shoppers know it has arrived.

---

## Target Audience

In-store beauty shoppers aged 20-35 who are discovering clean-beauty brands. This is a broad and enthusiastic group: students, young professionals, skincare hobbyists, and gift buyers. They browse beauty chains regularly, they care about ingredients, and they are always open to trying something new. Many of them already follow skincare content online, so they will recognize the clean-beauty cues in Velura's branding when they see it on shelf.

---

## The Big Idea: "The Open Shelf"

Velura's whole brand is transparency, so our shelf presence should be too. **The Open Shelf** turns the Velura in-store display into a giant, readable ingredient label. Every fixture, endcap, and shelf-talker literally shows what is inside the jar, with nothing hidden and nothing abbreviated. Testers sit in an open tray shaped like the label itself, inviting shoppers to try before they buy.

The same visual system extends to social content, creator videos, and out-of-home near stores, so a shopper who sees Velura online will immediately recognize the display in the aisle. It is simple, ownable, and unmistakably Velura.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

1. **In-store activation & sampling** — Branded Open Shelf fixtures, endcaps, and tester bars in launch-wave stores, supported by trained brand ambassadors on peak weekend traffic days. In-store sampling is a proven way to drive trial for beauty products.

2. **Short-form video / paid social** — Reels, TikTok, and Shorts content showing the Open Shelf, ingredient close-ups, and "first time seeing Velura in real life" moments. Social is very effective for beauty brands and short video performs especially well with younger shoppers.

3. **Creator partnerships** — A roster of skincare creators visits stores, films honest first-impression reviews, and posts shelf-location content. Creators are hugely influential in the skincare space and their audiences trust them.

4. **Retail launch events** — Opening-week events in flagship locations with skin consultations, mini-facials, and gift-with-purchase. Events generate buzz and give the retailer a reason to feature Velura prominently.

5. **Out-of-home near stores** — Transit and street-level placements within walking distance of launch stores, using the Open Shelf visual language to drive foot traffic in.
