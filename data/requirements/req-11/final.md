# Campaign Proposal: "Baked Different" — Sunbrook Better-For-You Baked Crisps Launch

**Client:** Sunbrook Foods
**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Reviewed by:** Daniel Okafor, Senior Planner
**Version:** Final
**Campaign window:** April 1, 2026 – August 31, 2026
**Budget:** $480,000

---

## 1. Opening: Introducing Sunbrook Baked

Sunbrook is about to launch something genuinely exciting: a new better-for-you sub-line of baked crisps. These are not just another diet snack. Sunbrook Baked crisps are oven-baked rather than fried, made with the same real-ingredient recipes that made Sunbrook Crisps a Midwest favorite, and they arrive in three launch flavors — Sea Salt & Cracked Pepper, Honey Sriracha, and Garden Herb. The refreshed packaging uses a lighter, matte design with a clear "Baked, Never Fried" call-out on the front panel, giving the line strong standout on shelf.

At $4.49 per bag, Sunbrook Baked sits at an accessible premium that signals quality without feeling out of reach. Better-for-you snacking continues to grow year over year, and consumers are actively seeking options that taste great and feel better to eat. This launch is Sunbrook's opportunity to bring its flavor credentials into that space. Our campaign, "Baked Different," will introduce the product to health-conscious shoppers across the Midwest and position it as the feel-good snack of summer 2026.

## 2. Target Audience

Our target is health-conscious snack buyers aged 25-44: young professionals, active parents, gym-goers, and everyday shoppers who read labels and want to snack smarter. They shop in mainstream grocery chains, care about ingredients, and will pay a modest premium for products that fit their lifestyle. Many already know the Sunbrook name from the core crisp line, which gives us a warm starting point.

## 3. The Big Idea: "Baked Different"

"Baked Different" celebrates the idea that choosing better doesn't mean settling for less. The creative captures real moments — the desk-drawer snack, the post-workout crunch, the road-trip bag — where Sunbrook Baked delivers full flavor without the fried-snack guilt. The line works across every channel: it is a product claim (baked, never fried), a lifestyle statement, and an invitation to try something new from a brand people already trust.

The hero activation is the **Baked Different Summer Sampling Tour**, a branded pop-up visiting farmers markets, running events, and grocery store parking lots across key Midwest metros through June and July, handing out single-serve bags and driving shoppers to the crisp aisle.

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

1. **Social & digital video.** Short-form video on Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts introducing the product and the "Baked Different" idea. Social is highly effective for reaching this audience, and short-form video performs especially well with younger consumers. We will run a mix of polished brand films and lighter, snackable clips.
2. **In-store sampling & shopper marketing.** The Summer Sampling Tour plus in-store demo days at major grocery partners. Sampling is a proven way to put a new snack directly into shoppers' hands, and taste is our strongest asset.
3. **Retail media.** Sponsored product placements and banner ads on grocery retailer sites and apps, reaching shoppers at the point of purchase. Retail media is one of the fastest-growing paid channels and performs well for snack brands.
4. **Creator partnerships.** A curated roster of food, fitness, and lifestyle creators featuring Sunbrook Baked in authentic everyday content. Creator recommendations carry real weight with health-conscious consumers and feel more trustworthy than traditional ads.
