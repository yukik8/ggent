# Campaign Proposal: "Baked Different" — Sunbrook Better-For-You Baked Crisps Launch

**Client:** Sunbrook Foods
**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Version:** Draft v1
**Campaign window:** April 1, 2026 – August 31, 2026
**Budget:** $480,000

---

## 1. Opening: Introducing Sunbrook Baked

Sunbrook is about to launch something genuinely exciting: a brand-new better-for-you sub-line of baked crisps. These are not just another diet snack. Sunbrook Baked crisps are oven-baked instead of fried, made with the same real-ingredient recipes that made Sunbrook Crisps a Midwest favorite, and they come in three launch flavors — Sea Salt & Cracked Pepper, Honey Sriracha, and Garden Herb. The bags feature a fresh, lighter packaging design with a matte finish and a clear "Baked, Never Fried" call-out on the front panel, so they will really stand out on shelf.

At $4.49 per bag, Sunbrook Baked sits at an accessible premium that signals quality without feeling out of reach. Health-focused snacking is one of the most talked-about trends in food right now, and consumers everywhere are looking for options that taste great and feel better to eat. This launch is Sunbrook's chance to bring its beloved flavor credentials into that conversation. Our campaign, "Baked Different," will introduce this product to health-conscious shoppers across the Midwest and make it the feel-good snack of summer 2026.

## 2. Target Audience

Our target is health-conscious snack buyers aged 25-44. This is a broad and attractive group: young professionals, active parents, gym-goers, and everyday shoppers who read labels and want to snack smarter. They shop in mainstream grocery chains, they care about ingredients, and they are willing to pay a little more for products that fit their lifestyle. Many of them already know the Sunbrook name from the core crisp line, which gives us a warm starting point.

## 3. The Big Idea: "Baked Different"

"Baked Different" celebrates the idea that choosing better doesn't mean settling for less. The creative shows real moments — the desk-drawer snack, the post-workout crunch, the road-trip bag — where Sunbrook Baked delivers full flavor with none of the fried-snack guilt. The line works hard across every channel: it's a product claim (baked, never fried), a lifestyle statement, and an invitation to try something new from a brand people already trust.

The hero activation is the **Baked Different Summer Sampling Tour**, a branded pop-up that visits farmers markets, running events, and grocery store parking lots across key Midwest metros through June and July, handing out single-serve bags and driving shoppers to the crisp aisle.

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

1. **Social & digital video.** Short-form video on Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts introducing the product and the "Baked Different" idea. Social is very effective for reaching this audience, and short videos perform especially well with younger consumers. We will run a mix of polished brand films and lighter, snackable clips.
2. **In-store sampling & shopper marketing.** The Summer Sampling Tour plus in-store demo days at major grocery partners. Sampling is a proven way to get a new snack into people's mouths, and taste is our strongest asset.
3. **Retail media.** Sponsored product placements and banner ads on grocery retailer sites and apps, capturing shoppers right at the point of purchase. Retail media is growing fast and works well for snack brands.
4. **Creator partnerships.** A roster of food, fitness, and lifestyle creators who will feature Sunbrook Baked in authentic everyday content. Influencers are hugely influential with health-conscious consumers, and their recommendations feel more trustworthy then traditional ads.
