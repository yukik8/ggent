# Client Request — Sunbrook Baked Sub-Line Launch

**To:** Meridian & Craft
**From:** Greg Hollis, VP of Marketing, Sunbrook Foods
**Date received:** 2026-02-18
**Subject:** Launch campaign — Sunbrook better-for-you baked crisps

---

Team,

Big one for us this year. After Bold Batch and the holiday multipacks, you know the Sunbrook playbook better than anybody, so I want your best swing at this.

## What's happening

We're launching a better-for-you sub-line: oven-baked crisps, same real-ingredient recipes as the core line, three launch flavors (Sea Salt & Cracked Pepper, Honey Sriracha, Garden Herb). New lighter packaging with a matte finish and a "Baked, Never Fried" call-out. Shelf price is $4.49 per bag. R&D has been at this for two years and the product genuinely tastes great — this is not a cardboard diet chip.

## What we want

A launch campaign that makes Sunbrook Baked the talked-about better-for-you snack of summer 2026. We want strong awareness with health-minded shoppers, we want people trying the product, and we want the new packaging and the baked story front and center. Bring us a big creative idea we can rally the whole company around.

## Audience

Health-conscious snack buyers aged 25-44 — label readers, gym people, parents trying to snack smarter. They shop the same Midwest grocery chains where the core line already lives.

Heads-up on the internal politics: this launch goes through our full pricing committee, and the committee is nervous. Our category buyer keeps waving private-label numbers at us — their baked snacks sit around $2.99 — and honestly nobody here has a crisp answer yet for why the shopper pays $4.49 for ours. I'll need to defend this line item more than once between now and August.

## Budget and timeline

- **Budget:** $480,000 all-in.
- **Timeline:** In market 2026-04-01 through 2026-08-31.

## Success measure

Our target: **an 8-point lift in aided awareness of Sunbrook Baked among 25-44 health-conscious snack buyers by end of campaign**, with trial and velocity building underneath it. That's the number the committee signed off on.

## Deliverables

1. Full launch proposal — strategy, creative platform, big idea
2. Channel and media plan
3. Budget breakdown against the $480K
4. Timeline and measurement approach

## Process

Because of the size, our CFO wants a competitive review this time — you plus two other agencies. Nothing personal; seven years together counts for a lot, but the deck has to stand on its own in front of the committee. Proposals due March 13, decision by mid-April.

Make it easy for me to fight for, guys.

Greg
