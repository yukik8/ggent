# Northtrail Step-Through Comfort E-Bike Proposal — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Northtrail Mobility
**Campaign window:** July 15 – October 15, 2027
**Budget:** $380,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The Best Ride of Your Life (So Far)"**

---

## Opening: A Beautiful New Way to Ride

Introducing the Northtrail step-through comfort e-bike, a gorgeous, effortless, wonderfully approachable ride built for active adults. The low step-through frame makes mounting easy, the upright riding position is comfortable for miles, and the smooth pedal assist flattens every gentle hill. With wide, stable tires, an easy-to-read display, and Northtrail's trusted battery system, it is simply a joyful machine, available at $1,999 upfront or an affordable $92/month bundle.

Active adults are rediscovering cycling everywhere, and this bike is the perfect invitation. Our campaign will bring its charm, comfort, and freedom to riders across flat-terrain suburbs and turn sunny weekends into selling season.

---

## Target Audience

Active adults aged 55-70 living in flat-terrain suburbs. They are energetic, social, and young at heart, with time to ride and disposable income to spend. Many rode bikes for years and would love an easier way back into the saddle. They respond to warm, authentic stories and trusted local voices.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The Best Ride of Your Life (So Far)"** celebrates riders who are not done having adventures. Real riders, real neighborhoods, real joy — morning loops with friends, rides to the farmers market, sunset spins with a spouse. The creative is warm, bright, and full of life, showing that this bike is not about slowing down, it is about getting going again.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social & Video
Facebook and YouTube are wonderful channels for this audience, who spend real time on both. We will run beautiful lifestyle video and carousel ads showing the bike in everyday suburban life, with warm testimonial cuts.

### 2. Local Print, Radio & Community Sponsorships
Community newspapers, local radio, and sponsorships of farmers markets, senior centers, and community rides put the brand in trusted local spaces where this audience already spends time.

### 3. Dealer Test-Ride Weekends
Partner dealers host relaxed weekend test-ride events with coffee, easy parking, and zero pressure. Nothing convinces like a comfortable first ride.

### 4. Regional Digital Display & Search
Display and paid search capture shoppers researching comfort bikes, e-bikes for seniors, and step-through models, guiding them to a friendly landing page and dealer locator.
