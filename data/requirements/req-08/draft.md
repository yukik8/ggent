# Campaign Proposal: "Swap & Go" — Northtrail Battery-Swap Membership Push

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Northtrail Mobility
**Product:** Battery-swap subscription (standalone membership, $39/month)
**Budget:** $350,000
**Flight:** February 1 – May 31, 2026

---

## 1. Opening

Introducing the Northtrail battery-swap membership as a standalone product for the first time. For $39 a month, riders get unlimited access to Northtrail's growing network of swap stations, where a depleted battery can be exchanged for a fully charged one in under sixty seconds. No home charging, no waiting, no range anxiety. The membership works with every Northtrail e-bike sold since 2023, and the swap stations themselves are sleek, well-lit, and easy to find through the Northtrail app.

This is a genuinely exciting product. E-bikes are more popular than ever, cities are investing in bike infrastructure, and riders increasingly expect the kind of convenience that subscription services provide. The battery-swap membership takes the single most annoying part of e-bike ownership — charging — and makes it disappear. Our campaign will introduce this convenience to riders everywhere and position Northtrail as the most rider-friendly brand in micromobility.

## 2. Target Audience

We see two audiences for this push:

- **Existing Northtrail e-bike owners** who bought a bike outright and currently charge at home. They already love the product, and the membership is a natural upgrade for them.
- **Price-sensitive commuters aged 25-40** who are curious about e-bikes but haven't committed yet. Many young commuters find e-bikes expensive, and a low monthly membership feels much more approachable than a big purchase. Young professionals in general respond well to subscription offers, so we expect broad appeal across urban and suburban commuters alike.

## 3. The Big Idea: "Swap & Go"

The creative platform is **"Swap & Go — power that never runs out."** Every asset dramatizes the sixty-second swap: a rider pulls up to a station, trades batteries, and rides off before the coffee in their cup holder goes cold. The visual language is fast, clean, and a little playful — the swap becomes a small daily ritual, like grabbing a coffee, that makes the whole city feel closer.

We will build the campaign around real riders and real stations, not studio shots. Authenticity matters to this audience, and social content that feels native performs much better than polished ads.

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| **Short-form video (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)** | Hero channel. Social is very effective with younger commuters, and short video is where they spend their time. We'll run swap-ritual spots in 15s and 30s cuts. |
| **Creator partnerships** | Local cycling and commuter-lifestyle creators document a month of swap-only riding. Creators drive strong engagement with this audience. |
| **Out-of-home near bike infrastructure** | Posters and station wraps along protected bike lanes and near swap stations, catching riders at the exact moment of relevance. |
| **Owned CRM / email** | Direct offer to the existing owner base: first month free when they activate before March 31. |
| **Campaign landing page & content** | A simple page with a station map, membership math, and rider testimonials. |

## 7. KPIs

- Membership sign-ups during the flight (primary)
- Conversion rate from existing owner base
- Landing-page visits and cost per visit
- Video views and completion rates
- Engagement rate on creator content
- Awareness lift of the swap membership among commuters 25-40 (survey)

## 8. Why This Will Work

The product sells itself once people see the swap happen — it's fast, it's satisfying, and it solves a real annoyance. Subscriptions are an increasingly popular way to pay for mobility, and this audience is very comfortable with monthly memberships across every category of their lives. By leading with the sixty-second swap moment and showing it everywhere our riders already look, we make the membership feel like an obvious upgrade for owners and an easy first step for the e-bike curious.

Northtrail has built something the market is clearly moving toward. "Swap & Go" gives it the launch energy it deserves.
