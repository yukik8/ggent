# Client Request — Sunbrook Crisps "Bold Batch" Launch

**To:** Meridian & Craft
**From:** Greg Hollis, VP of Marketing, Sunbrook Foods
**Date received:** 2025-01-24
**Subject:** Launch campaign for our new Bold Batch spicy line — spring 2025

---

Team,

Good to see everyone at the January check-in. As promised, here's the formal ask for the big spring project. This one matters a lot to us internally, so I want to get you started early.

## Where we are

You know the story: Sunbrook Crisps has been a Midwest grocery staple for decades, and the core line is steady. This spring we're launching **Bold Batch**, our first real swing at the spicy snacking trend — three heat levels (Ember, Blaze, Inferno), real chili blends, and a matte-black packaging system our design team is very proud of. It will retail at $3.99 per bag, a $0.50 premium over the core line.

Honestly, the product is the best thing our kitchen has done in years, and leadership wants it to be LOUD. Spicy is having a moment and we don't want to watch it from the sidelines.

## What we want

An awareness-driving launch campaign that gets Bold Batch seen absolutely everywhere our shoppers are — social, in-store, wherever. We want buzz, we want the bags in people's hands, and we want the launch to feel like an event, not just a new SKU quietly appearing on shelf three.

A couple of things rattling around in the back of my head, for what they're worth: our repeat purchase numbers on the core line could frankly be better, and the private-label stuff keeps creeping onto more of our shelf every reset. But the main thing right now is getting Bold Batch in front of as many young snackers as possible.

## Target

Snack buyers aged 18-34 in Midwest grocery drive markets. The younger crowd that's always trying new flavors. Secondary: our existing loyalists who might trade up.

## Budget & timeline

- **Budget:** $420,000 all-in (media, production, activation, measurement)
- **Campaign window:** 2025-03-10 to 2025-06-30 — we need to be live for the spring resets and run through early summer

## Success

The number leadership has landed on: **40% aided awareness of Bold Batch among 18-34 snack buyers in our core Midwest markets by end of June**, plus healthy launch velocity at our priority retailers.

## Deliverables

- Full campaign strategy and creative platform
- Channel/media plan with budget allocation
- Launch timeline phased against the window above
- Measurement approach

## Process

Heads up — because of the spend level, procurement is making this a competitive review. You're up against two other shops. I'll be straight with you: you're the incumbent and I want you to win it, but bring your A-game, because the other decks I've seen floating around are slick.

Proposals due three weeks from today. Call me anytime.

Greg
