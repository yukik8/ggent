# Velura Fragrance-Free Body-Care Line Extension — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Velura Skin
**Campaign window:** March 1 – June 30, 2028
**Budget:** $430,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Below the Chin" — Velura Body Care, Fragrance-Free from Head to Toe**

---

## Opening

Introducing Velura Body Care, a three-SKU fragrance-free line extension ($26-$34): the Body Barrier Lotion, the Gentle Body Wash, and the Repair Balm for rough patches. Each product carries the same barrier-first, fragrance-free formulation philosophy as the face line, extended to the 90% of skin the brand does not currently touch. The line launches simultaneously on velura.com and at specialty retail partners.

---

## Target Audience

| Segment | Description |
|---|---|
| Primary | Sensitive-skin shoppers aged 20-40 extending face routines to body |
| Secondary | New-to-brand sensitive-skin shoppers who start with body care |

These shoppers already read labels for their face products and are frustrated that body care rarely meets the same standard. Fragrance-free is a real unmet need in the body aisle.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Below the Chin"** points at the double standard: you check every ingredient on your face cream, then use whatever on the rest. The creative pairs face-routine care rituals with their new body-care equivalents, making the extension feel obvious.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Routine-extension content and ingredient-transparency explainers on Reels and TikTok |
| Creator partnerships | Sensitive-skin and eczema-adjacent creators trial the line over several weeks |
| Specialty retail launch activation | Launch displays, testers, and staff training at retail partners |
| CRM & email | Existing face-line customers get first access and bundle offers |

Social introduces the double-standard idea, creators validate it with real skin, retail makes the line discoverable in-aisle, and CRM converts the warmest audience first.

## Why This Will Work

The face line earned trust; the body line cashes it in. The unmet need is real, the products meet the brand standard, and the plan sequences warm audiences before cold ones. "Below the Chin" gives Velura a bigger daily footprint in the customer's routine.
