## Campaign Title

**"Rent the Big Stuff"**

---

## Opening

Hartline's tool-rental counter is one of the best-kept secrets in the store. Every Hartline location carries a fleet of professional-grade equipment — tile saws, floor sanders, post-hole diggers, pressure washers, carpet cleaners — available from $19 a day, and most shoppers walk past it without knowing it exists.

"Rent the Big Stuff" puts a spotlight on the rental counter and delivers one clear message to every occasional DIYer in the trade area: you don't have to buy a $600 tile saw to redo one bathroom floor. Rent it, finish the project, hand it back.

---

## Target Audience

Occasional DIYers who take on a real project once or twice a year and are unwilling to buy specialty tools they will use once. They are practical, budget-aware, and often surprised to learn rental is an option at all — which makes awareness the primary job of this campaign.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Rent the Big Stuff"** celebrates having the right tool for one weekend. The creative shows real projects transformed by serious equipment — the floor sander that saved the hardwood, the pressure washer that resurrected the deck — always ending on the price: from $19 a day.

---

## Channel Plan

### 1. Paid Social & Video
Satisfying before-and-after videos across Reels, TikTok, and YouTube — sanded floors, gleaming decks, clean tile lines — each one revealing that the hero tool was rented. Transformation content performs consistently well in the home category and carries the campaign's awareness load.

### 2. Content Production
A compact set of short films and photography covering the rental fleet's greatest hits, plus quick explainers on how renting works: reserve, pick up, return.

### 3. Local Out-of-Home
Playful billboards near stores — "That tile saw? Rented." / "Own the weekend, not the sander." — build broad local awareness that the rental counter exists.

### 4. In-Store Signage & Rental Counter Displays
Wayfinding from the front door to the rental counter, project-pairing signage in relevant aisles ("Redoing floors? Rent the sander →"), and a refreshed counter presentation.

### 5. Email & CRM
Rental spotlights to the customer list, timed to seasonal projects — deck season, garden season, spring refresh — with simple reservation links.

## Why Hartline Wins

The rental counter is a strong asset waiting for a spotlight. The equipment is professional-grade, the price point is friendly, and the story is simple to tell: rent the big stuff, do the big project, give it back. With transformation content, local out-of-home, and in-store wayfinding working together, this campaign introduces Hartline's rental program to the occasional DIYers who have been walking right past it — and turns a back-of-store counter into a reason to choose Hartline for the whole project.
