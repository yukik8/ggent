## Campaign Title

**"Row Together" — the Kinetiq Corporate Wellness Program**

## Opening

Introducing Row Together, Kinetiq's entry into corporate wellness: a $30/month per-employee subsidized membership that puts connected rowing into the benefits stack of remote-heavy companies. Workplace wellness is one of the hottest categories in HR right now, remote-work wellness stipends are exploding, and companies are competing hard on benefits. Row Together rides all three waves with a turnkey program: subsidized memberships, team leaderboards, company challenges, and quarterly wellness reporting for HR.

## The Tactics

### 1. LinkedIn Thought-Leadership Flight
Sponsored posts and articles on the future of remote wellness, positioning Kinetiq executives as voices in the benefits conversation.

### 2. HR-Benefits Platform Co-Marketing
Joint webinars, marketplace listings, and co-branded content with Kinetiq's HR-benefits platform partner, putting Row Together directly in front of benefits buyers at enrollment-planning time.

### 3. Company Challenge Toolkit
A gamified team-challenge layer — department leaderboards, milestone badges, a "Row the Distance" company goal — that makes the program visible inside each workplace.

### 4. Pilot Demo Kits
Loaner rowers and a 60-day pilot package shipped to interested companies so HR teams can test real engagement before committing.

### 5. Webinar & Virtual Event Series
Monthly "State of Remote Wellness" webinars with HR guests, feeding the pipeline between flights.

### 6. Case-Study Content Engine
Every pilot becomes a story: engagement stats, employee testimonials, and HR quotes packaged for sales follow-up.

## Target Audience

HR and benefits leads at 500+ employee remote-heavy companies, who are evaluating wellness benefits for the next enrollment cycle and need engagement data to justify spend. Secondary: wellness committee members and office managers who champion programs internally.

## Media Plan Summary

LinkedIn carries the awareness flight to benefits titles. The platform partnership provides the distribution moment, webinars nurture, and pilot kits convert. Case studies close.
