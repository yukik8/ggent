# Ledgerline Manufacturing Vertical Expansion Program — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Ledgerline
**Campaign window:** March 1 – August 31, 2028
**Budget:** $490,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Built for the Floor and the Books" — Ledgerline for Manufacturing**

---

## Opening

Introducing Ledgerline for Manufacturing, a vertical go-to-market program bringing the AP Automation Suite to 100-500 employee manufacturers at a $30,000 average annual contract value. The manufacturing tier includes three-way PO matching, inventory-system integrations, and multi-entity support — capabilities manufacturers ask about most. Manufacturing finance teams process high invoice volumes across many vendors, which makes them a strong fit for automation.

| Program Facts | Detail |
|---|---|
| Offer | AP Automation Suite, manufacturing tier |
| ACV | $30,000 average |
| Target segment | 100-500 employee US manufacturers |
| Key capabilities | 3-way PO matching, inventory integrations, multi-entity |

---

## Target Audience

Finance directors at 100-500 employee manufacturers. They manage complex vendor networks and high invoice volumes, evaluate software carefully, and rely on peer input when building shortlists.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Built for the Floor and the Books"** positions Ledgerline as the AP platform that understands manufacturing, not a generic finance tool with a manufacturing label. Creative pairs plant-floor imagery with finance outcomes.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Peer review site program | Build presence in manufacturing-relevant review categories |
| LinkedIn ABM media | Targeted creative to finance directors at named manufacturers |
| Manufacturing trade media & events | Sponsored content and booth presence at two industry events |
| Case study production | Manufacturing customer stories with concrete outcomes |
| Webinars with manufacturing customers | Peer-led sessions on AP transformation |
| Landing pages & creative | Vertical landing experience with demo booking |

Review sites establish credibility, ABM creates demand at named accounts, trade media builds category presence, and case studies plus webinars carry proof through the funnel.

## Why This Will Work

Manufacturing is a natural vertical for AP automation, and this program gives Ledgerline a structured, credible entry: proof assets first, demand generation second, conversion support third. Each channel has a clear role, and the sequencing builds a durable manufacturing presence rather than a one-off campaign.
