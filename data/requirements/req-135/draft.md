# Stackwise High-Yield Savings Account Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Stackwise
**Campaign window:** April 1 – August 31, 2028
**Budget:** $420,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Let It Grow"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Stackwise High-Yield Savings Account: a 4.1% APY account, free with the $8/month premium tier. The account features automatic round-ups, instant transfers from the main Stackwise dashboard, and no minimum balance. It is the most significant product expansion in the company's history, turning Stackwise from a budgeting tool into a place where money actually lives.

The rate is competitive with the leading online banks, and existing users are the natural first market: many hold meaningful balances in low-interest checking connected to the app already. Our campaign introduces the account, drives openings, and grows deposits through the summer.

## Target Audience

Existing Stackwise users holding balances in low-interest checking accounts. They already trust the app with their financial picture, and moving idle money into the new account is a small step with an obvious payoff.

## The Big Idea

**"Let It Grow"** shows what idle money could be doing. Simple split-screen creative: the same $1,000 sitting still in checking versus quietly growing in the new account, tracked over months inside the Stackwise interface users already know.

## Channel Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| In-app & lifecycle email | Announce the account to the existing base and prompt idle-balance users directly |
| Short-form video (TikTok, Reels) | Explainers and split-screen creative for users and lookalikes |
| Creator explainers | Finance creators walk through opening the account and their first month of growth |
| Paid social retargeting | Convert announcement viewers and site visitors who did not open an account |

## KPIs

- Account openings among existing users in the first 60 days
- Average opening deposit and 90-day balance growth
- Conversion rate from idle-balance prompt to funded account
- Premium-tier upgrades attributed to the account

## Why This Will Work

The product is genuinely competitive, the audience already trusts the app, and the value is self-evident: the same money, earning more. A clean announcement to the existing base plus creator-led explainers for new audiences gives Stackwise a straightforward path to deposits.
