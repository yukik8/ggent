# Kinetiq Resolution-Season Campaign Proposal — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Kinetiq Home
**Campaign window:** November 15, 2026 – January 31, 2027
**Budget:** $580,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"This Year, Actually" — the Kinetiq Resolution Season**

---

## Opening: The Most Wonderful (Fitness) Time of the Year

Introducing "This Year, Actually," Kinetiq's biggest, boldest seasonal campaign yet, built for the electric fresh-start energy of resolution season. The Kinetiq rower is a gorgeous machine — a whisper-quiet magnetic flywheel, a stunning rotating touchscreen, daily live classes with coaches people genuinely love, and a slim upright-storage design that disappears into a corner when the workout is done. At $1,795 with a $200 holiday discount running through the gifting window, it has never been easier to say yes.

And the moment could not be better. Every January, millions of people resolve to get fit, and home fitness is one of the most exciting and fastest-moving spaces in consumer wellness. The holidays put big-ticket gifts on the table, January puts motivation in the air, and Kinetiq sits perfectly at the intersection of both. This campaign captures that seasonal wave from the first gift-guide scroll in November to the last resolution push in January.

---

## Target Audience

New Year fitness resolvers aged 25-45 — optimistic, busy people who genuinely want this year to be different. They are planning their resolutions as early as November, they respond to warmth and encouragement over drill-sergeant messaging, and they are scrolling social feeds full of gift guides and new-year content all season long. Secondary: holiday gift buyers shopping for a partner or parent, and lapsed shoppers who considered Kinetiq earlier in the year but did not buy. Our 30-day in-home trial remains available all season, which gives hesitant resolvers a lovely low-pressure way in.

---

## The Big Idea

**"This Year, Actually."** Everyone has said "this year is the year" before. Our campaign celebrates the version of you that means it this time — not with intimidating transformation montages, but with warm, funny, deeply relatable scenes of real people fitting real workouts into real Januaries. The line works twice: as a holiday gift message ("give them the year they keep talking about") and as a January rallying cry ("this year, actually"). It is optimistic, a little knowing, and completely ownable.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Short-form video is incredibly effective at this time of year, when feeds are full of gift inspiration and resolution content. We will run our hero films and cutdowns across Reels, TikTok, and Shorts through the entire window, shifting creative from gifting to resolution messaging after the holidays.

### 2. Creator Partnerships
We will partner with lifestyle and fitness creators to share their own "this year, actually" stories — the resolutions they finally kept and how the rower fit into their mornings. Creator content feels personal and trustworthy, which matters enormously for a considered purchase like this.

### 3. Lifecycle Email, SMS & Retargeting
Kinetiq's email list, SMS subscribers, and site visitors from earlier in the year are warm audiences we should absolutely re-engage for the season. We will run gift-guide sends, discount-deadline reminders, and a January re-engagement flight for shoppers who browsed but did not buy, plus retargeting across the funnel.
