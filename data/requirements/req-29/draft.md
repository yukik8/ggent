## Campaign Title

**"Second Wind" — the Northtrail Trade-In & Upgrade Launch**

---

## Opening: The Easiest Upgrade in Cycling

Introducing the Northtrail Trade-In & Upgrade Program, the most exciting thing to happen to e-bike ownership since the battery swap. For the first time, Northtrail riders can bring in any e-bike — yes, even a competitor's — and receive up to $600 in instant trade-in credit toward a brand-new Northtrail commuter model. The program is beautifully simple: ride in on your old bike, get a fast in-store appraisal, and ride out on a new one the same day. Trade-in credit stacks with Northtrail's battery-swap subscription, so upgraders also unlock the freshest battery experience in the category from day one.

The program removes every bit of friction from upgrading. No listing your old bike online, no haggling with strangers, no wondering what a three-year-old battery is even worth. Northtrail handles all of it, refurbishes what can be refurbished, and responsibly recycles the rest — which gives the whole program a wonderful sustainability story on top of the value story. E-bikes have come a long way in the last few years, and riders on older models are missing out on better range, smoother motors, and smarter connectivity. "Second Wind" is our chance to bring them back into the excitement.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is owners of e-bikes that are three or more years old, in Northtrail's core mid-size city markets. Many of these riders love e-biking but have watched their battery range slowly shrink and their tech fall behind. A lot of them are curious about newer models but assume upgrading is expensive and complicated. They are practical, value-conscious people who respond to a clear deal, and many of them are already familiar with the Northtrail brand from seeing it around their cities.

Secondary audiences include lapsed riders whose old e-bike is gathering dust in the garage, and current Northtrail owners who bought early and are ready for their next model.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Second Wind"** turns the aging e-bike from a quiet embarrassment into an asset. The creative celebrates the moment a rider realizes their old bike is worth real money — and their next bike is closer than they thought. Our hero visual language is the side-by-side: the faded, duct-taped 2023 commuter next to the gleaming new Northtrail, with the line "Your old ride just earned you $600 toward this one." Every channel drives to one action: book a trade-in appraisal.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Email & CRM to the Owner Base
Northtrail's owner list is a goldmine for this campaign, and email is a very effective way to reach existing riders. We will build a lifecycle email series that identifies owners of older models, shows them their estimated trade-in value, and invites them to book an appraisal. Lapsed riders get their own reactivation flow.

### 2. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Short-form video is where riders discover and share bike content, and it performs really well for before-and-after storytelling. We will run Reels and TikTok flights featuring real upgraders, appraisal-day moments, and satisfying "old bike in, new bike out" transitions. Social is great for making the program feel like a movement, not just an offer.

### 3. YouTube & Online Video
E-bike owners are big researchers, and many of them watch long-form review content before making decisions. We will place pre-roll and partner with e-bike review channels to walk through the trade-in process honestly, including what different bikes appraise for. This builds the trust the program needs.

### 4. In-Store Trade-In Weekends
Nothing makes the program real like seeing appraisals happen live. We will run "Second Wind Weekends" at Northtrail retail locations — on-the-spot appraisals, test rides on the new lineup, and same-day upgrade incentives. These events create urgency and give our social channels a steady stream of authentic content.
