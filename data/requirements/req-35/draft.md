## Campaign Title

**"Countdown to Close" — the Ledgerline Webinar Series Relaunch**

---

## Opening: A Fresh, Exciting New Era for Ledgerline Webinars

Introducing the all-new Ledgerline webinar series — a completely reimagined, beautifully produced monthly program that shows off everything the Ledgerline platform can do. The relaunched series features a gorgeous new visual identity, a snappier 35-minute live format, real in-product walkthroughs instead of slideware, guest appearances from finance leaders, and a brand-new on-demand library so nobody ever has to miss an episode. Each session spotlights a different corner of the platform: invoice capture, approval workflows, payment scheduling, audit trails, and the reporting suite.

Registration stays completely free, which makes the series an incredibly welcoming front door to the Ledgerline world. With annual contracts around $24,000, every attendee who eventually becomes a customer is genuinely valuable, and a vibrant, energetic webinar program is one of the most engaging ways to bring the platform to life. Webinars are hugely popular in the B2B software space right now, and this relaunch is Ledgerline's chance to turn its series into the show that finance people actually look forward to.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is mid-market finance teams that are actively evaluating AP automation. These are controllers, AP managers, and finance directors at growing companies who are busy, practical, and curious about better ways to work. Many finance professionals are looking into automation these days, and lots of them prefer to learn from live sessions and peer conversations rather than sales calls. They spend time on professional networks and in their inboxes, so those channels will be central to our plan.

Secondary audiences include existing Ledgerline customers who might expand their usage, and CFOs who sign off on the final decision.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Countdown to Close"** turns the webinar series into a month-end companion. Every episode is framed around the moment finance teams feel most — the days counting down to close — and shows, live and unscripted, how one specific close headache disappears inside Ledgerline. Episodes carry countdown-themed titles ("T-minus 5 Days: Taming the Approval Pile," "T-minus 3: Where Did That Invoice Go?"), and each one ends with a guest finance leader sharing how their own close got shorter. The countdown framing is instantly relatable, endlessly repeatable, and gives the whole series a fun, ownable identity that generic product demos simply don't have.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. LinkedIn — Paid & Organic
LinkedIn is where finance professionals live, and it's a fantastic environment for B2B content. We'll run paid registration campaigns targeted at finance titles at mid-market companies, supported by an always-on organic drumbeat of episode teasers, speaker announcements, and clipped highlights from past sessions. Thought-leader style posts from Ledgerline's own team will make the series feel human and credible.

### 2. Email Marketing & Nurture
Ledgerline's existing contact database is a goldmine for this relaunch. We'll build a warm, personality-filled email program: episode invitations, countdown-day reminder sequences, post-webinar replay links, and a nurture track that gently walks engaged attendees toward a demo conversation. Email is a dependable performer for webinar registrations and it keeps the series top of mind between episodes.

### 3. Finance Newsletter & Podcast Sponsorships
Finance-ops newsletters and podcasts have wonderfully loyal audiences, and sponsorship placements there put "Countdown to Close" directly in front of people who already care about better close processes. We'll book a steady rotation of sponsorships timed to each episode's registration window, with copy written in the series' countdown voice.

### 4. Webinar Production & On-Demand Library
The show itself is a channel. We'll invest in polished production — proper lighting, clean graphics, tight run-of-show — plus a searchable on-demand library on the Ledgerline site so every episode keeps working long after the live date. Great production values signal a great product, and the library becomes an evergreen registration driver.

### 5. Retargeting
Visitors who check out an episode page but don't register, and registrants who don't attend, will see friendly retargeting ads nudging them to the replay or the next episode. It's an efficient way to squeeze more value out of every click the bigger channels generate.
