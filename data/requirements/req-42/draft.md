## Campaign Title

**"The Good Start Club" — the Pawstead Puppy Starter Program**

---

## Opening: Introducing the Pawstead Puppy Starter Kit

The Pawstead Puppy Starter Kit is a complete first-two-weeks feeding system for new puppy owners, priced at $49 and designed to convert into the full $120/month fresh meal plan. The kit packages Pawstead's vet-formulated refrigerated recipes into puppy-sized portions and pairs them with practical onboarding tools, so a brand-new owner has everything they need from day one.

### What's in the kit

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| Fresh meal packs | 14 days of vet-formulated refrigerated puppy portions |
| Portion & transition guide | Week-by-week feeding schedule, kibble-to-fresh transition plan |
| Fridge organizer sleeve | Slim stackable sleeve so the packs take minimal fridge space |
| Milestone card set | 90-day puppy milestone cards (first walk, first vet visit, etc.) |
| Plan voucher | First month of the $120/month plan at starter pricing |

The fresh pet food category is growing quickly and more owners than ever are open to refrigerated meals for their dogs. Puppies are the perfect entry point for the brand, because owners are setting habits that can last the dog's whole life. With Pawstead now appearing in grocery fridge aisles as well as online, the starter kit gives us a single, easy-to-explain product to put at the center of a full acquisition program.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is new puppy owners in their first 90 days of ownership. This is an emotional, high-spend period where owners are actively researching food, training, and vet care, and they are very receptive to guidance. Many new owners feel overwhelmed in the first weeks and appreciate brands that make things simpler. They spend a lot of time watching pet content on social media and often ask for recommendations in online communities.

| Segment | Description | Role in plan |
|---|---|---|
| First-time puppy owners | Adopted or purchased in last 90 days, researching everything | Primary conversion target |
| Experienced owners with a new puppy | Already have food habits, open to upgrading | Secondary |
| Gifters | Friends/family buying for a new puppy household | Tertiary, seasonal (holidays) |

---

## The Big Idea

**"The Good Start Club"** turns the starter kit into a membership, not a purchase. Buying the $49 kit enrolls the owner and their puppy in a 90-day guided program: week-by-week feeding and transition content, puppy milestone badges to share on social, and vet Q&A sessions. The club framing gives the campaign warmth and community, and it gives us a natural story arc — from first bowl to full plan — that mirrors the owner's actual first three months. Every channel drives to one action: join the club with the $49 kit.

## Channel & Media Plan

| # | Channel | Role |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Paid social short-form video | Awareness & kit sales |
| 2 | Vet & real-pet creator partnerships | Trust & education |
| 3 | Grocery retail media & in-fridge POS | Point-of-purchase visibility |
| 4 | Lifecycle email & SMS onboarding | Kit-to-plan conversion |
| 5 | Shelter & adoption-day partnerships | Reaching owners at day zero |

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
Short-form video is extremely effective for pet content, and puppy content in particular performs very well. We will run a continuous flight of Reels, TikTok, and Shorts featuring real puppies experiencing their first Pawstead bowl, milestone moments from club members, and simple explainers of the kit.

### 2. Vet & Real-Pet Creator Partnerships
New owners trust vets and real owners far more than brand ads. We will build a roster of vet and vet-tech creators plus accounts starring real rescue puppies to explain the formulation on camera and document their own 90-day club journeys. Creator content will also feed our paid flight.

### 3. Grocery Retail Media & In-Fridge POS
With Pawstead expanding into grocery fridge aisles, we will support the launch with sponsored placements on grocery retail media networks and in-fridge shelf materials that flag the starter kit, so shoppers who saw the social content recognize the brand at the fridge.

### 4. Lifecycle Email & SMS Onboarding
Email and SMS are great tools for keeping new members engaged. Every kit purchase triggers a 90-day onboarding flow: transition tips, portion reminders, milestone prompts, and a well-timed offer to activate the $120/month plan before the kit runs out.

### 5. Shelter & Adoption-Day Partnerships
We will place co-branded Good Start Club vouchers in adoption packets at partner shelters and run adoption-day booths in priority metros, reaching owners on literally their first day of ownership.
