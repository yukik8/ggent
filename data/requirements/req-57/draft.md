## Campaign Title

**"The 30-Day Proof" — the Row One In-Home Trial Launch**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Kinetiq Row One, the most complete connected rower Kinetiq has ever built. Row One pairs a smooth magnetic-resistance flywheel with a 22-inch rotating touchscreen, daily live classes, and a 500-title on-demand library, all in a frame that stores upright in about two square feet. It is a serious machine at a fair price, and it launches with the strongest offer in the category: a 30-day in-home trial with free pickup on returns.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Hardware price | $1,795, or $63/month financing |
| Membership | $39/month, unlimited household profiles |
| Trial offer | 30-day in-home trial, free return pickup |
| Storage | Upright, approx. 2 sq ft footprint |
| Content | Daily live classes + 500-title on-demand library |

The launch plan below is built around one job: get Row One into homes on trial, then let the product convert. Home fitness is a large and recovering market, rowing is a growing modality, and trial offers are known to convert well, so the timing for a trial-led launch is good.

---

## Target Audience

Primary: home-fitness intenders aged 28-45 who want to work out at home but hesitate because they worry the machine will go unused. Many have quit a gym before, they research carefully, and they respond to honest reviews more than hype. Secondary: former connected-fitness owners considering a second try, and gift purchasers in the holiday window.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The 30-Day Proof."** We do not ask this audience to believe our marketing; we ask them to test it in their own living room. Every asset in the campaign makes the same offer: row for 30 days where you actually live, and if it is not working, we pick it up for free. Creative focuses on real weekday routines — the 6 a.m. row before the kids wake up, the lunch-break session in the basement — rather than transformation stories.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Primary reach and trial-signup driver on Reels, TikTok, and Shorts |
| Creator partnerships | Review-style, "one month in" content from everyday-credible creators |
| Lifecycle email & SMS | Trial nurture from signup through day 30, plus holiday and financing reminders |
| Landing experience | Dedicated trial page with clear terms, financing math, and FAQ |

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Short-form video is a very effective format for fitness content, and this audience spends a lot of time there. We will run a steady flight across Reels, TikTok, and Shorts centered on the trial offer, with cutdowns of creator reviews as supporting assets.

### 2. Creator Partnerships
We will contract creators who feel like our buyer — busy parents, desk workers, ex-gym members — to document their first 30 days on Row One honestly. Review-style content suits a considered purchase like this better than polished ads, and audiences trust it more.

### 3. Lifecycle Email & SMS
Trial signups enter a structured nurture: setup help, first-week class picks, and check-ins through day 30, then conversion messaging around the $63/month financing option. Holiday-window sends support gifting.

### 4. Landing Experience
A single trial-focused landing page carries the offer terms, the financing calculator, and the returns FAQ, keeping the path from ad to trial request as short as possible.
