## Campaign Title

**"The 9.90 Club" — Blue Fork Goes Back to School**

---

## Opening: Campus Is the Culture Engine

Blue Fork Kitchen is launching a student meal deal — a full bowl, a side, and a drink for **$9.90** with student verification in the app — across the 22 locations that sit next to college campuses. And honestly, the timing is perfect, because campus food culture is exploding online right now. "What I eat in a day as a college student" is a top-performing format, dining-hall rating videos pull millions of views, GRWM-before-class content is everywhere, and food that fits a student budget without looking like instant ramen is basically its own genre.

Students set trends for everyone else. What gets adopted on campus in September is what the entire 18-24 cohort is doing by spring. A $9.90 bowl deal is exactly the kind of thing that becomes a running bit — the order that gets screenshotted, memed, and put in the group chat. This campaign gets Blue Fork into that conversation with every tool campus culture gives us: creators, ambassadors, stunts, points, and a verification flow that makes the deal feel like a membership card.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The 9.90 Club."** Being a student finally pays. Verify once in the app and you're in the club — the full meal for $9.90, every day, no coupon hunting. We treat the club like an actual club: a membership moment when verification clears, club-only drops during midterms and finals, and a running series where we "induct" campus micro-celebrities — the beloved TA, the intramural legend, the guy who brings his cat to the quad.

The stack:

- **Campus ambassador crew** — paid student ambassadors at priority campuses seeding the club in dorms, group chats, and club fairs.
- **Creator series** — "9.90 Club rush week" content with campus food creators rating the build combos.
- **Midterm fuel drops** — surprise free-side codes pushed to club members during exam weeks.
- **Study-night takeovers** — late-afternoon bowl deals timed to library peak hours, promoted by ambassadors.
- **Meme-ready assets** — a sticker pack, a club card in the app, and formats built to be remixed.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — TikTok & Instagram Reels
Geo-targeted flights around the 22 campus locations with creator-style content: combo builds under $10, "rating every bowl as a broke student" formats, and club induction videos. Constantly refreshed so we ride whatever sound is working that week.

### 2. Campus Ambassador & Creator Program
Ambassadors at priority campuses plus a roster of campus food creators. This is where the club actually spreads — peer-to-peer, chat-to-chat. Ambassadors get content quotas, event kits, and their own codes to track.

### 3. On-Campus Sampling & Events
Club-fair tables, tailgate-adjacent sampling, and study-week pop-ups with mini bowls and QR codes straight into the verification flow. Physical presence at the moments campus energy peaks.

### 4. App, CRM & Verification Flow
The student verification moment is the conversion event, so the app experience gets its own push: streamlined sign-up, the club card, points on every $9.90 meal, and exam-week bonus drops to keep members active through December.

---

## Target Audience

College students at the 22 campus-adjacent locations — undergrads and grad students living on a fixed budget, eating off-campus several times a week, and deciding where to go based on price, speed, and what their feed and friends are saying. They're app-native, deal-savvy, and loyal to places that respect their budget without making them feel broke. Reach them where they actually are: their phones, their group chats, and the quad.
