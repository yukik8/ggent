# Cascade Valley Dark-Sky Stargazing Winter Weekends — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Cascade Valley Tourism Board
**Campaign window:** November 1, 2027 – February 15, 2028
**Budget:** $280,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"After Dark in the Valley"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Cascade Valley Dark-Sky Stargazing Winter Weekends, a new $59 stargazing package add-on to lodging that turns the valley's certified dark-sky viewpoints into a bookable winter product. The package includes a guided constellation walk, access to heated observation decks with telescope stations, a hot-drinks fire pit session, and a downloadable night-photography guide. Astro-tourism is growing in popularity, and Cascade Valley has some of the darkest skies within drive distance of several major metros. This is a premium, photogenic experience that lodging partners can attach to any winter stay.

---

## Product Details

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| Price | $59 per person, added to any participating lodging booking |
| Includes | Guided constellation walk, heated deck access, telescope stations, fire pit session |
| Season | November through mid-February, clear-sky reschedule guarantee |
| Partners | 14 lodging properties, 3 certified dark-sky viewpoints |

---

## Target Audience

Couples under 40 seeking novel off-season drive-market escapes. They enjoy unique experiences, they like sharing trips on social media, and winter is a time when many of them are looking for something different to do. The stargazing package gives them a memorable reason to visit in the off-season.

---

## The Big Idea

**"After Dark in the Valley"** positions winter nights as the valley's best-kept attraction. Creative centers on the contrast between city light pollution and the valley's pristine night sky, with the tagline "You've never seen night like this."

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video (Reels, TikTok) | Hero night-sky videos drive inspiration and clicks to the booking page |
| Creator partnerships | Regional travel and photography creators experience and share the package |
| Regional paid search & display | Capture winter getaway and stargazing searches |
| Lodging-partner co-op, booking page & email | Convert interest into packaged bookings with partner properties |

Social media works very well for travel inspiration, and night-sky content tends to perform strongly. Creators will make the experience feel authentic, and search will capture people already planning winter trips.
