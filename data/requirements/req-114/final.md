## Campaign Title

**"After Dark in the Valley"**

---

## Opening: The Problem We Are Actually Solving

Cascade Valley's problem is empty winter beds, not a shortage of scenery. Shoulder-season (Apr-May, Sep-Oct) occupancy for regional destinations averages 46%, versus 78% peak-season (ref-05), and the valley's November-to-February trough sits at the same off-peak depth — rooms, restaurants, and guides already built and staffed, with no one in them. Every recovered visitor is high-value: the average regional weekend visitor spends $412 per trip across lodging, food, and activities (ref-05), and drive-market weekend trips make up 61% of visits to small US regional destinations (ref-05), so the demand we need lives within a tank of gas.

The Dark-Sky Stargazing Winter Weekends package is our answer to that specific problem: a $59 add-on to lodging — guided constellation walk, heated observation decks with telescope stations, fire pit session, night-photography guide, clear-sky reschedule guarantee — that gives off-season travelers a bookable reason to fill those beds. Every tactic below is selected to convert off-peak occupancy, not just to admire the sky.

## Target Audience

Couples under 40 seeking novel off-season drive-market escapes — precisely the cohort whose trips start on a phone: 54% of travelers under 35 pick regional trips based on short-form video seen in the prior 30 days (ref-05). Our creative therefore shows a specific, bookable night — a named viewpoint, a named lodge, a $59 add-on — not generic scenery.

---

## The Big Idea

**"After Dark in the Valley"** positions winter nights as the valley's best-kept attraction, with the tagline "You've never seen night like this." Because the core problem is winter occupancy, the creative always pairs the sky with the stay: every video ends on the lodge, the package, and the booking link.

## Channel & Media Plan

Each channel maps back to the occupancy problem named above.

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video (Reels, TikTok) | Because winter trips are picked from short-form video — 54% of travelers under 35 pick regional trips based on short-form video seen in the prior 30 days (ref-05) — this carries the weight. Instagram Reels average a $9.40 CPM and TikTok $7.80 (ref-06), and short-form video ads average 2.4x the completion rate of static feed ads (ref-06) |
| Creator partnerships | Creator-partnership posts deliver 31% lower cost-per-engagement than brand-owned posts (ref-06); regional travel and photography creators make the night bookable, not just beautiful |
| Regional paid search & display | Captures in-market winter getaway demand at the moment of planning |
| Lodging-partner co-op, booking page & email | Converts inspiration into packaged bookings; sustained publishing matters because destinations running always-on social content see 27% higher repeat visitation than campaign-only destinations (ref-05) |
