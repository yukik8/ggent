# Clearbrook Wellness Newsletter Subscriber Drive — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft

**Campaign window:** March 1 – June 30, 2028
**Budget:** $130,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The Clearbrook Check-In"**

---

## Opening

Introducing The Clearbrook Check-In, a free wellness newsletter from Clearbrook Health. Health newsletters are having a genuine moment — wellness content is one of the fastest-growing corners of email and Substack culture, "health optimization" creators are everywhere, and inbox-first media is back in a big way. The Check-In puts Clearbrook into that stream with seasonal health tips, clinic updates, and a soft path toward the $79/month membership.

## Tactics

1. **Paid social sign-up ads:** Short, punchy creative across Instagram and Facebook promoting the newsletter with a one-tap sign-up.
2. **Content production:** A polished biweekly newsletter template plus a launch archive of five evergreen wellness guides to make the sign-up feel substantial.
3. **Display retargeting:** Follow up site visitors and past booking-page traffic with sign-up prompts.
4. **Email acquisition:** Sign-up prompts woven into existing post-visit emails and the booking confirmation flow.
5. **Local wellness influencer posts:** Regional fitness and wellness creators share their favorite tips from the launch issues.
6. **Growth loops:** A "forward to a friend" referral nudge in every issue, plus shareable tip graphics formatted for Stories.

## Target Audience

Past Clearbrook patients and residents across the clinic footprint. Broadly, anyone interested in staying on top of their health between visits — the newsletter is intentionally wide in appeal so the list can grow quickly.

## Amplification

Best-performing tips get boosted as standalone social posts, and each issue's top link becomes next issue's lead story to keep open rates climbing.

## Why This Will Work

Inbox-first wellness content is where audience attention is heading, and no local health network in the footprint has a newsletter presence. With sign-up ads, retargeting, influencer amplification, and built-in referral loops all feeding one list, The Check-In gives Clearbrook an owned channel it can use for every future campaign.
