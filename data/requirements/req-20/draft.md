# Sunbrook Crisps Single-Serve Convenience Expansion — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Sunbrook Foods
**Campaign window:** January 15 – April 30, 2026
**Budget:** $240,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Grab Greatness" — the Sunbrook Single-Serve Launch**

---

## Opening: Big Flavor, Pocket-Sized

Introducing the all-new Sunbrook Crisps single-serve bag — the same beloved, crave-worthy Sunbrook flavors, now in a perfectly portable 1.5oz format built for the convenience-store rack. These bags are bright, bold, and impossible to miss, with a punchy redesigned front panel, a resealable-feel matte finish, and a proud "$1.99" callout baked right into the packaging so the value story is instant. Every hero flavor makes the jump, from Original Sea Salt to the fan-favorite Honey BBQ, so there's a bag for every mood and every road trip.

At $1.99, the single-serve line is an easy, almost impulsive yes. It sits beautifully next to the register, it fits in a cup holder, and it turns the everyday gas-station stop into a chance to fall in love with Sunbrook. Convenience stores are where so much snacking actually happens these days, and grab-and-go formats are absolutely booming right now. This launch takes everything people already adore about Sunbrook and puts it exactly where hungry hands are reaching.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is on-the-go snackers aged 18-34 who shop at convenience stores — commuters grabbing fuel and a treat, students between classes, shift workers on break, and road-trippers stocking the passenger seat. They're busy, spontaneous, and phone-first. They make snack decisions in seconds, often right at the counter, and they love brands that feel fun and a little bit rewarding in the middle of a hectic day. Social media plays a huge role in what they notice and what they crave, and many of them are constantly discovering new snacks through short videos and word of mouth.

Secondary audiences include existing Sunbrook grocery loyalists who will be delighted to see their favorite crisps waiting for them at the pump.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Grab Greatness"** turns the humble c-store snack run into a tiny moment of triumph. The creative celebrates all the small wins of a busy day — nailing the yellow light, finding the good parking spot, surviving the 3pm meeting — and positions the Sunbrook single-serve as the trophy you grab on the way. Every asset ends on the same satisfying beat: a hand grabbing a Sunbrook bag off the rack like it's a medal. It's playful, endlessly meme-able, and it makes a $1.99 purchase feel like a celebration instead of an afterthought.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (TikTok & Instagram Reels)
Short-form video is where our audience spends their scroll time, and food content performs wonderfully there. We'll run a steady flight of "small wins" vignettes — quick, funny, relatable clips that end with the grab. Geo-flighted around priority c-store markets so the content lands where the racks are.

### 2. Gas-Station Screens & Proximity OOH
Pump-top screens and digital boards near convenience stores catch people at the perfect moment — literally minutes before they walk past the rack. We'll run snackable six-second cuts of the "Grab Greatness" spots plus bold price-forward frames, so the $1.99 bag is already on their mind when the door chimes.

### 3. C-Store Retail Media & Loyalty App Offers
The big convenience chains have really strong apps and loyalty programs now, and shoppers love a deal. We'll place sponsored tiles and a launch offer (think a first-bag deal or a fuel-points bonus) inside partner loyalty apps to drive that very first trial and encourage repeat grabs.

### 4. Point-of-Sale Displays & Counter Racks
Nothing beats being right there at the register. We'll invest in eye-catching counter racks, shelf strips, and a "Grab Greatness" header card program across priority locations, making sure the new bags own the impulse zone during the launch window.
