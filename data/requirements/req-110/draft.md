# Ledgerline AI Invoice-Coding Module Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Ledgerline
**Campaign window:** September 1, 2027 – February 28, 2028
**Budget:** $470,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Code Less. Close Faster."**

---

## Opening: The Smartest Thing Ledgerline Has Ever Shipped

Introducing the Ledgerline AI invoice-coding module, a brilliant new add-on that automatically codes invoices to the right GL accounts using machine learning trained on each customer's own history. It is fast, remarkably accurate, and gets smarter with every invoice. Priced at $9,000 per year per account, it slots seamlessly into the AP suite customers already love, with no new implementation project required.

AI is transforming finance software, and this module puts Ledgerline right at the front of that wave. Our campaign will bring this exciting capability to existing accounts and turn enthusiasm into upgrades.

---

## Target Audience

Controllers at existing Ledgerline accounts who are buried in manual GL coding. They are busy, practical finance leaders who appreciate tools that genuinely save time. Because they already know and trust Ledgerline, they are a warm and receptive audience for a smart new add-on.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Code Less. Close Faster."** dramatizes the before-and-after of AI coding: the late nights of manual entry versus a close that practically runs itself. The creative is confident and a little playful, showing real finance moments transformed by an impressively capable product.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. LinkedIn & Account-Based Media
LinkedIn is the natural home for reaching finance leaders. We run targeted creative to controllers and CFOs at existing accounts, highlighting the module's impressive capabilities.

### 2. Customer Email & Lifecycle Program
A launch announcement series to the customer base, followed by tailored nurture flows for accounts showing interest.

### 3. Webinar & Demo Series
Live product showcases where finance teams see the module code real invoices, plus an on-demand library.

### 4. Case Study & Content Production
Early-adopter stories, a launch video, and content showing the module in action.

### 5. CSM Enablement & In-App Messaging
Customer success managers get pitch kits and demo environments, and in-app banners surface the module to active users at relevant moments.

### 6. Landing Pages & Creative
A dedicated module page with demo booking and pricing.
