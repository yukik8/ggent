# Kinetiq Podcast Sponsorship Flight — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Kinetiq Home
**Campaign window:** February 1 – May 31, 2027
**Budget:** $190,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Heard While Moving" — the Kinetiq Podcast Flight**

---

## Opening

Introducing "Heard While Moving," a four-month host-read podcast sponsorship flight for the Kinetiq rower at $1,795. Podcasts are an intimate, trusted medium, host-read ads consistently outperform standard spots, and fitness and productivity shows reach listeners during commutes and workouts — moments when they are already thinking about their routines. The plan is straightforward: a curated show list, a consistent host-read script framework, and unique promo codes per show.

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Format | 60-second host-reads + 30-second mid-rolls |
| Show mix | Fitness, health, business, and productivity podcasts |
| Offer | Unique promo code per show, $100 off accessories bundle |
| Cadence | 2 reads per show per month, all four months |
| Tracking | Promo codes + dedicated landing pages per show |

---

## Target Audience

Fitness and productivity podcast listeners aged 25-45. They are routine-oriented, self-improvement minded, and comfortable making considered purchases online. Podcast listening fits naturally into their day, and host recommendations carry real weight with them.

---

## The Big Idea

Let trusted voices make the case. Hosts receive a rower and a simple script framework — their honest experience, the 30-day trial, and the show's promo code — and tell the story in their own words. Consistency across four months builds familiarity; the codes and landing pages tell us exactly which voices convert.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Tier | Shows | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Anchor | 3 large fitness/health shows | Reach and credibility, monthly reads |
| Tier 2 — Core | 8 mid-size fitness and productivity shows | Frequency and conversion, twice-monthly reads |
| Tier 3 — Test | 6 smaller niche shows | Low-cost tests, rotated quarterly based on code redemptions |

### 1. Host-Read Sponsorships — Fitness & Health Shows
Anchor and core fitness shows carry the product story to listeners already invested in their health. Hosts speak from their own use of the rower.

### 2. Host-Read Sponsorships — Business & Productivity Shows
Productivity and business shows reach the routine-builder audience — people who think in systems and habits and appreciate the at-home time efficiency angle.

### 3. Promo Landing Pages & Tracking
Each show gets a dedicated landing page and code so we can compare performance across tiers and reallocate reads to what converts.

## Why Kinetiq Wins

Podcast host-reads put Kinetiq's story in the most trusted slot in audio, told by voices listeners already believe, at moments when routines are top of mind. With a tiered show list, disciplined tracking, and four months of consistent presence, the flight builds familiarity and shows us exactly where to double down.
