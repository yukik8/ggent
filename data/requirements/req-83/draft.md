**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft

## Campaign Title

**"Spring Starts at Hartline"**

---

## Opening: The Most Beautiful Season in the Store

Introducing the Hartline Spring Yard Shop — a gorgeous, fully refreshed seasonal lineup that turns every one of Hartline's 40 stores into the most inviting garden destination in the lower Midwest. Picture it: lush live-goods displays spilling out onto the sidewalk, vibrant raised-bed kits, beautiful new garden bundles starting from just $99, and friendly associates who actually know when to plant tomatoes in Tulsa. The Spring Yard Shop brings together everything a homeowner needs for a stunning spring transformation — soil, seed, tools, live goods, and expert advice — all in one delightful, walkable seasonal zone.

Spring is the biggest, most exciting moment of the year in home improvement, and homeowners are itching to get outside after winter. The garden bundles are the heroes of the lineup: curated, seasonal, and priced from $99, they make it incredibly easy for any homeowner to say yes to a beautiful yard. This campaign will make Hartline the place where spring officially begins.

---

## Target Audience

Suburban homeowners across Hartline's trade areas who are preparing their yards for spring. They love their outdoor spaces, they browse inspiration content through the winter, and they get truly excited when the weather turns. Many of them are wonderful, loyal Hartline shoppers already, and many more could be.

## The Big Idea

**"Spring Starts at Hartline"** declares Hartline the official starting line of the season. Everything — the ads, the store zones, the weekend events — carries one warm, confident message: when you're ready for spring, we're ready for you.

---

## Channel Plan

### 1. Paid Social & Short-Form Video
Beautiful, energizing short-form videos of yard transformations, bundle unboxings, and planting walkthroughs across Reels, TikTok, and YouTube. Social video is incredibly effective for seasonal inspiration and will carry awareness for the campaign.

### 2. Content Production — How-To & Inspiration
A lovely series of how-to videos and photography: "Your First Raised Bed," "Weekend Lawn Rescue," "Patio Refresh Under $250." This content fuels the paid flight and lives on Hartline's site and channels.

### 3. In-Store Weekend Garden Demos
Every Saturday through peak season, stores host cheerful garden demos — seed starting, bed prep, container gardening — right in the Spring Yard Shop zone, with bundles stacked beside the demo table.

### 4. Local Out-of-Home & Radio
Big, sunny billboards and drive-time radio across the store markets announce that spring has arrived at Hartline. These channels build broad seasonal presence in the community.

### 5. Email & CRM
A spring countdown series to the full customer list, with planting-calendar content localized by region and bundle offers timed to the weather.
