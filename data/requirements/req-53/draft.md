# Blue Fork Kitchen Summer Limited-Time Bowl Promotion — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Blue Fork Kitchen
**Campaign window:** June 1 – August 15, 2026
**Budget:** $210,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Golden Hour" — the Blue Fork Summer Bowl**

---

## Opening: The Most Beautiful Bowl of the Year

Introducing the Golden Hour Bowl, the most gorgeous thing to ever come off a Blue Fork make-line. Charred sweet corn, blistered peaches, honey-lime chicken (or crispy chickpeas), whipped feta, and a chili-honey drizzle over golden turmeric rice — it's summer in a bowl, it photographs like an absolute sunset, and it lands at **$13.90** for a strictly limited run from June 1 to August 15. When it's gone, it's gone.

Limited-time menu items are wonderfully exciting for guests, and this one has everything: seasonal ingredients at their peak, colors that glow on camera, and a name that taps into the golden-hour moment everyone already loves to post. Summer is when people crave something bright and fresh, and the Golden Hour Bowl gives both loyal regulars and guests we haven't seen in a while a truly delicious reason to come back through the door. This is Blue Fork at its sunniest, and the campaign around it should feel just as warm and joyful as the bowl itself.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is Blue Fork's existing guests — the wonderful regulars who already have the app and a favorite order, and who light up at something new to try. Our second audience is lapsed visitors: people who used to come in but have drifted away over the past six months or more. A stunning, strictly seasonal bowl is exactly the kind of gentle, delightful nudge that reminds them why they loved Blue Fork in the first place. Both groups are active on social, comfortable ordering ahead, and very responsive to limited-time news from brands they already know.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Golden Hour"** celebrates the fleeting, glowing, best part of the day — and of the menu. The bowl is only here for the summer, just like golden hour is only here for a moment, and the whole campaign leans into that lovely urgency: catch it while it glows. Creative shows the bowl bathed in warm evening light, ingredients tumbling in slow motion, and real guests catching their own golden hour on patios and park benches, bowl in hand. Every asset ends on the same line: *Here for a golden moment. Gone August 15.*

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Instagram Reels & TikTok)
Gorgeous food deserves gorgeous video. We'll run geo-targeted Reels and TikTok flights around all 85 locations with sun-drenched hero footage, make-line ASMR of the bowl coming together, and countdown creative as August 15 approaches. Food content thrives in short-form video, and this bowl was practically designed for the format.

### 2. CRM — App Push, Email & In-App Features
Our warmest audience already carries Blue Fork in their pocket. Members get first taste: an early-access day before the public launch, a "Golden Hour is here" announcement flow, bonus points on their first Golden Hour Bowl, and a lovely little "last call" series in the final two weeks. Lapsed guests get their own gentle win-back message — the bowl is the perfect excuse to say "come see what's new."

### 3. Local Creator Partnerships
We'll invite food and lifestyle creators across Blue Fork's markets to share their golden hour — their favorite patio, their post-beach stop, their bowl in the evening light. Creator content brings a genuine, personal warmth that brand ads can't quite match, and their footage feeds our paid flight beautifully.

### 4. In-Store Merchandising
The restaurants themselves become golden. Window clings, counter cards, and pickup-shelf signage in warm sunset tones announce the bowl, and team members wear "Ask me about Golden Hour" pins during launch weeks. The in-store moment is where curiosity becomes a first taste.
