## Campaign Title

**"Shift Into Spring" — the Northtrail Tune-Up Season**

---

## Opening: The $89 Tune-Up Package

The Northtrail Spring Tune-Up is an $89 all-in service package delivered through Northtrail's certified service network. The package is genuinely well built. It covers a full battery health diagnostic with a printed report, brake and drivetrain service, tire inspection and pressure calibration, firmware updates, torque check on all safety-critical fasteners, and a frame and connector clean. Riders book online, drop the bike off, and pick it up within 48 hours with a checklist of everything done.

The package is a strong offer at a fair price. Comparable à-la-carte service at an independent shop would cost noticeably more, and independent shops generally cannot run Northtrail's proprietary battery diagnostic. Spring is the natural moment for this product, since many riders are pulling bikes out of winter storage and want confidence before the riding season starts. E-bike ownership keeps growing, so the pool of bikes that need annual service grows with it. This campaign brings the tune-up package to every owner in Northtrail's service-area cities.

## Target Audience

The primary audience is e-bike owners in Northtrail's service-area cities. This includes Northtrail owners in the CRM as well as owners of other e-bike brands, since the tune-up package is open to all makes. These riders care about reliability and battery longevity, and a lot of them worry about what winter storage did to their battery. Many owners skip annual service simply because nobody reminds them at the right moment.

| Segment | Description | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Northtrail owners (CRM) | Purchased in the last 3 years, addressable by email | High |
| Other-brand e-bike owners | In service-area cities, reachable via paid media | Medium |
| Lapsed service customers | Serviced in 2024 or earlier, not since | Medium |

## The Big Idea

**"Shift Into Spring"** frames the tune-up as the official start of riding season. The creative centers on a simple ritual: the first warm Saturday of the year, the bike comes out of the garage, and before the first real ride it gets its 48-hour spring reset. We anchor the campaign to a "Ride-Ready Report" — the printed battery and safety checklist every customer takes home — which turns an invisible service into something owners can hold, photograph, and share. The message across every channel is the same: don't start the season on a winter battery.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Owner Email & CRM Journey
A three-touch email sequence to the Northtrail owner base: an early-bird booking invitation in March, a battery-health education send, and a last-call reminder in May. Email to an owned list is very cost-efficient and owners tend to respond well to service reminders. Lapsed service customers get a dedicated win-back variant.

### 2. Geo-Targeted Paid Social (Instagram Reels & Facebook)
Short videos targeted to service-area cities showing the tune-up process, the battery report, and the 48-hour turnaround. Social video is an effective way to reach riders who own other brands and do not know Northtrail services all makes. Creative rotates weekly so frequency stays comfortable.

### 3. Local Search & Maps
Paid search on tune-up and e-bike repair terms, plus sponsored map placements for Northtrail service locations. When a rider decides to get their bike serviced, search is usually the first place they go, so we want to own that moment in every service-area city.

### 4. In-Store & Service-Center Activation
Window kits, counter cards, and a "Spring Check Saturday" event series where walk-ins get a free 5-minute battery health read and a same-day booking discount code. In-person moments convert well and give the campaign a physical presence in each city.
