# Blue Fork Limited-Run Merch Drop Collaboration — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Blue Fork Kitchen
**Campaign window:** September 1 – November 15, 2028
**Budget:** $130,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The Fork Drop" — Limited-Run Merch Collaboration**

## Opening: Blue Fork Goes Streetwear

Introducing The Fork Drop, an absolutely iconic limited-run merch collaboration between Blue Fork and a rising Southeastern streetwear label. The collection features gorgeous heavyweight tees, embroidered caps, an insulated bowl tote, and a statement crewneck, all built around Blue Fork's signature blue with genuinely beautiful, wearable design. Pieces average $28 and drop in three numbered waves, each one limited, each one special, each one gone when it's gone.

Food-brand merch is one of the most exciting spaces in culture right now. The best drops sell out in hours, generate incredible buzz, and turn everyday customers into walking billboards. The Fork Drop is Blue Fork's moment to join that conversation and show the world it's not just a lunch spot — it's a brand people love enough to wear.

## Target Audience

Brand-loyal Blue Fork guests and streetwear-adjacent diners aged 18-30. These are trend-aware, style-conscious fans who love limited things, love their local spots, and love telling people about both. They follow drop culture closely and move fast when something limited appears. If we make the collection desirable enough, they will show up, share it, and sell it out for us.

## The Big Idea

**"Wear your order."** Each wave of the drop is themed to a fan-favorite bowl, with colorways and embroidery inspired by the ingredients. The collection celebrates the devotion Blue Fork regulars already feel and makes being a regular something you can literally put on.

## Channel Plan

### 1. Paid Social Teasers & Launch Flights
Cinematic teaser content and drop-day countdowns on Reels and TikTok, building hype wave by wave.

### 2. Streetwear & Food Creator Seeding
Early pieces seeded to local streetwear creators and food personalities who genuinely love the brand, driving authentic unboxings and fit checks.

### 3. Launch-Day Pop-Ups
Drop-day pop-up moments at three flagship city locations with exclusive colorways, DJ sets, and bowl-and-merch bundles. The lines themselves become content.

### 4. App & Email Announcements
Members get early access windows through the app, making loyalty feel like a VIP pass.
