# Pawstead Multi-Dog Household Bundle Campaign — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Pawstead Fresh
**Campaign window:** February 1 – May 31, 2028
**Budget:** $300,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Feed the Whole Pack"**

---

## Opening: One Bundle, Every Wagging Tail

Introducing the Pawstead Multi-Dog Household Bundle, a wonderfully simple $190/month two-dog plan that brings fresh, vet-formulated meals to every dog in the house. The bundle features individually portioned meals for each dog's size and needs, one combined delivery, one consolidated fridge footprint, and meaningful savings versus two separate plans. It is everything multi-dog families have been asking for — flexible, fresh, and delightfully easy.

Multi-dog homes are some of the most passionate pet households out there, and right now most of them are juggling mixed brands, mismatched deliveries, and crowded fridges. The bundle sweeps all of that chaos away in one delicious subscription.

---

## Target Audience

Two-or-more-dog households currently mixing food brands. These are devoted, big-hearted pet parents who want the best for every dog they own. They love sharing their pack online, and they respond warmly to brands that understand the multi-dog lifestyle.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Feed the Whole Pack"** celebrates the beautiful chaos of multi-dog life — the synchronized dinner dances, the double zoomies, the one dog who always finishes first. Every asset lands the same promise: one plan, every bowl, no chaos.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Multi-dog content is absolutely adored on social media, and our hero videos of packs eating together will be irresistible and highly shareable.

### 2. Creator Partnerships
Multi-dog creator households will document switching their whole pack to the bundle, showing the real fridge, the real delivery, and the real savings.

### 3. CRM & Email to Existing Subscribers
Many current Pawstead customers own more than one dog and feed the second one something else. Warm, personal emails will invite them to bring the whole pack over.

### 4. Paid Search & Display
We will capture searches around multi-dog feeding, fresh dog food for two dogs, and subscription comparisons.
