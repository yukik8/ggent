This cohort is the perfect bridge between those two worlds, and our campaign will make it the most talked-about career-change program of the spring. Skillharbor has built something genuinely special here, and we cannot wait to show it off.

Career changers aged 25-40 in stagnant roles. These are ambitious, capable professionals who feel stuck where they are and know there is something better out there for them. They are active on LinkedIn, they listen to career and money podcasts on their commutes, and they spend evenings scrolling short-form video. They are motivated but cautious, and they respond to content that feels genuine and encouraging.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
Short-form video is a fantastic way to reach busy professionals, and career-transformation content performs wonderfully in these feeds. We will run an energetic flight of cohort-journey videos, instructor introductions, and day-in-the-life-of-an-analyst content that makes the destination feel real and reachable.

### 2. LinkedIn Paid + Paid Search
LinkedIn is where our audience already thinks about their careers, so sponsored content and lead-gen placements will feel completely native there. Paid search captures people actively looking for analytics courses and career-change advice.

### 3. Podcast Sponsorships
Career and personal-finance podcasts are a beloved companion for commuting professionals. Host-read spots let trusted voices tell the Skillharbor story in their own words, which feels far more personal than a standard ad.

### 4. Creator Partnerships
We will partner with career-pivot creators who have genuinely made the leap into data roles. Their stories are inspiring and authentic, and their audiences are exactly the people we want in this cohort.

### 5. Free Intro Workshop Funnel
A live, free "First Dashboard in 60 Minutes" workshop gives prospects a taste of the teaching quality and the cohort feeling. Workshop attendees flow into an email nurture sequence leading up to enrollment deadlines.
