## Campaign Title

**"The Summer Seeding" — Kinetiq Creator Gifting Program**

---

## Opening

Introducing The Summer Seeding, a creator gifting program that puts the $1,795 Kinetiq rower in the hands of the internet's most watchable fitness people all summer long. Seeding is the play of the moment — every breakout DTC brand of the last few years got there by getting product into creator content early and often, and fitness is the most giftable category there is. Unboxings are still huge, "day in my life" formats are everywhere, morning routine content never dies, and 75 Hard-style challenge content is having another wave. A rower showing up organically across those formats, week after week, is how a brand becomes part of the culture instead of an ad in it.

---

## The Big Idea

Gift the machine, harvest the summer. Forty creators receive a rower with zero posting obligations — just a beautiful unboxing experience, a starter class plan, and a group chat with our team. No scripts, no mandated hashtags. The bet is that a genuinely great machine plus a genuinely great onboarding will earn its way into content that money cannot buy, and the handful of creators who fall in love become long-term partners.

---

## Tactics

### 1. The Gifting Wave
Forty rowers shipped across three drops (June, July, August), each drop themed to the season — summer reset, mid-year push, back-to-routine. White-glove delivery and assembly included, because setup friction kills gifting content.

### 2. Unboxing Experience
A custom crate, a welcome letter, heat-transfer name plate on the rower, and a QR code to a private starter collection. The unboxing is designed to be filmable from the first latch.

### 3. Creator Group Chat & Drops
A private channel with our team for support, plus periodic surprise drops (branded gear, class collabs) to keep the rower present in content past week one.

### 4. Amplification of Winners
When a post pops, we put paid spend behind it fast and negotiate usage rights, converting organic moments into a rolling stream of proven ad creative.

### 5. Content Capture Days
Two shoot days with the most engaged creators to bank higher-production content for Kinetiq's own channels.

---

## Target Audience

Audiences of fitness creators — the followers who watch morning routines, home gym tours, and challenge content. They skew engaged, aspirational, and already primed for home fitness, and they take recommendations from creators they follow seriously.

Seeding works because it does not feel like advertising — it feels like discovery. Put a genuinely beautiful machine into forty of the right feeds with zero strings attached, make the unboxing irresistible, and amplify whatever the summer produces. The brands winning fitness right now all started exactly this way.
