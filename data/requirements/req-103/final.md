## Campaign Title

**"12 Days of Glow" — the Velura Advent Calendar**

## Opening

Introducing the Velura 12-Day Skincare Advent Calendar at $78: twelve doors, twelve minis, and a full-size hero product behind the final door. The beauty advent calendar is a proven seasonal format — calendars sell out year after year, and the unboxing structure generates a steady stream of content across the holiday window. This program puts Velura into that format with a tightly run seasonal plan.

## Target Audience

Holiday gift shoppers and self-gifting beauty buyers aged 20-40 — people gifting a skincare lover, and skincare lovers treating themselves. Both groups respond to scarcity and to watching the calendar opened door by door in their feeds.

## The Program

1. **Door-a-day creator series** — partner creators open one door daily for twelve days, creating a twelve-episode arc.
2. **ASMR unboxing content** — the format's most-watched genre, produced in advance for launch week.
3. **Sellout-clock mechanic** — live "calendars remaining" counters in stories and on the product page.
4. **Ranking and reveal formats** — "best to worst door" and "guess tomorrow's door" participation content.
5. **Gift-guide seeding** — placements with holiday gift-guide creators and roundup accounts.
6. **Early-access email drop** — the existing list shops first, with scarcity messaging carrying the follow-up.
7. **Self-gifting lane** — a "you earned this" message track for buyers treating themselves.

## Channels

- Paid and organic social (TikTok, Reels)
- Creator partnerships (unboxing, gift-guide, skincare creators)
- Email & CRM to the existing list
- Retail pop-up & product-page support

## KPIs

- Calendar sellout ahead of December 12
- Door-a-day series completion and engagement rates
- Email early-access conversion rate
- Earned pickup in gift guides and roundups

## Why This Will Work

The advent calendar is the rare product that is also a content format: every door is a post and every open is an ad. The plan front-loads production so the twelve-day finale runs itself, the early-access drop rewards the existing list, and the sellout mechanic converts attention into urgency. Velura gets daily brand presence through the highest-spend weeks of the year, on a schedule our production team can hit with margin to spare.
