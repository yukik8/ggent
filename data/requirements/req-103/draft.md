## Campaign Title

**"12 Days of Glow" — the Velura Advent Calendar**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Velura 12-Day Skincare Advent Calendar at $78 — twelve doors, twelve minis, one grand-finale full-size hero. Advent calendar content is one of the most reliable seasonal formats on social: unboxing runs, door-a-day series, ranking videos, ASMR opens. Beauty advent calendars sell out every year and the content practically makes itself. Velura needs to be in this format now.

---

## The Tactic Stack

1. **Door-a-day creator series** — creators open one door daily for twelve days, a built-in twelve-episode content arc.
2. **ASMR unboxing content** — paper tears, jar clicks, tissue rustle; the format's most-watched genre.
3. **Sellout-clock mechanic** — live "calendars remaining" counters in stories and on the product page to drive urgency.
4. **Ranking and reveal formats** — "best to worst door" and "guess tomorrow's door" duet bait.
5. **Gift-guide seeding** — placements with holiday gift-guide creators and roundup accounts.
6. **Early-access email drop** — the list shops first; scarcity does the rest.
7. **Self-gifting angle** — a "you survived this year" message lane for buyers treating themselves.

---

## Target Audience

The buyers are holiday gift shoppers and self-gifting beauty enthusiasts aged 20-40 — a mix of people gifting a skincare lover and skincare lovers gifting themselves. Both respond to scarcity and to seeing the calendar opened door by door in their feed.

## Why This Will Work

The advent calendar is the rare product that is also a content format. Every door is a post, every open is an ad, and scarcity is baked into the SKU. Velura gets twelve days of daily brand presence during the highest-spend weeks of the year.
