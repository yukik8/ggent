## Campaign Title

**"Give Good Skin" — the Velura Holiday Gift Program**

---

## Opening: The Prettiest Box Under the Tree

Introducing the Velura Holiday Gift Set — honestly one of the most giftable things Velura has ever made. The $58 hero set bundles the beloved Barrier Repair Moisturizer with a travel-size cleanser and the new silk-touch headband, all wrapped in a gorgeous frosted-glass-inspired box with a botanical sleeve that slides off to reveal a hand-illustrated winter garden. No wrapping paper needed — the box IS the wrapping. There is also a $32 mini set for stocking-stuffer territory and a deluxe $88 set for the serious skincare lover on your list.

What makes these sets so special is that they carry everything people already adore about Velura: fragrance-free formulas, short and readable ingredient lists, and that clean, calming aesthetic that photographs beautifully. Gifting season is a magical time for beauty brands, and shoppers are always hunting for presents that feel thoughtful without being risky. A fragrance-free set is the ultimate safe-but-special gift — nobody's sensitive skin gets angry, and everybody's shelfie gets prettier. This is Velura's moment to shine under the tree.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is gift shoppers aged 20-45 buying beauty gifts. This is a wonderfully broad group — partners shopping for their significant others, friends doing gift exchanges, adult kids buying for moms, and coworkers grabbing something elegant for Secret Santa. Many of them are not skincare experts themselves, which is exactly why they gravitate toward brands that feel trustworthy and premium. They browse gift guides, they save ideas on social media, and they respond to products that look as good as they feel.

A big secondary audience is existing Velura customers, who already know and love the moisturizer and just need a nudge to buy it for someone else (and maybe sneak a set into their own cart too).

---

## The Big Idea

**"Give Good Skin"** turns the gift set into the season's easiest thoughtful gift. The whole campaign is built around one warm, reassuring promise: you don't need to know their routine to give them something their skin will love. Creative leans into the unboxing moment — the sleeve slide, the frosted box, the little gasp — and pairs it with real talk about why fragrance-free is the kindest gift for any skin type. Every asset ends on the same gentle tagline: *"When in doubt, give good skin."*

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Reels & TikTok)
Short-form video is where gift inspiration happens, and unboxing content is practically made for this format. We will run a strong flight of Reels and TikTok placements showing the unboxing moment, gift-pairing ideas ("for your mom / your roommate / your picky sister"), and cozy holiday vignettes. Social will do the heavy lifting on awareness and inspiration all season.

### 2. Creator Gift Guides
We will partner with skincare and lifestyle creators to feature the sets in their holiday gift guides. Creator recommendations feel so much more genuine than brand ads, especially for gifting, where shoppers really want a trusted voice saying "this one is safe to give." We will prioritize creators whose audiences already trust their skincare picks.

### 3. Paid Search & Shopping
Lots of gift shoppers go straight to search when they're stuck for ideas. We will capture terms like "skincare gift set," "gifts for her," and "fragrance-free gift ideas" with shopping placements and text ads pointing to a dedicated holiday gift shop page.

### 4. Specialty Retail In-Store Support
Velura's specialty retail presence gives the sets a physical stage at exactly the moment shoppers are browsing for gifts. We will fund holiday endcap displays and shelf-talkers in partner stores, so the beautiful packaging can do its thing in person.

### 5. Email & CRM
Our warmest audience is the existing customer list. A holiday email series will introduce the sets, spotlight the gift-shipping cutoff dates, and remind loyalists that the easiest gift is the moisturizer they already swear by — in a prettier box.
