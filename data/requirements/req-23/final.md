## Campaign Title

**"Give Good Skin" — the Velura Holiday Gift Program**

---

## Opening: The Prettiest Box Under the Tree

Introducing the Velura Holiday Gift Set, one of the most giftable products Velura has ever made. The $58 hero set bundles the Barrier Repair Moisturizer with a travel-size cleanser and the new silk-touch headband, presented in a frosted-glass-inspired box with a botanical sleeve that slides off to reveal a hand-illustrated winter garden. No wrapping paper is needed — the box is the wrapping. The lineup is rounded out by a $32 mini set for stocking-stuffer territory and a deluxe $88 set for the serious skincare lover.

The sets carry everything people already love about Velura: fragrance-free formulas, short and readable ingredient lists, and a clean, calming aesthetic that photographs beautifully. Gifting season is a major moment for beauty brands — industry reporting suggests roughly a third of annual beauty gifting purchases happen in the final six weeks of the year — and shoppers are hunting for presents that feel thoughtful without being risky. A fragrance-free set is the safe-but-special gift: no sensitive skin gets irritated, and every shelf gets prettier. This is Velura's moment under the tree.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary target is gift shoppers aged 20-45 buying beauty gifts. It is a broad group — partners shopping for significant others, friends doing gift exchanges, adult kids buying for moms, and coworkers choosing something elegant for Secret Santa. Many are not skincare experts themselves, which is exactly why they gravitate toward brands that feel trustworthy and premium. They browse gift guides, save ideas on social media, and respond to products that look as good as they feel.

A meaningful secondary audience is existing Velura customers, who already know and love the moisturizer and simply need a nudge to buy it for someone else — and perhaps add a set to their own cart while they're at it.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Give Good Skin"** turns the gift set into the season's easiest thoughtful gift. The campaign is built on one warm, reassuring promise: you don't need to know their routine to give them something their skin will love. Creative leans into the unboxing moment — the sleeve slide, the frosted box, the small gasp — paired with straightforward messaging on why fragrance-free is the kindest gift for any skin type. Every asset closes on the same tagline: *"When in doubt, give good skin."*

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Reels & TikTok)
Short-form video is where gift inspiration happens, and unboxing content is made for the format. We will run a strong flight of Reels and TikTok placements showing the unboxing moment, gift-pairing ideas ("for your mom / your roommate / your picky sister"), and cozy holiday vignettes. Social carries the awareness and inspiration load throughout the season.

### 2. Creator Gift Guides
We will partner with skincare and lifestyle creators to feature the sets in their holiday gift guides. Creator recommendations feel more genuine than brand ads, especially in gifting, where shoppers want a trusted voice confirming "this one is safe to give." We will prioritize creators whose audiences already trust their skincare picks.

### 3. Paid Search & Shopping
Many gift shoppers go straight to search when they're stuck for ideas. We will capture terms like "skincare gift set," "gifts for her," and "fragrance-free gift ideas" with shopping placements and text ads pointing to a dedicated holiday gift shop page.

### 4. Specialty Retail In-Store Support
Velura's specialty retail presence gives the sets a physical stage at precisely the moment shoppers are browsing for gifts. We will fund holiday endcap displays and shelf-talkers in partner stores, letting the packaging work in person.

### 5. Email & CRM
Our warmest audience is the existing customer list. A holiday email series will introduce the sets, spotlight gift-shipping cutoff dates, and remind loyal customers that the easiest gift is the moisturizer they already swear by — in a prettier box.
