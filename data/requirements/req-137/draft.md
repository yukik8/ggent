# Stackwise "Money Wrapped" Year-in-Review Promo — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Stackwise
**Campaign window:** November 15 – December 31, 2028
**Budget:** $140,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Your Year in Money"**

---

## Opening: The Most Shareable Thing Stackwise Has Ever Made

Introducing Money Wrapped, a gorgeous, personality-packed year-in-review experience inside Stackwise. Every user gets a beautifully animated story of their money year: total saved, biggest no-spend streak, their "saver personality," and a shareable card designed for stories and group chats. It's free for everyone, with premium users getting extra slides and custom themes.

Year-in-review content is one of the most beloved formats on the internet, and Money Wrapped gives Stackwise users something wonderful to share at exactly the moment everyone else is sharing theirs. This is the brand's big organic moment of the year.

---

## Target Audience

Active Stackwise users who love sharing milestones — the people already screenshotting their savings streaks. Secondary: their followers and group chats, who see the cards and get curious about the app.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Your Year in Money"** turns personal finance into a celebration. The creative embraces the warm, confetti-filled energy of December recap season: proud, funny, a little competitive, and never preachy.

---

## Channel Plan

### 1. In-App & Push Reveal
A delightful reveal moment when Wrapped drops, with push and email driving every active user to their story in the first week.

### 2. Paid Social Amplification (TikTok & Reels)
Fun, fast-cut promos of the Wrapped experience and reaction-style creative, boosting the best organic card-shares.

### 3. Creator Amplification
Finance and lifestyle creators share their own Wrapped cards and react to their saver personalities, seeding the share wave.

### 4. Share-Card Production
A polished template system so every shared card looks stunning in stories, feeds, and group chats.

Money Wrapped is joyful, personal, and perfectly timed. Recap season is when everyone shares, the cards are designed to be shown off, and every share is a warm, authentic ad for Stackwise from someone a future user actually trusts.
