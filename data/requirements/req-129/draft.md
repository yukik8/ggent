**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft

**Budget:** $180,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The Kinetiq Regatta" — Live Race-Event Series**

## Opening

Introducing the Kinetiq Regatta, a quarterly series of live in-app rowing races broadcast from the Kinetiq studio. Members race in real time against the full community across three distance classes — 500m sprint, 2,000m classic, and a 10,000m endurance event — with live leaderboards, instructor commentary, and finisher badges. Entry is free with the $39/month membership. The Regatta turns the class library into a live sporting event and gives the platform an energy that on-demand content alone cannot match.

## Event Summary

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Format | Three live race weekends, one per month |
| Distance classes | 500m sprint, 2,000m classic, 10,000m endurance |
| Broadcast | Live from Kinetiq studio with instructor commentary |
| Entry | Free with $39/month membership |
| Rewards | Finisher badges, class winners featured in-app |

## Target Audience

Competitive members and rowing-community prospects. This includes existing members who chase leaderboards, former club and collegiate rowers, and indoor-racing enthusiasts who follow erg competitions online.

## The Big Idea

**"Race day comes home."** The Regatta gives every member a start line, a crowd, and a result that counts. Creative builds around race-day rituals: the warm-up, the countdown, the final sprint, the leaderboard refresh.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| In-app & member email promotion | Race announcements, registration pushes, training-plan tie-ins |
| Paid social to rowing communities | Race hype creative targeted at rowing and erg-racing interest audiences |
| Event broadcast production | Professional multi-camera live production for the three race weekends |
| Community & creator partnerships | Rowing creators and club ambassadors race live and recap each event |

Rowing communities are highly engaged, and live events create appointment moments that on-demand libraries lack. Social clips of race finishes should travel well among enthusiast audiences.

## KPIs

- Race registrations and live participation rates
- Member workout frequency during race season
- Social engagement on race clips and recaps
- New memberships attributed to Regatta content

## Why This Will Work

Live racing is a natural extension of connected rowing, and the Regatta packages it with real production quality. Competitive members get a stage, prospects see the platform at its most exciting, and Kinetiq gets a recurring event franchise that builds community quarter after quarter.
