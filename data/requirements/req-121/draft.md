# Pawstead Dog-Birthday Club Promo — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Pawstead Fresh
**Campaign window:** July 15 – October 15, 2027
**Budget:** $140,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The Pawstead Birthday Club"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Pawstead Birthday Club, a $25 birthday box that turns every dog's birthday into a Pawstead moment. The box includes a fresh birthday cake portion, a party hat, a candle-shaped chew, and a shareable "it's my barkday" card. Pet birthdays are absolutely everywhere on social right now — barkday parties, dog cake smashes, gotcha-day posts — and Pawstead can own the trend with a product built for the camera.

---

## Tactic Stack

We run every play that is working in pet social right now:

1. **Short-form social video.** Cake-smash reaction videos, birthday morning POVs, and "wait for the candles" formats. This content is proven viral territory.
2. **UGC birthday contest.** A monthly "Barkday of the Month" contest where owners post their box unboxing with a branded hashtag; winners get a free month of meals.
3. **Creator seeding.** We send boxes to pet creators timed to their dogs' actual birthdays for authentic celebration content.
4. **Paid social boosting.** We amplify the best-performing UGC and creator posts rather than producing heavy brand assets.
5. **Email & CRM.** Birthday-date capture at signup, then an automated "Biscuit's birthday is coming up" send with one-tap box ordering.

---

## Target Audience

Existing Pawstead subscribers and social followers who celebrate pet birthdays. They already love their dogs like family, they post about them constantly, and a $25 box is an easy impulse add-on to their plan.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Every dog gets one day."** The Birthday Club makes Pawstead the brand that shows up on the most emotional day of the pet-parent calendar, with a box designed to be filmed from the first sniff to the last crumb.

---

## More Ways to Extend the Trend

- **Gotcha-day variant** of the box for rescue dogs
- **A birthday-song audio** for owners to use on their videos
- **Milestone badges** in the unboxing for puppy first birthdays and senior double-digits
