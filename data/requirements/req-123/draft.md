# Blue Fork Late-Night Hours Pilot Campaign — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Blue Fork Kitchen
**Campaign window:** August 1 – November 15, 2027
**Budget:** $310,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Fork After Dark" — the Late-Night Pilot**

## Opening

Introducing Fork After Dark, Blue Fork's extension into the late-night daypart. Twenty urban locations will stay open until 1 a.m. Thursday through Saturday, serving the full build-your-own bowl menu at the standard $12.90 average check, plus a compact late-night board of three signature bowls for speed. The pilot brings Blue Fork's fresh, customizable format to a time of day currently dominated by pizza, burgers, and gas-station snacks. Late-night dining is a growing occasion, and a fresh-food option stands out in it.

## Program Summary

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Locations | 20 urban stores near hospitals, campuses, and nightlife districts |
| Hours | Open until 1 a.m., Thu-Sat |
| Menu | Full menu + 3-bowl express board |
| Price | $12.90 average check |
| Ordering | In-store, app pickup, delivery apps |

## Target Audience

Night-shift workers and students near the 20 pilot locations. This includes hospital staff, service workers ending shifts, and college students studying or socializing late. They value speed, availability, and food that does not feel like a compromise at midnight.

## The Big Idea

**"Real food doesn't sleep."** The campaign positions Blue Fork as the first real meal available after 10 p.m. Creative contrasts sad late-night food cliches with fresh bowls, shot in the blue-lit calm of a late-night store.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social (Reels, TikTok) | Late-night creative flight geo-targeted around pilot stores |
| Delivery-app placements | Sponsored placement in late-night browsing windows |
| Campus & hospital-area out-of-home | Posters and transit ads near the 20 locations |
| App push, SMS & email | Announce hours to existing members near pilot stores |

Social media reaches this audience effectively during late hours, and delivery apps see significant late-night browsing. Out-of-home near hospitals and campuses reaches shift workers on their actual routes.

## KPIs

- Awareness of late-night hours near pilot stores
- Late-night transactions per store per week
- Delivery-app order volume in the 10 p.m. – 1 a.m. window
- App push opt-in engagement near pilot locations

## Why This Will Work

Late night is an underserved daypart, and Blue Fork's fresh format is genuinely differentiated in it. With targeted media around each pilot store and a clear "real food doesn't sleep" promise, the pilot should build a loyal late-night following and prove the case for a wider rollout.
