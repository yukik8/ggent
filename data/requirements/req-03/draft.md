# Campaign Proposal: "Charge Ahead" — Northtrail Commuter One Launch

**Client:** Northtrail Mobility
**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Version:** Draft v1
**Campaign window:** May 1, 2025 – September 30, 2025
**Total budget:** $650,000

---

## 1. Opening: Meet the Commuter One

Introducing the Northtrail Commuter One, the most complete commuter e-bike Northtrail has ever built. The Commuter One pairs a lightweight aluminum frame with a torque-sensing mid-drive motor, integrated lights, a rear rack rated for panniers, and Northtrail's signature quick-release battery. And that battery is the real star, because the Commuter One is the first Northtrail model designed from the ground up for the battery-swap subscription service. Riders never have to worry about charging at home, waiting around, or battery degradation. They just swap at a Northtrail station and keep riding.

The pricing is flexible too. Riders can buy the Commuter One outright for $1,899, or choose the $89/month subscription bundle that includes the bike and unlimited battery swaps. It is a genuinely exciting product, and this campaign is about bringing it to as many urban commuters as possible over the summer riding season.

## 2. Target Audience

Our audience is urban commuters aged 25-40 living in mid-size US cities with bike infrastructure. These are busy professionals who commute regularly, care about convenience and sustainability, and are open to alternatives to driving. Many young commuters are interested in e-bikes but find them expensive, so we believe the subscription option will appeal to them. This audience spends a lot of time on social media, and short videos perform especially well with this group, so digital channels will do a lot of the heavy lifting.

## 3. The Big Idea: "Charge Ahead"

"Charge Ahead" celebrates the feeling of gliding past traffic while everyone else is stuck. The creative centers on real commute moments, sunrise rides, the swap-and-go ritual at a battery station, arriving at work without the parking hunt. The campaign tagline is **"Swap the battery. Skip the traffic. Charge Ahead."**

The signature activation is the **Charge Ahead Demo Tour**, a traveling pop-up that sets up in five mid-size cities across the summer. Commuters can test-ride the Commuter One on a short urban loop, try the battery swap themselves, and sign up for the subscription on the spot with a first-month promo.

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Short-form video (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts) | Primary awareness driver. Social is very effective for this audience, and short-form video gets great engagement. |
| Creator partnerships | Local cycling, commuting, and lifestyle creators produce authentic ride-along content. Creator content feels more trustworthy than brand ads. |
| Charge Ahead Demo Tour (pop-ups) | Hands-on trial in five cities. Nothing sells an e-bike like riding one. |
| Local OOH & transit ads | Placements along commute corridors and near transit hubs to catch people mid-commute, when the pain of the current commute is most vivid. |
| Campaign landing page | Product configurator, subscription sign-up, demo tour schedule, and test-ride booking. |

The channels work together: video builds awareness, creators build trust, the demo tour converts interest into test rides, and the landing page captures sign-ups.

## 7. KPIs

- Reach and video views across short-form platforms
- Engagement rate on creator content
- Demo tour attendance and test rides completed
- Landing page traffic and configurator sessions
- Subscription sign-ups and bike sales attributed to the campaign
- Cost per acquisition trending downward through the flight

## 8. Why This Will Work

The Commuter One is a standout product with a feature set the competition cannot match, and the battery-swap subscription is a genuinely new story in the category. Young consumers love discovering products through social video and trusted creators, and summer is the natural season for bike consideration. By pairing broad awareness with real-world test rides, "Charge Ahead" gives commuters every reason, and every opportunity, to make the switch this summer.
