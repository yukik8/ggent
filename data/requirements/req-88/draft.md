**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft

---

## Campaign Title

**"Hartline Lands Here" — Two-Store Grand Opening**

---

## Opening: Grand Openings Are Back

Retail grand openings are having a real resurgence — the pop-up energy, the line-out-the-door content, the local-event feel that translates perfectly into short-form video. Hartline is opening two new stores this spring, and "Hartline Lands Here" wraps both openings in a full modern launch stack: doorbuster pricing, opening-weekend festival events, drone reveal content, local influencer walkthroughs, a giveaway wheel, radio remotes, and a hashtag moment for each market.

Opening-week doorbuster pricing anchors the whole program — deep, headline-grabbing offers that give every household within range a reason to make the first trip during week one.

---

## The Tactic Stack

### 1. Opening-Weekend Festival
Each store opens with a two-day festival: food trucks, kids' build stations, tool demos, hourly giveaways, and a ribbon-cutting with local officials. Event-style openings generate attendance and coverage at the same time.

### 2. Doorbuster Promotion Flight
The doorbuster offers run across flyers, paid social, local display, and radio for the two weeks around each opening. Aggressive opening pricing is the classic traffic driver, and it gives all the creative a hard hook.

### 3. Drone Reveal & Short-Form Content
A drone reveal film for each store, cut into Reels and TikToks, plus fast-turn content from the opening weekends themselves. Short-form is where local buzz builds now.

### 4. Local Influencer Walkthroughs
Area lifestyle and DIY creators get early-access walkthroughs and share first-look content, bringing local audiences with them.

### 5. Radio Remotes & Local PR
Live drive-time remotes from each opening weekend, plus press pushes to local news and community calendars.

### 6. Hashtag & Giveaway Wheel
Each market gets its own hashtag and an in-store prize wheel during opening month, keeping visits and posts flowing after the first weekend.

---

## Target Audience

Households within 15 miles of the two new stores. Homeowners and renters alike, anyone with a project list — the opening program is designed to get the broadest possible first wave through the doors during the doorbuster window.

---

## Why This Works

Openings are inherently newsworthy, doorbusters give people a concrete reason to come during week one, and the content stack turns each opening into a market-wide moment rather than a single-day event. Two stores means two waves of buzz, and learnings from the first opening sharpen the second.
