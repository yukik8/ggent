**Client:** Velura Skin

**Budget:** $380,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Nothing to Hide" — The Barrier Repair Relaunch**

## Opening

Velura is relaunching its hero product, the Barrier Repair Moisturizer, with a completely reworked formula, and it is genuinely a great product story. The new formula features a ceramide-dominant lipid complex, a shorter and cleaner ingredient list, a fragrance-free base that stays true to the brand's original promise, and a new recyclable 50ml jar at an accessible $32 price point. It absorbs faster than the previous version, layers well under makeup and SPF, and performs across skin types. This is the strongest product Velura has ever shipped, and this campaign is about making sure the market knows it.

Clean beauty is a fast-growing space and consumers are more interested in skincare than ever. Shoppers today care about what goes into their products, and Velura's reformulation gives us a lot of authentic material to work with. Our job is to turn this upgraded formula into renewed excitement around the brand and drive strong sales through the relaunch window.

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is skincare consumers roughly aged 18–35, mostly but not exclusively women, who are interested in clean beauty and take skincare seriously. Many of them research products online before buying and pay attention to ingredients. We will also reach a broader secondary audience of beauty-curious shoppers who may be encountering Velura for the first time, since a relaunch is a natural moment to grow awareness beyond the existing customer base.

Young consumers love brands that feel honest and modern, and social media is where they spend their time, so a social-led plan is the natural fit for this audience.

## Big Idea

**"Nothing to Hide."** We put the entire formula in the spotlight, literally. Every creative asset is built around radical openness about what is in the jar and why. The centerpiece is the **Open Formula Series**, a run of short videos in which Velura's formulator walks through the ingredient list line by line, explaining what each ingredient does and, just as importantly, what was removed and why. We pair this with a redesigned ingredient page on the Velura site, transparent packaging call-outs, and creator content that invites skincare enthusiasts to scrutinize the label themselves.

The idea works because it turns the reformulation itself into the campaign. We are not asking people to trust a claim, we are showing them the receipt.

## Channel & Media Plan

1. **Short-form video (TikTok + Instagram Reels)** — the core of the plan. Short-form video is very effective for beauty and performs especially well with younger audiences. We will run the Open Formula Series plus texture, application, and before/after content as paid and organic.
2. **Creator partnerships** — we will partner with a roster of skincare-focused creators, favoring credible niche voices over big celebrity names, since audiences tend to trust smaller creators more. Creators receive the full formula documentation and are free to give honest reviews.
3. **Ingredient transparency hub (owned web)** — a rebuilt product and ingredient page that hosts the full formula breakdown, formulator videos, and FAQ, serving as the landing destination for all paid traffic.
4. **CRM & email** — a relaunch announcement flow to the existing customer list, with a formula-comparison edition for past Barrier Repair buyers, plus a post-purchase education series to encourage repeat orders.
