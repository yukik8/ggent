Hi team,

Following up on our call last week — here's the written brief for the Barrier Repair relaunch. You've been with us since 2021 so I'll skip the brand history.

## The situation

We've spent fourteen months reformulating our hero product. The new Barrier Repair Moisturizer has a ceramide-dominant lipid complex, a shorter and cleaner ingredient list, the same fragrance-free promise, and a new recyclable 50ml jar. Price stays at $32 per 50ml jar. Our founder considers this the best formula we've ever shipped, and frankly she's right.

Clean beauty keeps growing and we want this relaunch to be the loudest moment in Velura's history. The formula is genuinely news, and we want the market to hear it.

## What we're asking for

A relaunch campaign that announces the new formula with real energy — social-first, creator-friendly, the kind of thing that gets screenshotted and shared. We want new customers discovering Velura through this, not just our list opening an email.

One thing I'll flag in passing, mostly so you have the full picture: our data team keeps reminding me we lose roughly 44% of first-time buyers before they ever order a second jar, which is painful for a moisturizer brand. Something to keep in mind, but the brief here is really about making the reformulation famous.

## Target

Skincare buyers under 30 who actually read ingredient lists — the transparency-obsessed crowd. They research everything before buying. Secondary: the broader clean-beauty-curious shopper.

## Budget & timeline

- **Budget:** $380,000
- **Window:** 2025-04-01 to 2025-07-15 — the new formula hits our warehouse in late March

## Success metric

We want **relaunch-period sales up 25% versus the prior quarter**, with meaningful growth in new-to-brand customers. If your plan can also nudge repeat behavior, wonderful, but sales lift is the headline number for my CEO.

## Deliverables

- Creative platform and campaign idea
- Channel plan with budget split
- Phased timeline within the window
- KPI framework and reporting cadence

## Process

No review this time — you're our agency and this is a direct engagement. That said, this relaunch has board visibility, so the proposal needs to stand up in front of people who don't know you like I do.

Can you have something to us within three weeks?

Thanks,
Nadia
