# Skillharbor Mobile App Launch Announcement — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Skillharbor
**Campaign window:** February 1 – April 30, 2028
**Budget:** $150,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Learning, Pocket-Sized"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Skillharbor mobile app: the full course library, offline downloads, streak tracking, and bite-size lesson mode, now on iOS and Android. Mobile-first learning is the moment — microlearning content is everywhere on TikTok, "learn something new in 60 seconds" formats keep going viral, and every major platform is racing to own the commute. Skillharbor finally has its seat at that table, and the launch should feel like a cultural event, not a changelog.

---

## Tactics

### 1. Launch-Week Social Blitz
A coordinated drop across TikTok, Reels, and Shorts: app-reveal edits, streak-flex content, "what I learned on my commute" formats, and a launch-day countdown. We ride microlearning trends as they surface and stitch popular study-tok creators.

### 2. Influencer Unboxing & Streak Challenges
Study and productivity creators show their first week in the app and challenge followers to a 7-day streak. Streak mechanics are inherently shareable and gamified content is peaking right now.

### 3. App Store Ads & ASO Push
Apple Search Ads and Google App Campaigns on learning keywords, plus a store-listing refresh with new screenshots and preview video.

### 4. Push, Email & In-App Announcement
Announcement flows to the full subscriber and lapsed-trial list with a download CTA and a streak-starter incentive.

---

## Target Audience

Existing subscribers and lapsed trial users — members who already know Skillharbor but haven't made it part of their daily routine, plus former trialists the app's lighter format might win back.
