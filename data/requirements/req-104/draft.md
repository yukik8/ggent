# Velura Travel-Size Minis Specialty-Retail Program — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Velura Skin
**Campaign window:** April 1 – June 30, 2028
**Budget:** $150,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Little Luxuries" — Velura Minis for Every Bag"**

---

## Opening: Small Jars, Big Love

Introducing Velura Minis, the most adorable addition to the lineup yet — beautifully miniaturized $12 versions of the brand's beloved heroes, sized for carry-ons, gym bags, and first tries. The minis come in irresistibly cute packaging that looks like a tiny jewel on the specialty retail shelf, and they arrive just in time for summer travel season.

Minis are wonderful for everyone: travelers get their routine on the go, curious shoppers get a low-risk way to fall in love with the brand, and gift-givers get a perfect little add-on. It is a delightful product with charm to spare, and shoppers are going to swoon.

---

## Target Audience

Trial-hesitant beauty shoppers and summer travelers under 35 — people who have been eyeing Velura but have not committed to a full-size jar, plus loyal fans who want their routine in the overhead bin. They browse specialty beauty stores for fun and love a checkout-counter treasure.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Little Luxuries"** celebrates the joy of tiny things. The creative is bright, collectible, and summery — minis lined up on hotel sinks, tucked into beach bags, peeking out of carry-ons — making the small jar feel like a small holiday.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. In-Store Displays & Checkout Placement
Charming counter displays and travel-themed shelf units at specialty retail partners, positioned for impulse pickup near checkout.

### 2. Paid Social
Sweet, colorful short-form video showing minis in travel moments, geotargeted around retail partner locations and travel hubs.

### 3. Creator Partnerships
Travel and skincare creators pack their minis for real trips and share the routine-on-the-go moment.

## Why This Will Work

The minis are simply delightful, and delight sells. The travel season gives us a natural moment, the checkout placement captures the impulse, and every mini in a stranger's bag is a tiny ambassador for the brand. "Little Luxuries" turns the smallest SKU into the friendliest front door Velura has ever had.
