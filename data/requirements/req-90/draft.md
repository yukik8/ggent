# Clearbrook Primary-Care Membership Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Clearbrook Health
**Campaign window:** September 1, 2025 – February 28, 2026
**Budget:** $510,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Your Doctor. Finally." — the Clearbrook Membership Launch**

## Opening

Clearbrook Membership is a new primary-care product priced at $79/month. Members get a named Clearbrook physician, same-week appointments booked online, unlimited messaging with their care team, an annual physical, and member pricing on urgent-care visits. It is primary care rebuilt as a simple subscription: one flat monthly price, no surprise bills, no phone calls.

The product is well designed and easy to explain, which makes it very marketable. Our campaign introduces the membership to adults under 45 across the Clearbrook footprint and drives sign-ups through a six-month launch flight ending February 28, 2026.

## Product Summary

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Price | $79/month, cancel anytime |
| Access | Same-week appointments, booked online |
| Care team | Named physician + unlimited messaging |
| Included | Annual physical, care planning |
| Extras | Member pricing on urgent-care visits |

## Target Audience

Adults under 45 without a primary-care physician. Many people in this group have not had a regular doctor since childhood and handle health issues as they come up. They are comfortable with subscriptions in every other part of life, and a flat monthly price is a familiar, low-friction way to buy. Messaging should feel practical and modern rather than clinical.

## The Big Idea

**"Your Doctor. Finally."** The campaign treats getting a doctor the way this audience treats every other useful subscription: a small monthly price that removes a recurring life problem. Creative shows real moments — the 2am symptom search, the form asking for a primary-care physician you don't have — resolved by one line: *You have a doctor for that now.*

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Launch films and member-story content; the main awareness driver |
| Paid search | Capture "doctor near me" and "primary care" demand and route it to the membership page |
| Podcast & streaming audio | Long-form explanation of how the membership works, in trusted shows |
| Out-of-home | Simple price-led boards near commuter routes and Clearbrook locations |
| Content production | Launch films, member stories, landing assets |
| Email & SMS | Convert existing urgent-care patients into members |

Social video is where this audience spends its time, and audio is a strong format for explaining a new model. Search captures active demand, out-of-home builds price familiarity, and the existing patient base is the warmest pool of prospective members.

## KPIs

- Membership sign-ups over the six-month flight
- Cost per sign-up trending down across the flight
- Strong traffic and conversion on the membership landing page
- Growing awareness of the membership among adults under 45 in the footprint
- Conversion rate of existing urgent-care patients into members

## Why This Will Work

The membership is a genuinely good product at a fair price, and the subscription framing fits how this audience already buys. The channel mix pairs broad awareness with strong capture of active demand, and the new-year window lands the message exactly when people resolve to finally sort out their health. With clean creative and disciplined measurement, "Your Doctor. Finally." can make Clearbrook Membership the default answer for anyone in the footprint who doesn't have a doctor.
