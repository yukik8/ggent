# Sunbrook Crisps-and-Dip Pairing Bundle Summer Promo — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Sunbrook Foods
**Campaign window:** May 1 – August 15, 2028
**Budget:** $240,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Dip Season" — the Sunbrook Crisps-and-Dip Bundle**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Sunbrook crisps-and-dip pairing bundle, a $6.99 shrink-banded combo that pairs a full-size bag of Sunbrook Crisps with a co-branded dip cup. The bundle comes in three pairings (Classic + French Onion, Bold Batch + Queso, Ridged + Ranch) and merchandises as a single grab-and-go unit for cookouts and picnics. Summer is the peak season for shared snacking, and the bundle makes the pairing decision for the shopper.

---

## Target Audience

| Segment | Description |
|---|---|
| Primary | Cookout and picnic hosts aged 25-44 |
| Secondary | Impulse shoppers browsing the snack aisle before weekends |

Hosts want easy wins for gatherings, and a pre-paired bundle removes a decision. Social content around summer entertaining performs well with this group.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Dip Season"** declares summer the season of the pairing. Every asset shows the bundle landing at a cookout — the pool table, the park blanket, the backyard spread — with the tagline "One grab. Both covered."

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Summer entertaining content and pairing reveals on Reels and TikTok |
| Grocery retail media | Sponsored placements against dip, chip, and cookout searches |
| In-store displays & sampling | Front-of-store bundle displays with weekend sampling in priority markets |
| Creator partnerships | Grilling and entertaining creators feature the bundle in cookout content |

Social builds the occasion association, retail media captures weekend planners, displays win the impulse grab, and creators make it feel like a real summer staple.

## Why This Will Work

The bundle solves a real shopper moment — grabbing something for a gathering without thinking twice. The pairing format is distinctive on shelf, the price is friendly, and summer gives us a steady run of occasions from Memorial Day to back-to-school. "Dip Season" keeps Sunbrook at the center of the summer table.
