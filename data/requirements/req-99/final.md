## Campaign Title

**"Dip Season" — the Sunbrook Crisps-and-Dip Bundle**

## Opening

Introducing the Sunbrook crisps-and-dip pairing bundle: a $6.99 shrink-banded combo pairing a full-size bag of Sunbrook Crisps with a co-branded dip cup. Three pairings (Classic + French Onion, Bold Batch + Queso, Ridged + Ranch) merchandise as a single grab-and-go unit for cookouts and picnics. Summer is the peak season for shared snacking, and the bundle makes the pairing decision for the shopper before they reach the aisle.

## Target Audience

| Segment | Description |
|---|---|
| Primary | Cookout and picnic hosts aged 25-44 |
| Secondary | Impulse shoppers browsing the snack aisle before weekends |

Hosts want easy wins for gatherings, and a pre-paired bundle removes a decision at a busy moment. Summer entertaining content performs reliably with this audience on social platforms.

## The Big Idea

**"Dip Season"** declares summer the season of the pairing. Every asset shows the bundle arriving at a cookout — the pool table, the park blanket, the backyard spread — under one line: "One grab. Both covered."

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Summer entertaining content and pairing reveals on Reels and TikTok |
| Grocery retail media | Sponsored placements against dip, chip, and cookout searches |
| In-store displays & sampling | Front-of-store bundle displays with weekend sampling in priority markets |
| Creator partnerships | Grilling and entertaining creators feature the bundle in cookout content |

Social builds the occasion association, retail media captures weekend planners, displays win the impulse grab, and creator content keeps the bundle feeling like a genuine summer staple rather than a promotion.

## KPIs

- Bundle unit velocity at participating retailers
- Engagement rates on pairing content
- Display compliance and sell-through in priority markets
- Awareness of the bundle among summer hosts

## Why This Will Work

The bundle solves a real shopper moment: grabbing something for a gathering without a second thought. The pairing format is distinctive on shelf, the price point is friendly, and the summer calendar provides a steady run of occasions from Memorial Day through back-to-school. With displays winning the impulse grab and media surrounding the weekend plan, "Dip Season" keeps Sunbrook at the center of the summer table.
