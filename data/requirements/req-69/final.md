## Campaign Title

**"First 14" — Make the Trial Count**

## Opening

Skillharbor's 14-day free trial is the front door to the $199/month all-access subscription, and it is a genuinely generous offer: full library access from day one, live session drop-ins, and immediate community entry. The product experience inside the trial is strong — stronger, frankly, than most trial users ever discover. What the trial needs is a structured push that turns browsing into momentum during the first two weeks, so more users reach the moment where keeping the subscription feels like the obvious call.

"First 14" is a lifecycle program built around the trial window itself. Rather than spending to acquire more trials, this campaign works the trials Skillharbor already generates, guiding each user to a first meaningful win inside the platform before the paywall decision arrives. It is the highest-leverage $240,000 in the plan year: the audience is already in the building.

## Target Audience

Trial users in their first 14 days. They have already raised their hands, but many sign up, browse the catalog once, and drift away before forming a habit. Industry benchmarks consistently suggest that most trial users who eventually convert make up their minds within the first week, so the campaign treats day 0 through day 14 as a structured journey rather than a waiting period — with the heaviest guidance up front.

## The Big Idea

**"First 14" — two weeks, one real win.**

Every trial user is guided toward completing one meaningful milestone inside the trial: a finished first module, a built dashboard, a graded practice project. The messaging counts down the trial as a challenge with a payoff, so conversion day arrives with proof of progress rather than an expiring clock. It is a simple mechanic, easy to execute well, and it reframes the paywall from a toll to a threshold.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Lifecycle email & push program | Day-by-day guidance sequence from onboarding to conversion offer |
| In-app onboarding content series | Milestone checklist, progress nudges, and "first win" content modules inside the product |
| Paid retargeting (social & display) | Reaches trial users off-platform with milestone reminders and social proof |
| SMS program | Time-sensitive nudges at key drop-off moments for opted-in users |
| Creative production | A refreshed onboarding asset kit shared across all lifecycle touchpoints |

The channel mix stays deliberately close to the user. Email and push carry the structured journey; in-app content removes friction at the exact moment of use; retargeting recaptures users who drift; SMS covers the highest-urgency moments for those who opt in. Lifecycle messaging of this kind is typically far cheaper per conversion than new acquisition, which is what makes a dedicated trial-window program worth funding — and worth funding first.

## KPIs

- Trial activation rate (users completing a first milestone within 7 days)
- Day-14 trial-to-paid conversion rate at the $199/month tier
- Email and push engagement across the sequence
- Retargeting re-entry rate for drifted trial users
- Month-two retention of converted subscribers

## Why This Will Work

The cheapest subscriber Skillharbor can win is the one already inside the trial. "First 14" gives every trial user a guided path to a real win, and users who experience real progress see the subscription as an investment rather than a cost. Because the journey is instrumented week by week, the program compounds: every cohort teaches us where users stall, and every fix lifts the next cohort's conversion. The plan builds on the onboarding assets and tracking already in place from our previous work together, so the program can be live for October trial cohorts without a standing start.
