## Campaign Title

**"The First Stack Challenge" — 90 Days to Your First Real Savings**

---

## Opening: The Most Exciting Thing Stackwise Has Ever Shipped

Introducing the First Stack Challenge, an absolutely brilliant new way to experience everything that makes Stackwise special. The challenge is a guided 90-day program built right into the app: users pick a goal (the default is a $500 "first stack"), Stackwise auto-moves small, painless amounts into a locked savings bucket, and a gorgeous progress screen fills up week by week with milestone celebrations along the way. It's simple, it's satisfying, and it shows off the app's best features — auto-save rules, savings buckets, and the weekly progress recap — in one irresistible package.

The free tier gets the full challenge experience, and the $8/month premium tier adds custom buckets and advanced rules, which makes the challenge an amazing front door to the whole Stackwise ecosystem. This is the kind of product moment that campaigns dream of, and our launch plan will make it huge.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Your first $500 is the hardest. Let's make it the easiest."**

We turn saving — normally a private, slightly embarrassing struggle — into a shared, celebratory challenge. Everything in the campaign radiates warmth and momentum: real progress screens, real milestone confetti, real people hitting their first stack. It's aspirational but achievable, and it's incredibly shareable.

---

## Target Audience

First-job earners aged 20-27 who don't have a savings habit yet. They're earning real paychecks for the first time, they know they *should* be saving, and they feel guilty that they aren't. They spend tons of time on TikTok and Reels, they love authentic content from relatable creators, and they're super skeptical of anything that feels like a lecture or a get-rich-quick scheme. The challenge meets them exactly where they are: no judgment, tiny steps, visible progress.

---

## Channel Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
TikTok and Reels are absolutely central. We produce a stunning series of challenge spots — real progress screens filling up, milestone celebrations, "day 90" reveal moments — and run them as a sustained paid flight across the whole window. Short-form video is incredibly effective with this audience and perfect for showing the app in action.

### 2. Creator Partnerships — "My First Stack" Series
We partner with a fantastic roster of relatable creators (not finance gurus — normal people with first jobs) who take the challenge on camera across the 90 days. Followers watch their stacks grow in real time, which is wonderfully authentic proof that the challenge works. Creator content gets amplified through paid.

### 3. Challenge Hub & Landing Experience
A beautiful challenge hub explains the mechanic in seconds, shows live community stats (total saved across all participants — a wonderfully motivating number), and routes straight to download. All paid and creator traffic lands here.

### 4. Lifecycle Email & Push
Once users join, a warm, encouraging lifecycle program keeps them moving: milestone celebrations, gentle nudges when a week goes quiet, and a big "you did it" moment at day 90 with a premium trial offer attached.
