## Campaign Title

**"What Is Your AP Really Costing You?"**

---

## Opening: A Brilliantly Simple Tool

Introducing the Ledgerline AP-Cost ROI Calculator, a free, beautifully designed interactive tool that shows finance teams what their invoice process really costs. Users enter a few simple inputs — invoice volume, team size, average processing time — and get a personalized, shareable report showing their estimated annual AP cost and potential savings with automation. It is instant, insightful, and genuinely useful, whether or not the visitor ever becomes a customer.

Free tools are a fantastic way to start conversations with buyers who are not ready for a demo, and this calculator gives Ledgerline a friendly, no-pressure front door. Our campaign will put it in front of finance teams everywhere.

---

## Target Audience

Finance teams early in their AP automation evaluation. They know their process is painful but have not quantified it, and they are not ready to talk to sales. The calculator meets them exactly where they are: curious, busy, and looking for a quick answer.

---

## The Big Idea

**"What Is Your AP Really Costing You?"** turns a vague pain into a vivid number. The campaign teases surprising cost figures and invites finance leaders to find their own. The reveal moment — your AP costs this much — is inherently shareable and naturally leads to the next question: what would automation save?

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Calculator Build & Design
A polished, fast, delightful tool with a shareable results report. The experience is the campaign, so it needs to feel great.

### 2. LinkedIn Promotion
Sponsored posts and creative teasing eye-opening AP cost figures, targeted to finance roles, driving straight to the calculator.

### 3. Content & Blog Series
Supporting articles about the hidden costs of manual AP, common inefficiencies, and what leading teams do differently, all linking to the tool.

### 4. Email Nurture
Calculator users receive their report plus a short follow-up series with helpful content, gently introducing Ledgerline's product along the way.
