## Campaign Title

**"In Your Corner"**

---

## Opening: The Problem We Are Actually Solving

Skillharbor's most engaged subscribers hit a wall the course library cannot break: they finish learning and still cannot see the path to a paycheck. 66% of career changers aged 25-40 say they cannot identify which skills would actually raise their salary (ref-12), and the completion data shows what happens without structure and accountability — completion rates average 12% for self-paced online courses versus 68% for cohort-based programs (ref-12). The subscribers stuck between courses and job applications are churn risks precisely because the product's guidance ends where their job search begins.

The 1:1 Career Coach add-on, at $79/month with biweekly sessions, async resume feedback, mock interviews, and a personalized roadmap, is our answer to that specific problem: it puts the accountability and skill-to-salary map into the subscription itself. Every tactic below is selected to convert stuck subscribers into retained, upgraded ones.

---

## Target Audience

Active Skillharbor subscribers stuck between finishing courses and landing a new role — the exact segment where the guidance gap and the accountability gap intersect, and where an add-on solves a measured problem rather than adding a feature.

---

## The Big Idea

**"In Your Corner"** positions the coach as the person who's got your back through the messy middle of a career change. Because the core problem is that members cannot connect skills to salary on their own, every coaching-moment story ends on a concrete outcome — a title, a number, an offer letter. Ads featuring a specific salary-outcome statistic drive 2.1x higher free-trial starts than generic skill-building ads (ref-12), and the same specificity discipline governs this creative.

---

## Channel Plan

Each channel maps back to the stuck-subscriber problem named above.

### 1. Lifecycle Email & In-App Messaging
Because the audience is already inside the product, lifecycle is the primary channel, and the benchmarks justify the weight: lifecycle-triggered emails average a 38% open rate, versus 21% for standard campaign sends (ref-16). In-app prompts fire at the exact stuck moments — course completion, repeat catalog browsing — where the coach is the obvious next step. Median ROI for retention marketing is $4.60 per dollar spent, versus $2.20 for prospecting (ref-16), which is why this program leads with the existing base.

### 2. Paid Social (Reels & TikTok)
Short-form coaching-moment videos retarget active subscribers and lookalikes, each ending on a specific member outcome rather than a generic glow-up, per the 2.1x outcome-specificity finding (ref-12).

### 3. Alumni & Creator Video Stories
Beta members who worked with coaches share their journeys — proof that guided members finish and land roles, directly answering the 12% versus 68% completion gap (ref-12).

### 4. Live Webinars
Monthly "Meet the Coaches" sessions function as the diagnosis step: attendees leave with a first cut of their own skill-to-salary map, then convert on the spot.
