# Skillharbor 1:1 Career-Coach Add-On Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Skillharbor
**Campaign window:** January 15 – May 15, 2028
**Budget:** $320,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"In Your Corner"**

---

## Opening: A Game-Changing New Offering

Introducing the Skillharbor 1:1 Career Coach — an incredible new add-on that pairs every subscriber with a dedicated, hand-picked career coach for just $79/month. Members get biweekly video sessions, unlimited async resume and portfolio feedback, mock interviews, and a personalized job-search roadmap. It's the warmest, most human product Skillharbor has ever launched, and it transforms the platform from a course library into a true career partner.

Career coaching is booming, and subscribers are hungry for guidance. This launch is Skillharbor's chance to deepen its relationship with its most engaged members and give them the amazing support they deserve.

---

## Target Audience

Active Skillharbor subscribers who are stuck between finishing courses and actually landing a new role. They've put in the work, they're motivated, and they spend real time on the platform — they just need that extra push and personal touch to cross the finish line.

---

## The Big Idea

**"In Your Corner"** positions the coach as the person who's got your back through the messy middle of a career change. Warm, personal, encouraging creative built around real coach-and-member moments: the pep talk before an interview, the resume that finally clicks, the offer-letter celebration.

---

## Channel Plan

### 1. Lifecycle Email & In-App Messaging
Beautifully designed announcement and nurture flows to the subscriber base, plus in-app prompts at natural moments like course completion.

### 2. Paid Social (Reels & TikTok)
Heartwarming short-form videos of coaching moments and member glow-ups, retargeting active subscribers and lookalikes.

### 3. Alumni & Creator Video Stories
Real members who worked with coaches during the beta share their journeys — authentic, emotional stories that build trust.

### 4. Live Webinars
Monthly "Meet the Coaches" sessions where prospects can experience the coaching vibe firsthand and sign up on the spot.
