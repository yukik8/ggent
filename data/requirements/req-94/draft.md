## Campaign Title

**"The Clinic Down the Hall" — Clearbrook Employer Partnerships**

## Opening

B2B marketing is in the middle of a full reinvention, and this campaign uses everything that is working right now. LinkedIn video is exploding, dark-social sharing is where B2B decisions actually get made, thought-leadership podcasts are the new trade press, and AI-personalized outbound is finally good enough to feel human. Clearbrook's employer on-site clinic offering — a staffed clinic room at the workplace, custom-contracted per employer — gives us a genuinely differentiated product to run this modern playbook on.

## Tactic Stack

### 1. LinkedIn Video & Lead Gen
Short, punchy video is outperforming everything on LinkedIn right now. We produce a series of walk-through films of an operating on-site clinic, CFO-angle explainers on absenteeism costs, and quick-hit myth-busting clips, backed by lead-gen forms and retargeting.

### 2. Executive Thought-Leadership Podcast Tour
We book Clearbrook's chief medical officer onto the HR and benefits podcast circuit — the shows benefits leaders actually listen to on commutes — with a repeatable narrative about the future of workplace care.

### 3. Webinar & Content Series
A monthly webinar series ("What On-Site Care Actually Costs," "The Post-Renewal Playbook") plus downloadable ROI one-pagers and case studies, all gated for pipeline capture and remixed into dark-social-friendly clips.

### 4. HR Trade Events & Conference Presence
A booth-plus-speaking strategy at the two biggest regional HR and benefits conferences in the window, with a live "clinic room" demo build people can walk through — extremely shareable, extremely concrete.

### 5. ABM Display & Retargeting
Account-based display against a programmatically built list of regional employers, sequenced to warm accounts before outbound and event invitations land.

## Target Audience

HR leaders at 500+ employee regional employers — benefits directors, CHROs, and the CFOs who sign off. They are drowning in benefits-vendor pitches, skeptical of healthcare promises, and moved by peer proof and hard cost numbers. We also target benefits brokers and consultants as a secondary influence audience, since they shape many employer decisions.

## The Big Idea

**"The Clinic Down the Hall."** Health benefits are abstract until the clinic is physically in the building. Every asset makes the offering concrete: real square footage, real staffing, real employees skipping a half-day off for a ten-minute visit. Concreteness is the differentiator in a category full of vague wellness promises.
