# Velura Packaging Refresh Announcement Campaign — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Velura Skin
**Campaign window:** April 1 – May 31, 2027
**Budget:** $160,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Same Formula, New Face" — the Velura Packaging Refresh Announcement**

---

## 1. Opening: What Is Changing

Velura is rolling out a complete packaging refresh across the line, led by the $32 Barrier Repair Moisturizer hero SKU. The new system introduces a frosted-glass jar with a recyclable mono-material lid, a cleaner label layout that moves the full ingredient list to the front panel, and a new color-coding system that distinguishes each franchise at a glance. It is a significant upgrade in both sustainability credentials and shelf presence.

The most important fact of this program is what is NOT changing: the formulas. Nothing inside the jar is different, and there is no price change. Our campaign exists to announce the new look, explain the improvements, and make absolutely sure every customer understands the product itself is identical. Skincare consumers can be sensitive about changes to products they rely on, so clear and repeated communication of "same formula" is essential.

A summary of the refresh:

| Element | Old | New |
|---|---|---|
| Jar | Standard plastic jar | Frosted glass, recyclable mono-material lid |
| Label | Ingredient list on back panel | Full ingredient list on front panel |
| Line navigation | Uniform look across SKUs | Color-coded by franchise |
| Formula | Current formula | **Unchanged** |
| Price | $32 hero SKU | **Unchanged** |

---

## 2. Target Audience

| Segment | Description | Role in Campaign |
|---|---|---|
| Existing customers | Active buyers and subscribers of Velura products | Reassure and inform; prevent confusion at reorder |
| Lapsed buyers | Past purchasers who have not reordered recently | Use the refresh news as a natural reason to re-engage |

Existing customers are the priority. Many loyal skincare buyers notice packaging changes quickly and can worry that a formula changed too, so proactive communication protects the relationship. Lapsed buyers are the secondary opportunity, since a fresh look gives us a timely and non-discount reason to appear in their inbox and feeds again.

---

## 3. The Big Idea

**"Same Formula, New Face."** We treat the refresh the way Velura treats ingredients: with full transparency. The campaign centers on a side-by-side storytelling device, old jar next to new jar, with a clear annotated breakdown of exactly what changed and exactly what did not. Every asset repeats the same reassurance hierarchy: (1) formula unchanged, (2) price unchanged, (3) here is what improved and why. For lapsed buyers, the message flips into an invitation: "A lot has changed on the outside. Come see what hasn't."

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

| # | Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Email & CRM | Primary announcement vehicle to owned audiences |
| 2 | Organic + paid social (Instagram, TikTok) | Visual reveal and reassurance content |
| 3 | Creator unboxings | Third-party validation of the new packaging |
| 4 | On-site & packaging inserts | Transition messaging at point of purchase and delivery |

### 4.1 Email & CRM
A three-part announcement flow to the full customer file: a teaser, the reveal with the annotated side-by-side, and a follow-up FAQ email addressing formula and price questions directly. Lapsed buyers receive a tailored version framed as a re-introduction to the brand. Email is the right lead channel because these are known audiences and email performs well for announcement-style messages.

### 4.2 Organic + Paid Social
A reveal-day content moment across Instagram and TikTok, followed by a paid flight retargeting site visitors, past purchasers, and lookalikes of lapsed buyers. Short video is a strong format for packaging content because the tactile, unbox-and-rotate visual style works very well on these platforms.

### 4.3 Creator Unboxings
A small group of skincare creators receive the new packaging early with a transparency brief: show the old and new jars together and read the (unchanged) ingredient list on camera. Creator content tends to feel more credible to skincare audiences than brand-only messaging.

### 4.4 On-Site & Packaging Inserts
A banner and FAQ module on velura.com, plus a printed insert in every order shipped during the transition window explaining the refresh. This catches customers at the exact moment they encounter the new jar, which is where confusion would otherwise happen.

## 7. KPIs

- Strong open and click rates on the announcement email flow
- Healthy engagement on reveal content across Instagram and TikTok
- Meaningful re-engagement from the lapsed-buyer segment
- Low volume of confused or concerned customer-service inquiries about the formula
- Stable reorder behavior among existing customers through the transition

---

## 8. Closing

This is a focused, well-organized program that does one job cleanly: it announces the new packaging while protecting the trust Velura has built with its customers. The transparent side-by-side approach fits how Velura's audience already evaluates the brand, and the refresh gives us a genuine, non-promotional reason to reach lapsed buyers. We recommend proceeding on the April 1 start to align with the first shipments of new packaging.
