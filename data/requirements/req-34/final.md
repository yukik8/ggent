## Campaign Title

**"The AP Glow-Up" — Ledgerline Essentials Launch**

---

## Opening

Introducing Ledgerline Essentials, the new entry tier that brings Ledgerline's accounts-payable automation platform down-market for the first time. Essentials packs the core Ledgerline experience — invoice capture, approval routing, payment scheduling, and a clean audit trail — into a self-serve product at a $6,000 annual price point. There is no implementation team, no six-week onboarding, and no procurement gauntlet: customers sign up, connect their accounting software, and invoices start flowing the same week.

This is a significant moment for the Ledgerline brand. The SMB software space is energetic right now — AI copilots, self-serve motions, and product-led growth have made small businesses more open than ever to adopting new tools, and the vendors who show up with modern, personality-forward marketing are the ones winning attention. Essentials gives Ledgerline the product to meet that moment; this campaign gives it the launch energy to match.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The AP Glow-Up"** — the before-and-after transformation story, applied to the messiest corner of small-business life: the accounts-payable shoebox.

Glow-up content is one of the most reliably engaging formats online, and it maps naturally to what Essentials does. Before: a desk buried in paper invoices, a late-payment email, a Sunday spent in spreadsheets. After: a clear queue, an approval done from a phone, a Friday that ends at five. Every asset in the campaign is a before/after pair, every channel gets its own flavor of the transformation, and the whole platform travels under a single tag: **#APGlowUp**.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

We recommend a stacked, always-on mix built around the channels where B2B attention is genuinely shifting.

### 1. LinkedIn Thought Leadership & Paid
B2B buyers increasingly trust people over logos. We will build a content engine around Ledgerline's leadership and selected customer-facing staff — glow-up stories, sharp takes on manual AP, and short native video — with paid amplification behind the best-performing posts to keep the engine compounding.

### 2. B2B Podcast & Newsletter Sponsorships
We will sponsor a slate of small-business, bookkeeping, and finance-ops podcasts and newsletters with host-read spots and native placements, all carrying the glow-up framing and a dedicated offer code so each channel feels personal rather than programmatic.

### 3. Finance Creator Partnerships
Accounting creators on YouTube, TikTok, and LinkedIn have built devoted audiences of exactly the practitioners we want to reach. We will commission a roster of creators to film their own AP glow-ups inside Essentials — real screens, real workflows, real reactions — and syndicate the strongest cuts as paid media.

### 4. "Glow-Up Live" Webinar Series
Monthly live sessions in which a working bookkeeper cleans up a messy AP process in Essentials, start to finish, in under an hour. Live product content converts because it is proof rather than promise, and each session becomes an evergreen asset we gate for lead capture and retarget against.

### 5. Paid Search & Retargeting
Always-on coverage of high-intent AP and bookkeeping-software search terms, plus retargeting pools built from every channel above, so that no one who touches the glow-up story is lost before reaching the trial page.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is owners and bookkeepers at firms under 50 employees — the people actually opening the invoice inbox. Many still run AP on spreadsheets, email threads, and paper; industry surveys suggest small-business owners spend upwards of ten hours a week on manual financial admin. They are busy, practical, and allergic to enterprise sales processes, which is precisely why the self-serve Essentials motion fits them. Our secondary audience is outsourced bookkeeping firms that manage AP for multiple small clients and can become multi-account champions.
