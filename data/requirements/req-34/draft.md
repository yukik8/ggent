## Campaign Title

**"The AP Glow-Up" — Ledgerline Essentials Launch**

---

## Opening: Ledgerline Goes Small (In a Big Way)

Introducing Ledgerline Essentials, the brand-new entry tier that brings Ledgerline's accounts-payable automation platform down-market for the first time. Essentials packs the core Ledgerline experience — invoice capture, approval routing, payment scheduling, and a clean audit trail — into a self-serve product at a $6,000 annual price point. No implementation team, no six-week onboarding, no procurement gauntlet. Sign up, connect your accounting software, and invoices start flowing the same week.

This is a huge moment for the Ledgerline brand. The SMB software space is absolutely buzzing right now — AI copilots, self-serve motions, product-led growth, community-led everything. Small businesses are more open than ever to adopting new tools, and the vendors who show up with modern, personality-forward marketing are the ones winning attention. Essentials gives Ledgerline the product to ride that wave, and this campaign gives it the launch energy to match.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The AP Glow-Up"** — the before-and-after transformation story, applied to the messiest corner of small-business life: the accounts-payable shoebox.

Glow-up content is one of the most reliably engaging formats on the internet, and it maps perfectly to what Essentials actually does. Before: a desk buried in paper invoices, a late-payment email, a Sunday spent in spreadsheets. After: a clear queue, an approval done from a phone, a Friday that ends at five. Every asset in the campaign is a before/after pair, every channel gets its own flavor of the transformation, and the whole thing hashtags neatly as #APGlowUp.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

We recommend a stacked, always-on mix that meets the moment across the channels where B2B attention is actually shifting.

### 1. LinkedIn Thought Leadership & Paid
Founder-led and employee-led content is having a real moment on LinkedIn, and B2B buyers increasingly trust people over logos. We will build a content engine around Ledgerline's leadership and a few charismatic customer-facing staff, posting glow-up stories, hot takes on manual AP, and short native video. Paid amplification behind the best-performing posts keeps the engine compounding.

### 2. B2B Podcast & Newsletter Sponsorships
Podcasts and niche newsletters are where trust lives now. We will sponsor a slate of small-business, bookkeeping, and finance-ops shows and newsletters with host-read spots and native placements, all carrying the glow-up framing and a dedicated offer code so the channel feels personal rather than programmatic.

### 3. Finance Creator Partnerships
The "finfluencer" wave has fully arrived in the bookkeeping world. Accounting creators on YouTube, TikTok, and LinkedIn have built devoted audiences of exactly the practitioners we want. We will commission a roster of creators to film their own AP glow-ups inside Essentials — real screens, real workflows, real reactions — and syndicate the best cuts as paid media.

### 4. "Glow-Up Live" Webinar Series
Monthly live sessions where a real bookkeeper walks through cleaning up a messy AP process in Essentials, start to finish, in under an hour. Live product content converts because it is proof, not promise, and each session becomes an evergreen asset we can gate for lead capture and retarget against.

### 5. Paid Search & Retargeting
Always-on search coverage on high-intent AP and bookkeeping-software terms, plus retargeting pools built from every channel above, so nobody who touches the glow-up story gets lost before they hit the trial page.

---

## Target Audience

Primary: owners and bookkeepers at firms under 50 employees — the people actually opening the invoice inbox. Many of them are still running AP on spreadsheets, email threads, and paper, and lots of small-business owners say admin work is one of the most draining parts of running their company. They are busy, practical, and allergic to enterprise sales processes, which is exactly why the self-serve Essentials motion fits them. Secondary: outsourced bookkeeping firms who manage AP for multiple small clients and can become multi-account champions.
