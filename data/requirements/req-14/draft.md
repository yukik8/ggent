# Campaign Proposal: "See Your Numbers Differently" — Ledgerline Analytics Add-On Upsell Campaign

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Ledgerline
**Product:** Ledgerline Analytics Add-On Module
**Campaign window:** June 1, 2026 – October 31, 2026
**Total budget:** $220,000

---

## 1. Opening: The Product

Ledgerline is proud to introduce the Analytics Add-On Module, the most powerful expansion to the Ledgerline platform yet. Built directly on top of the AP automation suite that customers already know and trust, the Analytics module transforms raw payables data into beautiful, real-time dashboards. Key features include:

- **Live spend dashboards** that update as invoices flow through the platform, with no exports or spreadsheets required.
- **Vendor intelligence views** that surface payment terms, discount capture, and vendor concentration at a glance.
- **Cash-flow forecasting** powered by the customer's own historical AP data.
- **One-click board-ready reports** with customizable branding and scheduled delivery.
- **Zero-setup deployment** — because the module sits inside the existing Ledgerline environment, it can be switched on for any account in minutes.

At $6,000 per year per account, the Analytics Add-On is a natural next step for every Ledgerline customer. Finance leaders love data, and this module gives them more of it, presented more elegantly than anything they could build in-house. Our campaign will make sure every existing customer knows about the module and understands how impressive it is.

## 2. Target Audience

Our audience is the existing Ledgerline customer base — specifically the CFOs and finance directors who hold budget authority at these companies. More broadly, we also want to reach the wider finance community around them: controllers, AP managers, FP&A analysts, and the finance-adjacent operations leaders who influence purchasing conversations. Finance decision-makers in general are increasingly data-driven, and analytics is a topic that resonates with almost everyone in the modern finance function. By speaking to the whole finance audience, we build a halo of awareness that supports the direct upsell conversations.

## 3. The Big Idea: "See Your Numbers Differently"

The creative platform is **"See Your Numbers Differently."** Every asset in the campaign contrasts the old way of seeing payables data (static, delayed, spreadsheet-bound) with the new way (live, visual, effortless). The hero visual is a split-screen: a cluttered spreadsheet dissolving into a clean Ledgerline dashboard. It is a simple, elegant idea that shows off the product's strongest attribute — how good the dashboards actually look — and it works consistently across email, webinar, social, and in-product placements.

The campaign centerpiece is a **live "Analytics Showcase" webinar series**, where the Ledgerline product team demos the module on realistic data and takes questions. Webinars are very effective for B2B software audiences, and finance professionals in particular enjoy seeing tools in action before committing.

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| **Email nurture to customer base** | Primary direct channel. A five-touch sequence introducing the module's features, sent to CFO and finance director contacts. Email remains one of the most reliable ways to reach business audiences. |
| **In-product messaging** | Banners and guided tooltips inside the Ledgerline app highlighting the new dashboards. Customers already logging in daily are a captive audience. |
| **LinkedIn paid + organic** | Sponsored posts and thought-leadership content about the power of finance analytics. LinkedIn is where finance leaders spend their professional time, and social is very effective for B2B awareness. |
| **Analytics Showcase webinar series** | Monthly live demos (June–October) with Q&A, promoted through email and LinkedIn. |
| **Customer success enablement** | Playbooks, one-pagers, and demo scripts so CSMs can pitch the module in every quarterly business review. |

## 7. KPIs

- **Awareness:** the large majority of the active customer base has seen at least one campaign touchpoint by end of campaign.
- **Engagement:** strong open and click rates on the email sequence, healthy webinar registration and attendance.
- **Pipeline:** a meaningful number of demo requests and CS-sourced upsell conversations generated per month.
- **Conversion:** a solid uptick in Analytics Add-On attach rate across the customer base by October 31.
- **Revenue:** incremental annual recurring revenue from module sales that comfortably exceeds campaign spend.

## 8. Why This Will Work

The Analytics Add-On is a genuinely impressive product, and impressive products sell when people see them. This campaign puts the module's best feature — its visual polish — front and center, reaches finance audiences where they already are, and equips the customer success team to close. Analytics is a growing priority for finance teams everywhere, and Ledgerline is well positioned to capture that enthusiasm within its own customer base.

We recommend proceeding to creative development immediately to hit the June 1 launch date.
