# Blue Fork Kids Menu Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Blue Fork Kitchen
**Campaign window:** May 1 – August 31, 2028
**Budget:** $230,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Little Forks" — the Blue Fork Kids Menu Launch**

## Opening

Introducing Little Forks, Blue Fork's first-ever kids menu: a $6.90 build-your-own kids bowl with pick-three toppings, a fruit side, and a collectible topper toy. Kid-friendly menus are having a major moment across fast casual right now, and family content is one of the hottest verticals on TikTok and Reels. Little Forks lets Blue Fork ride both waves at once with a product that is genuinely fun, healthy-ish, and built to be filmed.

## The Tactics

### 1. TikTok Family Trend Flights
We jump on trending family formats — "ordering for my kid," lunchbox reveals, picky-eater challenges — with rapid-response creative refreshed every two weeks so we are always on the current sound.

### 2. AR "Build Your Bowl" Filter
A branded AR filter where kids stack virtual toppings. Filters are huge for organic reach and give families a reason to create content for us.

### 3. Collectible Topper Series
Six collectible bowl toppers rotating monthly. Collectibles drive repeat visits and unboxing-style content, which performs incredibly well right now.

### 4. Family Creator Partnerships
Parenting and family-vlog creators film real visits and taste tests. Kid reaction videos are among the most shared food content on every platform.

### 5. In-Store Family Weekends
Saturday "Little Forks Days" with activity sheets, stickers, and a mascot appearance at flagship suburban stores.

### 6. Gamified App Stickers
Kids' orders earn digital sticker packs in the parent's app, adding a collection layer that mirrors the physical toppers.

## Target Audience

Primary: suburban families with kids aged 4-12, especially millennial parents who already eat fast casual themselves and want an option the whole table can agree on. Secondary: grandparents and caregivers handling weekend meals.

## Media Plan Summary

Paid social carries the trend flights and creator amplification, geo-focused on suburban trade areas. The AR filter and sticker mechanics run as organic layers. In-store weekends anchor the family occasion at flagship locations.

## Why This Will Work

Little Forks stacks the tactics that are working right now — trends, collectibles, AR, creators, and in-store moments — into one high-energy launch. Families will see it everywhere, kids will want the toppers, and Blue Fork becomes the fast-casual table where everyone wins.
