## Campaign Title

**"Little Forks" — the Blue Fork Kids Menu Launch**

## Opening

Introducing Little Forks, Blue Fork's first kids menu: a $6.90 build-your-own kids bowl with pick-three toppings, a fruit side, and a collectible topper toy. Kid-friendly menus are gaining momentum across fast casual, and family content remains one of the strongest-performing verticals on TikTok and Reels. Little Forks gives Blue Fork a product that is genuinely fun, wholesome, and made to be shared on camera — and it opens the family table to a brand that has historically skewed toward solo lunch occasions.

## The Tactics

### 1. TikTok Family Trend Flights
We participate in proven family formats — "ordering for my kid," lunchbox reveals, picky-eater challenges — with creative refreshed every two weeks to stay current without chasing every passing sound.

### 2. AR "Build Your Bowl" Filter
A branded AR filter where kids stack virtual toppings, giving families an easy, playful reason to create content around the launch.

### 3. Collectible Topper Series
Six collectible bowl toppers rotating monthly. Collectibles encourage repeat visits and generate natural unboxing-style content.

### 4. Family Creator Partnerships
Parenting and family-vlog creators film real visits and taste tests. Authentic kid reactions consistently outperform polished brand food content.

### 5. In-Store Family Weekends
Saturday "Little Forks Days" with activity sheets, stickers, and a mascot appearance at flagship suburban stores.

### 6. Gamified App Stickers
Kids' orders earn digital sticker packs in the parent's app, mirroring the physical topper collection and keeping the family occasion connected to Blue Fork's digital ordering.

## Target Audience

Primary: suburban families with kids aged 4-12, especially millennial parents who already eat fast casual themselves and want an option the whole table can agree on. Industry reporting consistently shows that kids heavily influence where families choose to eat out, so winning the child wins the table. Secondary: grandparents and caregivers handling weekend meals.

## Media Plan Summary

Paid social carries the trend flights and creator amplification, geo-focused on suburban trade areas around participating stores. The AR filter and sticker mechanics run as organic layers on top of the paid plan. In-store weekends anchor the family occasion at flagship locations and give local teams a repeatable event format.

## KPIs

- Reach and engagement on family trend content and AR filter usage
- Kids bowl attach rate on family orders
- Repeat visits tied to topper collection
- Little Forks Day turnout at flagship stores

## Why This Will Work

Little Forks combines a well-designed product with the tactics currently performing best for family brands — trend content, collectibles, creators, and in-store moments — in one coordinated launch. Families will encounter it across every touchpoint, kids will want the toppers, and Blue Fork earns a durable new occasion at the family dinner table.
