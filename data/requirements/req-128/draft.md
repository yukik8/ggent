# Kinetiq Annual Membership Prepay Promotion — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Kinetiq Home
**Campaign window:** November 1 – December 31, 2028
**Budget:** $240,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Lock In Your Year" — the Annual Prepay Promotion**

## Opening: The Best Deal Kinetiq Has Ever Offered

Introducing the Kinetiq Annual Pass, an absolutely fantastic prepay offer for the members who love Kinetiq most. For $399 upfront — versus $39 a month — tenured members lock in a full year of live and on-demand classes at a beautiful savings, plus an exclusive members-only welcome kit with a premium water bottle, seat cushion, and limited annual badge in the app. It is a genuinely irresistible deal, perfectly timed for the holiday season when members are already thinking about their new year.

Annual prepay is a win for everyone. Members save meaningfully and feel celebrated for their loyalty, and Kinetiq starts the new year with committed, energized members who have every reason to keep rowing.

## Target Audience

Active monthly members with six or more months of tenure. These are Kinetiq's true fans — they row regularly, they love the instructors, they show up for challenges, and they are already planning to stay. They just need a nudge, a reward, and a moment to make it official. The holidays are exactly that moment.

## The Big Idea

**"Lock In Your Year."** The creative celebrates commitment as a gift to yourself — a warm, confident, end-of-year message that says: you showed up all year, now make next year yours. Instructors deliver the message personally in class and in video, making the offer feel like it comes from the community, not a billing department.

## Channel Plan

### 1. In-App & Email Promotion
The offer lives where members already are: in-app banners, a dedicated offer screen, and a warm email series from favorite instructors.

### 2. Paid Social Retargeting to Members
Cozy, celebratory retargeting creative keeps the offer visible through the busy holiday weeks.

### 3. Direct Mail to Tenured Members
A beautiful printed piece — offer card plus a year-in-review of the member's own stats — lands in early November. Physical mail feels special and personal in a way email cannot.

### 4. Incentive Fulfillment
The welcome kit ships within a week of purchase, turning the prepay into an unboxing moment members will share.

## KPIs

- Strong prepay conversion among eligible tenured members

## Why Kinetiq Wins

Lock In Your Year rewards the members who already love Kinetiq with a deal that feels generous and a moment that feels personal. The timing is perfect, the creative is warm, and the offer practically sells itself to an audience that is already sold on the product.
