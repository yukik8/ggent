## Campaign Title

**"Lock In Your Year" — the Annual Prepay Promotion**

## Opening

Introducing the Kinetiq Annual Pass, a prepay offer built for the members who use Kinetiq most. For $399 upfront — versus $39 a month — tenured members lock in a full year of live and on-demand classes at a meaningful saving, plus a members-only welcome kit with a premium water bottle, seat cushion, and a limited annual badge in the app. The offer is timed for the holiday season, when members are already thinking about the year ahead.

Annual prepay works for both sides. Members save and feel recognized for their loyalty, and Kinetiq begins the new year with committed members and predictable membership revenue. Industry reporting generally finds that prepaid annual plans improve 12-month retention relative to month-to-month billing.

## Target Audience

Active monthly members with six or more months of tenure. These members row regularly, know the instructors, and are already likely to stay; the offer gives them a concrete reason to commit now rather than drift month to month. The holiday window provides a natural decision moment.

## The Big Idea

**"Lock In Your Year."** The creative frames commitment as a gift to yourself — a warm, end-of-year message: you showed up all year, now make next year yours. Instructors deliver the message personally in class and on video, so the offer feels like it comes from the community rather than a billing department.

## Channel Plan

### 1. In-App & Email Promotion
The offer lives where members already are: in-app banners, a dedicated offer screen, and an email series fronted by favorite instructors.

### 2. Paid Social Retargeting to Members
Celebratory retargeting creative keeps the offer visible through the busy holiday weeks without expanding beyond the member audience.

### 3. Direct Mail to Tenured Members
A printed piece — the offer card plus a year-in-review of the member's own workout stats — lands in early November. Physical mail stands out during the holiday inbox crush and suits a considered, year-long commitment.

### 4. Incentive Fulfillment
The welcome kit ships within a week of purchase, turning the prepay into a small unboxing moment members are likely to share.

## KPIs

- Prepay conversion rate among eligible tenured members
- Engagement with instructor offer videos and the email series
- Direct mail response rate against the in-app baseline
- Member sentiment around the offer and welcome kit

## Why Kinetiq Wins

Lock In Your Year rewards the members who already love Kinetiq with a deal that feels generous and a moment that feels personal. The audience is warm, the mechanics are simple, and the two-month window is long enough to convert without discount fatigue. It is a low-risk program with a clear payoff: a committed member base heading into the new year.
