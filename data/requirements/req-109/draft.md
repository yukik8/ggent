**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft

**Budget:** $160,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Streak Season"**

---

## Opening

Introducing Streak Season, a gamified ride-streak feature drop for the Northtrail companion app. Riders build daily and weekly ride streaks, unlock badges and tiers, climb city leaderboards, and earn real perks along the way. Streaks are the stickiest mechanic in consumer apps right now — fitness apps, language apps, games — and it is time the Northtrail app got its own. The feature is free, and it creates a natural upsell moment for the $39/month battery-swap membership as riders rack up miles.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Streak Season"** turns spring and summer riding into a game everyone is already playing. Keep the streak alive, flex the badge, beat your neighborhood. Every ride becomes content, and every streak becomes a reason to open the app.

---

## Tactic Stack

### 1. In-App & Push Campaign
Streak reminders, milestone celebrations, near-miss saves ("ride 1 mile today to keep your 12-day streak"), and tier-up moments. Push is free reach and streak mechanics practically write the notifications themselves.

### 2. Social Video & Meme Content
Streak-flex content built for sharing: milestone screenshots, leaderboard drama, "day 47" energy. We lean into current meme formats and keep the content calendar fast and reactive.

### 3. Creator Duet Challenge
Riders and creators duet their streak milestones. We seed the challenge with cycling and commuter creators, then amplify the best community entries. User-generated momentum is the whole game here.

### 4. Streak Rewards & Partner Perks
Local coffee shops, bike shops, and gear brands supply streak rewards — free coffee at day 7, service discount at day 30, gear drops for top streaks. Partners get exposure, riders get real stuff, the app gets opens.

---

## Target Audience

Existing Northtrail riders who use the companion app. They already ride; we want them riding more often, opening the app daily, and sliding toward the battery-swap membership as their mileage climbs. Leaderboards give competitive riders a hook, while casual riders chase badges and perks.

## Why This Wins

Streaks work. Every sticky app of the last decade proves it, and Northtrail riders are exactly the kind of habit-driven audience streaks were made for. Streak Season gives riders a daily reason to ride, a weekly reason to share, and a season-long reason to stay inside the Northtrail ecosystem.
