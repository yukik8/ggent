# Northtrail Certified-Refurbished Outlet Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Northtrail Mobility
**Campaign window:** August 1 – November 30, 2028
**Budget:** $260,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Northtrail Renewed" — the Certified-Refurbished Outlet Launch**

---

## Opening

Introducing Northtrail Renewed, the official online outlet for certified-refurbished Commuter One e-bikes at $1,299. Every Renewed bike is inspected, serviced, and re-certified by Northtrail technicians, then sold with the same app integration and battery-swap eligibility as a new bike. Refurbished e-commerce is a growing space, and Renewed lets Northtrail capture value-focused shoppers with an official, quality-assured offer.

| Product Facts | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product | Certified-refurbished Commuter One |
| Price | $1,299 (vs $1,899 new) |
| Channel | Dedicated online outlet storefront |
| Includes | Inspection, service, re-certification, app + swap eligibility |

---

## Target Audience

Price-sensitive first-time e-bike buyers aged 22-35. They want an e-bike but consider a new one out of reach. They shop online, compare prices carefully, and respond to clear value framing.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Northtrail Renewed"** frames refurbished not as second-hand but as second-life: the same commuter machine, officially restored, at a price that finally works. The storefront and creative emphasize the certification process and the savings.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Value-framing creative introducing Renewed and the $1,299 price |
| Paid search & shopping | Capture "cheap e-bike", "refurbished e-bike", and brand searches |
| Retargeting & display | Re-engage storefront visitors who did not purchase |
| Email / CRM to waitlist | Announce launch to Northtrail's list and price-drop waitlist |
| Landing page & production | Storefront landing experience and creative assets |

Social introduces the offer, search captures existing demand, retargeting closes considered shoppers, and email activates the warmest audience first.

## Why This Will Work

The offer is strong and the structure is clean: a clear product, a clear price, and a channel plan where each layer has a defined role. Value-focused shoppers are actively looking for exactly this kind of official refurbished program, and Renewed gives them a trustworthy way to buy.
