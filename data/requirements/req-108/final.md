## Campaign Title

**"Northtrail Renewed" — the Certified-Refurbished Outlet Launch**

## Opening

Introducing Northtrail Renewed, the official online outlet for certified-refurbished Commuter One e-bikes at $1,299. Every Renewed bike is inspected, serviced, and re-certified by Northtrail technicians, then sold with the same app integration and battery-swap eligibility as a new bike. Industry reporting suggests refurbished e-commerce has been growing at double-digit rates for several years, and Renewed positions Northtrail to capture value-focused shoppers with an official, quality-assured offer rather than ceding them to the used market.

| Product Facts | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product | Certified-refurbished Commuter One |
| Price | $1,299 (vs $1,899 new) |
| Channel | Dedicated online outlet storefront |
| Includes | Inspection, service, re-certification, app + swap eligibility |

## Target Audience

Price-sensitive first-time e-bike buyers aged 22-35. They want an e-bike but see a new one as out of reach. They shop online, compare prices carefully, and respond to clear, honest value framing. Renewed speaks to them in exactly those terms.

## The Big Idea

**"Northtrail Renewed"** frames refurbished not as second-hand but as second-life: the same commuter machine, officially restored, at a price that finally works. The storefront and creative put the certification process and the $600 savings front and center, so the offer feels like a smart buy, not a compromise.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social — short-form video | Value-framing creative introducing Renewed and the $1,299 price |
| Paid search & shopping | Capture "cheap e-bike", "refurbished e-bike", and brand searches |
| Retargeting & display | Re-engage storefront visitors who did not purchase |
| Email / CRM to waitlist | Announce launch to Northtrail's list and price-drop waitlist |
| Landing page & production | Storefront landing experience and creative assets |

Each layer has a defined job: social introduces the offer, search captures shoppers already hunting for a deal, retargeting closes considered visitors, and email activates the warmest audience first. The sequencing keeps acquisition costs disciplined across the flight.

## KPIs

- Storefront traffic and conversion rate
- Cost per acquisition by paid channel
- Waitlist email open and click rates
- Refurbished units sold during the campaign window

## Why This Will Work

The offer is genuinely strong and the structure is clean: a clear product, a clear price, and a channel plan where every layer has a defined role and a measurable job. Value-focused shoppers are actively looking for exactly this kind of official refurbished program, and Renewed gives them a trustworthy, brand-backed way to buy. With disciplined media sequencing and a storefront built to convert, this launch establishes Renewed as a durable second revenue line for Northtrail.
