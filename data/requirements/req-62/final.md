## Campaign Title

**"Renewed & Ready" — the Kinetiq Certified-Refurbished Launch**

---

## Opening

Introducing Kinetiq Certified Refurbished, a new storefront where trial returns and trade-ins get a second life. Every Renewed rower is inspected against a 40-point checklist, fitted with a fresh seat and strap, backed by the same one-year warranty as a new machine, and priced at $1,195 — a full $600 below new. It is the same machine, the same classes, and the same quiet ride, at a price that opens the brand to a much wider audience.

The timing is right. Value-conscious shopping continues to grow, refurbished has shed its old stigma thanks to phones and laptops, and sustainability-minded buyers feel good about giving hardware a second home. Certified Refurbished lets Kinetiq welcome shoppers who always wanted in but could not justify the new-unit price.

---

## Target Audience

Price-sensitive home-fitness shoppers — people who have admired connected rowers but bounce off the four-figure price tag. They compare carefully, hunt for deals, browse resale marketplaces, and would welcome an official option that removes the risk of buying secondhand from a stranger. Secondary: bargain-savvy gift shoppers ahead of the holidays, and past trial returners who liked the machine but not the price.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Renewed & Ready."** Same rower, same sweat, smaller price. The creative presents the refurbished machine as the smart shopper's badge of honor — macro shots of the inspection process, the 40-point checklist as a design element, and real owners sharing what they did with the $600 they saved. Refurbished is not the consolation prize; it is the savvy choice.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Short-form video across Reels, TikTok, and Shorts tells the inspection story and the price story together. Deal-hunting audiences respond well to "here's how I got it for less" content, and the checklist material is naturally satisfying to watch.

### 2. Paid Search & Shopping
Shoppers actively searching "rower deals," "refurbished rowing machine," and Kinetiq brand terms are the warmest traffic available, so we will capture them with search and shopping placements pointing straight at the storefront.

### 3. Email & CRM
Kinetiq's list includes many people who browsed but never bought — a natural audience for a $1,195 reveal. We will run a launch announcement, a price-drop story series, and holiday-timed reminders.

## Why Kinetiq Wins

Certified Refurbished opens Kinetiq's doors to shoppers the new-unit price has kept out, and "Renewed & Ready" makes choosing refurbished feel proud rather than like settling. With a clean storefront, an honest inspection story, and a price that finally says yes, this launch can turn Kinetiq's returns pipeline into a genuine growth channel.
