# Kinetiq Certified-Refurbished Storefront Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Kinetiq Home
**Campaign window:** August 1 – November 30, 2026
**Budget:** $270,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Renewed & Ready" — the Kinetiq Certified-Refurbished Launch**

---

## Opening: A Beautiful Second Life

Introducing Kinetiq Certified Refurbished, a wonderful new storefront where trial returns and trade-ins get a meticulous second life. Every Renewed rower is inspected across a rigorous 40-point checklist, fitted with a fresh seat and strap, backed by the same one-year warranty as a new machine, and priced at a truly compelling $1,195 — a full $600 below new. It is the same gorgeous machine, the same beloved classes, the same whisper-quiet ride, just gently pre-loved and dramatically more affordable.

This is such an exciting moment for Kinetiq. Value-conscious shopping is bigger than ever, refurbished has completely shed its old stigma thanks to phones and laptops, and sustainability-minded buyers actively feel good about giving hardware a second home. Certified Refurbished lets Kinetiq welcome a whole new audience who always wanted in but could never quite justify the price.

---

## Target Audience

Price-sensitive home-fitness shoppers — people who have admired connected rowers from a distance but bounce off the four-figure price tag. They compare carefully, hunt for deals, browse resale marketplaces, and would leap at an official option that removes the risk of buying secondhand from a stranger. Secondary: bargain-savvy gift shoppers ahead of the holidays, and past trial returners who loved the machine but not the price.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Renewed & Ready."** Same rower, same sweat, smaller price. The creative celebrates the refurbished machine as the smart shopper's badge of honor — beautiful macro shots of the inspection process, the 40-point checklist as a design element, and real owners sharing what they did with the $600 they saved. Refurbished is not the consolation prize; it is the savvy choice.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Short-form video across Reels, TikTok, and Shorts is a fantastic way to tell the inspection story and the price story together. Deal-hunting audiences love a good "here's how I got it for less" video, and the checklist content is naturally satisfying to watch.

### 2. Paid Search & Shopping
Shoppers actively searching "rower deals," "refurbished rowing machine," and Kinetiq brand terms are the warmest possible traffic, so we will capture them with search and shopping placements pointing straight at the storefront.

### 3. Email & CRM
Kinetiq's list is full of people who browsed but never bought — the perfect audience for a $1,195 reveal. We will run a launch announcement, a price-drop story series, and holiday-timed reminders.
