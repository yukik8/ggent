# Skillharbor Course Catalog Refresh Announcement — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Skillharbor
**Campaign window:** February 1 – March 31, 2027
**Budget:** $130,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The New Wave" — Skillharbor's Biggest Catalog Refresh Ever**

---

## Opening: So Much New to Love

Introducing the most exciting catalog refresh in Skillharbor history! This spring, the platform welcomes over forty brand-new and fully rebuilt courses: gorgeous new AI and data tracks, refreshed design and marketing programs, updated career-services content, and a beautiful new catalog experience that makes browsing feel effortless and inspiring. Everything looks better, flows better, and teaches better, and every bit of it is already included in the $199/month all-access subscription our members love.

This is a moment worth celebrating loudly. Existing subscribers get a wave of fresh value, and lapsed or hesitant members on the email list get the perfect reason to take another look. "The New Wave" makes the refresh feel like an event — energetic, colorful, and impossible to ignore.

---

## Target Audience

Existing Skillharbor subscribers and the email list. These are people who already know and like the platform — active members who will be delighted by the new content, and past trialists or lapsed subscribers who might come back for the right reason. They are friendly audiences, and this campaign is about giving them something wonderful to get excited about.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The New Wave"** treats the catalog refresh like a season premiere. A bold announcement moment, a stream of daily reveals showing off the most eye-catching new courses, and a celebratory tone that reminds everyone how alive the platform is. Every asset ends with the same warm invitation: it is all waiting in your membership.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Email Announcement Series
A festive multi-part email program: a big reveal email, category spotlight follow-ups, and a personal "picked for you" send highlighting new courses based on each member's history. The email list is our most direct line to both audiences, so it carries the heart of the campaign.

### 2. In-App & Site Takeover
The platform itself gets dressed up for the occasion — a homepage takeover, new-course badges, an interactive "what's new" tour, and celebratory banners throughout the member experience.

### 3. Paid Social Announcement Flight
A bright, fast-moving flight of announcement videos and new-course showcases on Reels, TikTok, and Shorts, aimed at our own follower base and retargeting pools. The energy of the creative carries the news to lapsed members where they already scroll.

### 4. Production & Design
A joyful new visual system for the refresh: catalog photography, motion graphics for the reveal series, and a template kit that keeps every touchpoint feeling like one big celebration.
