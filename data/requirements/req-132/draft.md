---

## Campaign Title

**"Upskill Your Bench"**

---

## Opening

Introducing Skillharbor Teams, a new plan that brings the full Skillharbor catalog to small businesses at $149 per seat per month. Teams includes an admin dashboard, seat management, progress reporting, curated learning paths by role, and priority support. It is a natural extension of the consumer product into a B2B motion, and small businesses are an underserved segment for structured upskilling.

The plan is simple to buy, simple to roll out, and priced for companies without an L&D department. Our campaign introduces Teams to owners and HR leads and drives them into a self-serve trial or a demo call.

## Target Audience

Owners and HR leads at 10-100 employee companies. These buyers wear multiple hats, have limited time to evaluate software, and respond to clear pricing and low-friction trials. Training budgets exist at these companies but are often unspent or spent ad hoc.

## The Big Idea

**"Upskill Your Bench"** frames Teams as the fastest way for a small company to grow its own talent instead of hiring it. The creative shows real small-business roles leveling up: the office manager who becomes the ops lead, the support rep who becomes the analyst.

## Channel Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn & paid social | Reach owners and HR leads with role-based creative and case snapshots |
| Paid search | Capture "team training software" and "employee upskilling" intent |
| Webinar & content program | Monthly "Build Your Bench" sessions plus downloadable rollout guides |
| Email & outbound nurture | Sequenced follow-up for trial signups and webinar attendees |

## KPIs

- Trial signups and demo requests from target-size companies
- Webinar registrations and attendance
- Cost per qualified lead trending down across the flight
- Seat activations within 30 days of account creation

## Why This Will Work

Teams is a strong product at a clear price point, and the small-business segment is underserved by enterprise-oriented competitors. With LinkedIn, search, webinars, and nurture working as one funnel, we will put Teams in front of the right buyers and give them an easy path from interest to seats.
