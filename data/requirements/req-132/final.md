## Campaign Title

**"Upskill Your Bench"**

## Opening

Skillharbor Teams brings the full Skillharbor catalog to small businesses at $149 per seat per month. The plan includes an admin dashboard, seat management, progress reporting, curated learning paths by role, and priority support — everything a company without an L&D department needs to run structured upskilling, and nothing it has to configure. It is a natural extension of a consumer product small-business employees already know into a clean B2B motion.

Teams is simple to buy, simple to roll out, and priced for the segment. Our campaign introduces it to owners and HR leads and moves them into a self-serve trial or a demo call with minimal friction.

## Target Audience

Owners and HR leads at 10-100 employee companies. These buyers wear multiple hats, have little time to evaluate software, and respond to transparent pricing and low-friction trials. Industry reporting suggests a majority of small businesses find it easier to grow existing staff into new roles than to hire specialized talent outright, and training budgets at these companies often go unspent or get spent ad hoc.

## The Big Idea

**"Upskill Your Bench"** frames Teams as the fastest way for a small company to grow its own talent instead of hiring it. The creative shows real small-business roles leveling up: the office manager who becomes the ops lead, the support rep who becomes the analyst. Every asset ends on the same practical promise — seats today, a stronger bench next quarter.

## Channel Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn & paid social | Reach owners and HR leads with role-based creative and case snapshots |
| Paid search | Capture "team training software" and "employee upskilling" intent |
| Webinar & content program | Monthly "Build Your Bench" sessions plus downloadable rollout guides |
| Email & outbound nurture | Sequenced follow-up for trial signups and webinar attendees |

The four channels operate as one funnel: paid builds awareness and captures intent, the webinar and guide program qualifies interest, and nurture converts it into activated seats.

## KPIs

- Trial signups and demo requests from target-size companies
- Webinar registrations and attendance
- Cost per qualified lead trending down across the flight
- Seat activations within 30 days of account creation

## Why This Will Work

Teams is a strong product at a clear price point, and the small-business segment is underserved by enterprise-oriented competitors. With LinkedIn, search, webinars, and nurture working as one funnel, we will put Teams in front of the right buyers, make the value easy to grasp, and give them a short, friction-free path from first click to activated seats.
