# Blue Fork "Strong Season" High-Protein Line Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Blue Fork Kitchen
**Campaign window:** January 15 – April 30, 2028
**Budget:** $420,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Strong Season" — the High-Protein Line Launch**

---

## Opening: The Boldest Menu Move Blue Fork Has Ever Made

Introducing the Blue Fork High-Protein Line, an absolutely game-changing set of three performance bowls — the Power Grain, the Smoky Chicken Quinoa, and the Double Steak Verde — each packing 40+ grams of protein with full macro counts printed right on the menu board. At $14.50, these beautiful, hearty, Instagram-ready bowls give fitness-minded guests everything they want: real protein, real flavor, and zero guilt. The bowls look incredible, taste incredible, and arrive exactly when New Year fitness energy is at its peak.

Health and fitness culture is bigger than ever, and protein is the macro everyone is talking about. This launch is Blue Fork's chance to own the post-workout meal and become the go-to spot for everyone chasing their goals this year.

---

## Target Audience

Fitness-minded fast-casual diners aged 18-34. These are gym-goers, runners, and wellness-curious young professionals who care about what they eat and love sharing their routines. They are super active on social media, follow fitness creators religiously, and are always looking for food that fits their lifestyle. If we show up in their feeds with gorgeous, protein-packed bowls, they will come.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Strong Season"** positions January through April as Blue Fork's season of strength. Every touchpoint celebrates showing up for yourself — at the gym, at work, and at lunch. The creative pairs energetic training moments with slow, delicious bowl beauty shots, and invites guests to make Blue Fork part of their routine, not just a one-time treat.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Reels & TikTok)
Short-form video is where our audience lives. A heavy flight of energetic, appetizing bowl content and training-day storytelling will build massive awareness fast.

### 2. Fitness Creator Partnerships
We will partner with fitness and food creators who genuinely resonate with their followers. Their honest "what I eat in a day" style content is exactly the format this line was made for.

### 3. Loyalty App & CRM Promotion
App pushes, emails, and a "Strong Season" bonus-points streak will introduce the line to existing members and keep them coming back through the flight.

### 4. In-Store & Gym Partnerships
Menu-board takeovers, macro cards at the register, and sampling partnerships with boutique gyms and run clubs in priority metros bring the line to people mid-routine.
