# Clearbrook Flu-Season Readiness Campaign — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Clearbrook Health
**Campaign window:** September 15 – December 15, 2026
**Budget:** $190,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Beat the Season" — Clearbrook Flu Readiness**

## Opening

Clearbrook's flu-season offer is simple and strong: a $45 flu shot, free for members, available at every location with walk-in and online-booked slots, evenings and weekends included. The shot takes ten minutes, the booking takes one, and members pay nothing. It is an easy product to communicate and an easy product to say yes to.

This campaign gets the offer in front of families and older adults across the clinic footprint before and during peak flu season, and gives Clearbrook a visible, useful role in the community's autumn routine.

## Offer Summary

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Price | $45 flat; free for members |
| Access | Walk-in or booked online, evenings and weekends |
| Locations | All Clearbrook clinics |
| Time | About ten minutes end to end |
| Extras | Family blocks — book the whole household in one slot |

## Target Audience

Families and older adults in the Clearbrook clinic footprint. Parents want the household handled in one trip with minimal disruption, and older adults want a trusted, easy, nearby option. Both groups respond to clear pricing, convenient hours, and reminders timed to the season. Messaging should be practical and warm rather than clinical or alarming.

## The Big Idea

**"Beat the Season."** Flu season arrives every year on schedule, so getting ahead of it should be as routine as changing the clocks. The creative frames the flu shot as the season's easiest errand: one stop, ten minutes, the whole family covered. Family-block booking is the hero feature, and the $45 price (free for members) appears in every asset.

## Channel & Media Plan

| Channel | Role |
|---|---|
| Paid social & local video | Seasonal reminders and family-block explainers to parents and adult children of older patients |
| Local radio & streaming audio | Reach older adults and commuters with schedule and price messaging |
| Out-of-home | Boards and transit near clinics and pharmacy corridors with price and hours |
| Email & SMS | Season-opening announcement and booking reminders to the patient list |
| Content production | Video, audio spots, OOH and social assets |

Radio and OOH carry the older-adult reach, social carries families, and the patient list is the warmest audience for early bookings. All channels drive to the booking flow.

## Timeline (2026-09-15 to 2026-12-15)

| Phase | Dates | Activity |
|---|---|---|
| Season opening | Sep 15 – Oct 15 | Full launch across all channels, early-bird push to patient list |
| Peak demand | Oct 16 – Nov 20 | Sustained flight, family-block emphasis, reminder sequences |
| Late-season sweep | Nov 21 – Dec 15 | "Not too late" messaging, walk-in emphasis, retargeting |

## KPIs

- Flu-shot volume across the flight versus last season
- Share of shots booked online versus walk-in
- Family-block bookings as a share of appointments
- Email and SMS booking conversion from the patient list
- Member flu-shot uptake (free-shot redemption)

## Why This Will Work

The offer is clear, the price is right, and the campaign meets each audience in the channel it actually uses. Flu season is a predictable demand spike, and a clean, well-timed reminder campaign with an easy booking path is exactly the tool to capture it. "Beat the Season" makes Clearbrook the obvious, easiest answer to an errand everyone already knows they should run.
