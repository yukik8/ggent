## Campaign Title

**"Go Big with Sunbrook" — the Club-Store Value Multipack Entry**

## Opening

Introducing the Sunbrook value multipack: a 10-count club pack that brings the Sunbrook Crisps lineup to warehouse club stores for the first time. At $9.49 per pack it is a strong everyday value, and the pack design is built to read clearly on a club-store pallet. This is a significant step for the brand — Sunbrook entering the largest baskets in retail with a format made for them.

Club shoppers respond well to trusted brands in bulk formats, and Sunbrook's familiar Midwest personality fits the treasure-hunt character of the club aisle. Industry coverage indicates club-channel foot traffic has grown steadily over the past several years, which makes the timing favorable.

## Target Audience

Bulk-buying households shopping warehouse club stores — larger families, entertainers, and value-driven planners who stock the pantry monthly. They are high-basket shoppers who reward brands that deliver clear value and consistent quality.

## The Big Idea

**"Go Big with Sunbrook"** celebrates abundance. The creative shows stocked pantries and full snack tables, anchored by the multipack. One message throughout: your favorite crisp, now at club scale.

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. In-Club Demo Events
A roadshow-style sampling program across priority club locations, with trained demo teams offering the full flavor lineup. The club demo is a proven, well-liked format, and it puts the product directly in shoppers' hands at the point of decision.

### 2. Club Retail Media
Sponsored placements on club retailers' digital properties, keeping the multipack visible when members plan trips and shop online.

### 3. Paid Social
Short-form video and feed placements geotargeted around priority club locations, presenting the multipack and the "go big" occasion.

### 4. Out-of-Home Near Clubs
Billboards and transit placements on key routes to priority club locations, timed to launch weeks.

- Multipack velocity at club locations against retailer targets

## Why This Will Work

Sunbrook arrives in the club channel with a purpose-built product at a compelling price. The demo roadshow puts it directly into shoppers' hands, retail media and social surround the club trip from planning to parking lot, and our team has run this exact style of in-store event program successfully for Sunbrook before. The plan is practical, tightly scheduled, and built to execute cleanly from day one.
