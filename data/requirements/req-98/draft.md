# Sunbrook Club-Store Channel Entry — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Sunbrook Foods
**Campaign window:** January 15 – April 30, 2028
**Budget:** $260,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Go Big with Sunbrook" — the Club-Store Value Multipack Entry**

---

## Opening: Sunbrook Goes Big

Introducing the Sunbrook value multipack, a gorgeous, generous 10-count club pack that brings the beloved Sunbrook Crisps lineup to warehouse club stores for the very first time. At $9.49 per pack, it is an irresistible everyday value, and the bright, cheerful pack design looks absolutely stunning stacked on a club-store pallet. This is a landmark moment for the brand — Sunbrook stepping confidently into the biggest baskets in retail.

Club shoppers love discovering trusted brands in bulk formats, and Sunbrook's warm, familiar Midwest personality is a perfect fit for the treasure-hunt feel of the club aisle. The opportunity is enormous and exciting.

---

## Target Audience

Bulk-buying households shopping warehouse club stores — larger families, entertainers, and value-driven planners who stock the pantry monthly. They are loyal, high-basket shoppers who reward brands that show up with real value and real quality.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Go Big with Sunbrook"** celebrates abundance. The creative shows overflowing pantries, packed snack tables, and happy houses full of people, all anchored by the unmistakable multipack. Everything says: your favorite crisp, finally at club scale.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. In-Club Demo Events
A wonderful roadshow-style sampling program across priority club locations, with warm, high-energy demo teams offering the full flavor lineup. Tasting is believing, and the club demo is a proven format shoppers genuinely enjoy.

### 2. Club Retail Media
Sponsored placements on club retailers' digital properties, keeping the multipack front and center when members plan their trips and shop online.

### 3. Paid Social
Bright, joyful short-form video and feed placements geotargeted around club locations, showing the multipack and the "go big" lifestyle.

### 4. Out-of-Home Near Clubs
Eye-catching billboards and transit placements on key routes to priority club locations, timed to launch weeks.

## Why This Will Work

Sunbrook is a brand people already love, and the club channel is where love turns into loyalty at scale. The multipack is a fantastic product at a fantastic price, the demo roadshow puts it directly into shoppers' hands, and the media plan surrounds the club trip from planning to parking lot. "Go Big with Sunbrook" will make the brand's club debut feel like an event — because it is one.
