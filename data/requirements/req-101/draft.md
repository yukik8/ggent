# Velura Overnight Repair Mask Launch — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Velura Skin
**Campaign window:** September 15 – December 15, 2027
**Budget:** $400,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"While You Sleep" — the Overnight Repair Mask Launch**

---

## Opening: Beauty Rest, Reinvented

Introducing the Velura Overnight Repair Mask, a dreamy, luxurious new hero product that works the night shift so your skin wakes up glowing. The mask features Velura's signature fragrance-free barrier complex in a rich, cushiony overnight texture, packaged in a stunning midnight-blue 75ml jar at $42. It is the perfect companion to the beloved Barrier Repair Moisturizer — the final, indulgent step of the evening routine.

Skincare lovers adore a great night mask, and overnight formats are having a wonderful moment. This launch gives the Velura community something genuinely exciting to add to their shelf, and gives new customers a beautiful reason to discover the brand.

---

## Target Audience

Existing Velura buyers and routine-builders under 35 — engaged, ingredient-savvy skincare enthusiasts who already trust the brand or are building a serious evening routine. They love learning about formulas, they follow skincare creators devotedly, and they get real joy from a well-designed routine.

---

## The Big Idea

**"While You Sleep"** romanticizes the eight hours skincare marketing forgets. The creative world is calm, dim, and gorgeous — bedside tables, moonlit bathrooms, the little click of the jar closing — positioning the mask as the most restorative part of the night.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video
Beautiful, soothing nighttime-routine content on Reels and TikTok, where skincare discovery happens. Evening-routine content is enormously popular and endlessly watchable.

### 2. Creator Partnerships
Dermatologists and niche skincare creators receive the mask early and document real results over weeks. Their audiences deeply trust this kind of honest, gradual storytelling.

### 3. CRM & Email to Existing Buyers
A warm launch moment for the existing customer base — early access, routine-pairing guides with the moisturizer, and replenishment-friendly messaging.

### 4. Specialty Retail Activation
Eye-catching shelf displays and swatch-friendly testers at Velura's specialty retail partners, plus staff education kits, so in-store discovery feels as special as the product.
