# Campaign Proposal: "Pass the Bowl" — Sunbrook Crisps Holiday Multipack Retail Media Push

**Client:** Sunbrook Foods
**Prepared by:** Ryo Tanabe, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Version:** Draft v1
**Campaign window:** October 15 – December 31, 2025
**Budget:** $290,000

---

## 1. Opening: The Product

Introducing the Sunbrook Crisps Holiday Multipack — a 6-count variety pack of Sunbrook's most popular flavors, priced at $8.99 and built for the biggest snacking season of the year. The multipack brings together the crunch, flavor variety, and quality ingredients that have made Sunbrook Crisps a Midwest grocery favorite, now in a festive, shelf-ready format designed for parties, family gatherings, and game-day tables.

The packaging is a real standout. The holiday sleeve design uses warm seasonal colors and a resealable carry format that makes it easy to grab for a gathering. Each pack contains six single-serve bags across three flavors, so there is something for every guest. It is a great product at a great price, and this campaign will put it in front of shoppers everywhere they buy their party snacks.

## 2. Target Audience

Our target is grocery shoppers buying party snacks for holiday gatherings. This is a broad and valuable audience. During the holidays, almost everyone hosts or attends gatherings, and snacks are a staple purchase for these occasions. Shoppers in this period are in a festive, generous mood and are open to trading up to convenient formats like multipacks. Young consumers especially love variety packs, and families appreciate the value of buying in bulk for the season.

## 3. The Big Idea: "Pass the Bowl"

Every holiday gathering has a snack bowl at the center of it. "Pass the Bowl" positions the Sunbrook Holiday Multipack as the easiest way to fill that bowl — one grab off the shelf, three flavors, zero decisions. Creative across every touchpoint shows the multipack turning into the shared bowl at the heart of the party: friendsgiving tables, watch parties, cookie exchanges, New Year's countdowns.

The idea is warm, simple, and extremely shoppable. It works as a sponsored product tile, as an end-cap display, and as a 15-second video.

## 4. Channel & Media Plan

We will run a retail-first plan with social support, because the holiday multipack is fundamentally a grocery purchase and retail media is where the shopper decision happens.

### 4.1 Retail Media Networks (primary channel)
Sponsored product placements and onsite display across the retail media networks of Sunbrook's key Midwest grocery chains. Retail media is growing quickly and is very effective for snack brands, since it reaches shoppers at the exact moment they are building their basket. We will run search-triggered sponsored tiles on terms like "party snacks," "chips variety pack," and "holiday snacks," plus category takeover placements in peak weeks (Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's).

### 4.2 In-Store Shopper Marketing
End-cap displays and front-of-store seasonal placements in top-performing stores, timed to the three biggest gathering moments of the season. In-store visibility is critical for impulse party purchases, and a festive display drives strong pickup.

### 4.3 Paid Social (short-form video)
Instagram Reels and TikTok ads showing quick, satisfying "fill the bowl" moments. Short video performs really well with holiday shoppers and keeps Sunbrook top of mind between store trips. We will geo-target the drive markets around Sunbrook's core grocery footprint.

### 4.4 Creator Partnerships
A small roster of Midwest food and entertaining creators producing hosting-hack content featuring the multipack. Creators feel authentic and their audiences trust their recommendations, which makes them a natural fit for a party-occasion product.

## 7. KPIs

- Multipack unit sales lift in participating stores during campaign weeks
- Retail media return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Sponsored placement click-through and add-to-cart rates
- Social video views and engagement rate
- Share of seasonal snack display presence versus last year

## 8. Why This Will Work

The Sunbrook Holiday Multipack is a strong product entering the strongest snacking season of the year. Retail media is one of the most exciting growth channels in marketing right now, and pairing it with festive in-store presence and fun social content gives us coverage across the whole shopper journey. Shoppers love convenience during the holidays, and "Pass the Bowl" makes Sunbrook the most convenient choice on the shelf. With this plan, Sunbrook will own the party snack occasion this season.
