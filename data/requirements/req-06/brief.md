## The setup

We're shipping a **Holiday Multipack** for the first time: a 6-count variety pack of our most popular flavors at **$8.99 per 6-count multipack**, with a festive sleeve design the packaging team has been polishing all summer. Resealable carry format, three flavors per pack, built for parties and gatherings. It looks terrific and the trade buyers like it.

We want a retail-forward push. Our grocery partners keep pitching us their retail media networks and this feels like the right product to lean in with — sponsored placements, onsite display, the works, backed up with in-store presence and some social.

Full disclosure on last year: we tried a smaller multipack test last holiday and it barely moved — the national brands' pallet displays and takeover placements basically boxed us out of the party-snack aisle from Thanksgiving onward. Lesson learned on going in undersized. But this year's product is much stronger and the new sleeve really pops, so mostly I just want it visible everywhere baskets are being built.

## Target

Grocery shoppers buying party snacks for holiday gatherings. Hosts, guests, game-day people, office-party organizers — everybody's buying snack bowls from November to New Year's.

## Budget & timeline

- **Budget:** $290,000
- **Window:** 2025-10-15 to 2025-12-31 — covering Thanksgiving, the December run-up, and New Year's

## Success

Two numbers: **retail media ROAS of at least 3.0x**, and **multipack unit sales at least 20% above our seasonal baseline** in participating stores during campaign weeks. Hit those and the multipack becomes a permanent seasonal SKU.

- Flight plan phased around the three big gathering moments
