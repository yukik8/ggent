# Blue Fork Kitchen Brand Refresh Announcement Campaign — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Blue Fork Kitchen
**Campaign window:** March 1 – May 15, 2027
**Budget:** $200,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Same Fork, Fresh Look" — the Blue Fork Refresh Reveal**

---

## Opening: A Gorgeous New Era for Blue Fork

Introducing the brand-new Blue Fork — a stunning, joyful refresh of everything guests see and touch. The new identity is absolutely lovely: a warmer blue, a hand-drawn fork mark with real personality, playful new illustrations across packaging and cups, refreshed interior palettes rolling out across all 85 locations, and a menu design that finally feels as fresh as the food. Nothing about the bowls is changing, and neither are the prices — the beloved $12.90 average check stays right where it is. This is purely a glow-up, and it's a beautiful one.

A refresh like this is such an exciting moment for a brand. It's a chance to remind everyone why they fell for Blue Fork in the first place, to give loyal regulars something delightful to notice on their next visit, and to make the brand feel vibrant and current for anyone who hasn't looked twice in a while. "Same Fork, Fresh Look" turns the rollout itself into the campaign — a warm, confident reveal across every touchpoint guests already love.

---

## Target Audience

Everyone who already knows Blue Fork! Our primary audience is the full guest base across all 85 locations — the regulars, the app members, the office lunch crowds, and the weekend families. They don't need to be convinced to love Blue Fork; they just need to hear, clearly and warmly, that the new look is still their Blue Fork. A secondary audience is the broader local community around each location, for whom the fresh look is a lovely reason to give the brand a first or second glance.

---

## The Big Idea

**"Same Fork, Fresh Look"** celebrates change without losing the familiar. The creative is a joyful before-and-after: the same beloved bowl, the same friendly team, the same fair price — dressed in a gorgeous new outfit. We reveal the identity piece by piece across the flight (the mark, the cups, the interiors, the bags), treating each element like a little gift to unwrap, and we let guests in on the story behind the design choices with charming behind-the-scenes content from the brand team.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. Paid Social — Short-Form Video (Instagram Reels & TikTok)
Reveal films, before-and-after transitions, and behind-the-design stories, geo-targeted around all 85 locations. Transformation content is wonderfully watchable, and the refresh gives us a whole season of it.

### 2. In-Store Reveal & Merchandising
The restaurants are the real stage. Reveal-day window treatments, new-look launch signage, cup-sleeve messages explaining the refresh, and team celebration kits so every location feels the moment on the same day.

### 3. CRM — Email, Push & In-App
A warm announcement flow for members: the story of the new look, a sneak peek before reveal day, and a little celebratory bonus-points gift on the first post-refresh visit. Our members should feel like insiders, not bystanders.

### 4. Local PR & Community Moments
Local press outreach in key metros, a founder-voice story about why the brand grew into its new look, and small community reveal moments — first-look mornings with free coffee — at flagship locations.

## Why Blue Fork Wins

A brand refresh can feel corporate, or it can feel like a celebration — and this one deserves to be celebrated. "Same Fork, Fresh Look" wraps the new identity in warmth and familiarity, gives regulars a delightful moment of discovery, and rolls out the new era with the same friendly confidence guests taste in every bowl. The fork is the same. The look is fabulous.
