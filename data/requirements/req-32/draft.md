## Campaign Title

**"The Connected Close" — the ERP-Integration Co-Marketing Program**

---

## Opening: Ledgerline, Now Speaking Fluent ERP

Introducing the Ledgerline ERP-integration co-marketing program, the most exciting expansion of the Ledgerline story yet. Ledgerline has built beautiful, certified, two-way integrations with the leading mid-market ERP platforms — NetSuite, Sage Intacct, and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central — and they are genuinely impressive. Invoices flow straight into the general ledger, purchase orders match automatically, approvals sync in real time, and reconciliation that used to take an afternoon happens with a single click. It is a seamless, elegant experience, and it deserves a campaign as polished as the product.

The big unlock here is co-marketing. Instead of Ledgerline telling its own story alone, we team up with the ERP partners themselves — joint webinars, co-branded content, shared audiences, marketplace visibility. ERP vendors love showcasing great ecosystem apps, and Ledgerline is exactly the kind of app they want to show off. At a $24,000 average annual contract value, every deal this program influences is meaningful revenue, and the integration story gives sales a fantastic new door-opener.

Many finance directors worry about whether a new AP tool will really play nicely with their ERP, and this program tackles that head-on by putting Ledgerline and the ERP brands side by side. It is a natural fit, a win-win for everyone, and a genuinely fresh way to reach buyers who are already deep in the ERP world.

---

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is finance directors evaluating ERP-connected AP tools. These are busy, detail-oriented leaders at 50-500 employee companies who live inside their ERP every day and are naturally cautious about bolting anything new onto it. They do a lot of research before talking to sales, they trust peers and analysts more than ads, and they respond well to proof over promises. LinkedIn is a big part of their professional world, and webinars are a popular format in this space.

Secondary audiences include controllers and AP managers who influence the evaluation, and ERP consultants and implementation partners who often recommend add-on tools to their clients.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The Connected Close"** turns the integration itself into the hero. Rather than pitching features, we show real month-end closes running through Ledgerline and the ERP together — one connected motion from invoice to ledger. The centerpiece is **Connected Close Live**, a co-hosted webinar series where a Ledgerline customer and their ERP partner walk through an actual close, screens and all. Around it we build a library of co-branded close stories, integration demo films, and partner-endorsed content that makes "Ledgerline + your ERP" feel like the obvious, safe, modern choice.

---

## Channel & Media Plan

### 1. LinkedIn Paid & ABM
LinkedIn is where finance decision-makers spend their professional time, so it will carry our co-branded awareness and demand flight. We will run sponsored content featuring the close stories and demo films, plus a targeted ABM layer against named accounts running our partner ERPs. B2B social is very effective for this kind of considered purchase.

### 2. Co-Hosted Webinar Series ("Connected Close Live")
Webinars are a great format for finance audiences, and co-hosting with ERP partners means we inherit their credibility and their audience lists. We will run a monthly episode from March through June, each anchored on a real customer close, with the ERP partner co-presenting and co-promoting to their own base.

### 3. Peer Review & Case-Study Syndication
Finance buyers do a lot of homework on peer review sites, so we will invest in a strong presence there — sponsored placement on relevant category pages, refreshed integration-specific reviews, and syndication of our co-branded case studies to the research destinations buyers already use.

### 4. Email Nurture & Lifecycle Program
Everyone who touches the campaign — webinar registrants, content downloaders, review-site clickers — enters a co-branded nurture stream tailored to their ERP. Email remains a workhorse channel in B2B, and a well-timed follow-up keeps Ledgerline top of mind through a long evaluation.
