## Campaign Title

**"First Paycheck Energy"**

## Opening

Stackwise is the app built to catch a first salary and do something smart with it: automated savings buckets, spending insights, and a premium tier at a transparent $8/month. For new graduates, it is the starter kit for adult money, and graduation season is the natural window to introduce it. From May through July, cap-toss edits, first-paycheck reactions, and adulting humor dominate young audiences' feeds, and Stackwise belongs inside those moments.

## Tactics

### 1. Graduation-Trend Social Blitz
A rolling calendar of trend-reactive TikTok and Reels content: first-paycheck reaction formats, "things they don't teach you in college" series, payday memes, and duets with graduation content as it surfaces. Volume and speed keep the brand present through the whole seasonal wave.

### 2. New-Grad Creator Squad
A roster of early-career and adulting creators shows their real first-paycheck breakdowns in the app — relatable, screenshot-friendly, group-chat-proof content that reads as advice from a peer rather than an ad.

### 3. Campus & Commencement OOH
Posters and transit placements around 20 major campuses during commencement weeks, plus geo-targeted mobile ads at graduation venues, so the brand is physically present at the milestone itself.

### 4. Paid Social Retargeting
Follow-up flights converting trend-content viewers into installs with a "set up in five minutes" promise that removes the last excuse to put it off.

## Target Audience

New college graduates starting their first salaried jobs — moving cities, receiving first direct deposits, and forming money habits in real time. They distrust finance-bro content but respond to humor and honesty from people their age. Industry reporting suggests most young adults settle on their core money apps within their first year of full-time work, which makes this window the one that matters.

## KPIs

- Impression volume and engagement on graduation-trend content
- Creator engagement and organic shares
- Install lift in campus metros during commencement weeks
- Install-to-signup conversion from retargeting flights

## Why This Will Work

Graduation is the single biggest money-habit reset of young adulthood, and it arrives with a built-in content wave every May. Trend-reactive social keeps Stackwise in the feed, the creator squad makes it credible, commencement OOH puts it at the milestone, and retargeting closes the loop — a full-funnel push timed to the exact weeks new grads decide what to do with that first paycheck.
