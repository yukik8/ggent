# Stackwise New-Grad First-Paycheck Onboarding Push — DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Stackwise
**Campaign window:** May 1 – August 15, 2028
**Budget:** $230,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"First Paycheck Energy"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Stackwise new-grad push: the app that catches your very first salary and actually does something smart with it. Stackwise offers automated savings buckets, spending insights, and a premium tier at a transparent $8/month — the perfect starter kit for adult money. Graduation season is a content goldmine: cap-toss edits, "first paycheck reaction" videos, dorm-to-apartment transitions, and adulting humor all spike from May through July. Stackwise should be stitched into every one of those moments.

---

## Tactics

### 1. Graduation-Trend Social Blitz
A rolling calendar of trend-reactive TikTok and Reels content: first-paycheck reaction formats, "things they don't teach you in college" series, payday memes, and duets with graduation content as it surfaces.

### 2. New-Grad Creator Squad
A roster of early-career and adulting creators shows their real first-paycheck breakdowns in the app. Relatable, screenshot-friendly, group-chat-proof content.

### 3. Campus & Commencement OOH
Posters and transit placements around 20 major campuses during commencement weeks, plus geo-targeted mobile ads at graduation venues.

### 4. Paid Social Retargeting
Follow-up flights converting trend-content viewers into installs with a "set up in five minutes" promise.

---

## Target Audience

New college graduates starting their first salaried jobs — moving cities, getting first direct deposits, and forming money habits in real time. They distrust finance-bro content but respond to humor and honesty from people their age.
