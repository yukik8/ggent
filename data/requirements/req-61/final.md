## Campaign Title

**"Row It Forward"**

---

## Opening

Introducing Row It Forward, a summer-long push for Kinetiq's member referral program with a $200 credit for both sides of every referral. Referral is one of the strongest growth channels in consumer subscription, and word of mouth remains the form of marketing people trust most. Row It Forward wraps Kinetiq's referral offer in a summer of shareable moments: leaderboards, member spotlight reels, a bring-a-friend class format, and a UGC challenge that turns every member's rowing story into an invitation.

---

## The Big Idea

Rowing is better with your people. Row It Forward makes referring feel less like forwarding a promo code and more like inviting someone into a club you love. The $200-each-way credit is generous and clean — you get $200, your friend gets $200 off their rower — and the campaign gives members a dozen good reasons to bring it up all summer. Industry reporting consistently finds that referred customers retain better than customers acquired through paid channels, which makes each referral worth more than the credit it costs.

---

## Tactics

### 1. Referral Leaderboard & Tiers
A summer leaderboard inside the app with tiers: one referral earns a limited badge, three earn exclusive swag, and five earn a year of membership free. Tiered rewards give the program momentum beyond the first referral.

### 2. Member Spotlight Reels
Short-form videos spotlighting real members and their rowing stories, posted on Kinetiq's channels and cut for members to reshare. Authentic member stories double as referral ammunition.

### 3. Bring-a-Friend Live Classes
A recurring live class format where members row "with" an invited friend who joins the class from the app on a free guest pass. The guest pass ends with the $200 referral offer.

### 4. UGC Summer Challenge
A hashtag challenge — post your summer rowing streak and tag a friend who should be on the leaderboard. The challenge keeps the program in feeds without heavy media spend.

### 5. In-App & Email Referral Promotion
Persistent referral placements in the app, a monthly email rhythm with leaderboard standings, and triggered sends after milestone workouts, when member enthusiasm peaks.

### 6. Paid Social Amplification
Paid support behind the best member reels and challenge content to keep the program visible beyond the existing follower base.

---

## Target Audience

Active Kinetiq members completing eight or more workouts per month — the members who love the product most, talk about it anyway, and are most likely to have friends asking about the rower in the corner. Their enthusiasm is the media channel.

## KPIs

- Referral volume and referral-driven rower sales over the flight
- Leaderboard participation and tier completion rates
- Guest-pass class attendance and conversion to the referral offer
- UGC challenge post volume and reach
- Cost per referred sale versus paid acquisition

## Why This Will Work

Kinetiq's happiest members are already its best salespeople; Row It Forward gives them the tools, the moments, and a genuinely great offer. With a leaderboard, spotlight reels, guest passes, and a UGC challenge built around a $200-each-way credit, the program can carry Kinetiq's summer acquisition at a cost per sale paid media cannot match.
