**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Kinetiq Home
**Campaign window:** June 1 – September 30, 2026
**Budget:** $230,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"Row It Forward"**

---

## Opening

Introducing Row It Forward, a summer-long push for Kinetiq's member referral program with a $200 credit for both sides of every referral. Referral is the hottest growth channel in consumer subscription right now — everyone from fintech to meal kits is leaning into member-get-member, and word of mouth is basically the only marketing people fully trust anymore. Row It Forward wraps Kinetiq's referral offer in a summer of shareable moments: leaderboards, member spotlight reels, a bring-a-friend class format, and a UGC challenge that turns every member's rowing story into an invitation.

---

## The Big Idea

Rowing is better with your people. Row It Forward makes referring feel less like forwarding a promo code and more like inviting someone into a club you love. The $200-each-way credit is generous and clean — you get $200, your friend gets $200 off their rower — and the campaign gives members a dozen fun excuses to bring it up all summer.

---

## Tactics

### 1. Referral Leaderboard & Tiers
A summer leaderboard inside the app with tiers: one referral gets a limited badge, three gets exclusive swag, five gets a year of membership free. Leaderboards and tiered drops are everywhere in consumer apps right now because they work.

### 2. Member Spotlight Reels
Short-form videos spotlighting real members and their rowing stories, posted on Kinetiq's channels and cut for members to reshare. Authentic member stories double as referral ammunition.

### 3. Bring-a-Friend Live Classes
A recurring live class format where members row "with" an invited friend who joins the class from the app on a free guest pass. The guest pass ends with the $200 referral offer.

### 4. UGC Summer Challenge
A hashtag challenge — post your summer rowing streak, tag a friend who should be on the leaderboard. UGC challenges keep the program in feeds without heavy media spend.

### 5. In-App & Email Referral Promotion
Persistent referral placements in the app, a monthly email rhythm with leaderboard standings, and triggered sends after milestone workouts when member enthusiasm peaks.

### 6. Paid Social Amplification
Paid support behind the best member reels and challenge content to keep the program visible beyond the existing follower base.

---

## Target Audience

Active Kinetiq members completing eight or more workouts per month — the members who love the product most, talk about it anyway, and are most likely to have friends asking about the rower in the corner. Their enthusiasm is the media channel.
