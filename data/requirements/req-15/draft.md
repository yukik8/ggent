# Proposal Draft: "Golden Rails" — Cascade Valley Fall Foliage Rail-and-Trail Campaign

**Client:** Cascade Valley Tourism Board
**Prepared by:** Emma Lindqvist, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Campaign window:** August 15 – November 15, 2026
**Budget:** $330,000

---

## Opening

Introducing the Cascade Valley Rail-and-Trail Day Pass, an exciting new way to experience one of the most beautiful fall foliage displays in the region. For just $49 per person, visitors get a full day of scenic heritage rail travel paired with curated hiking and biking trail access, all timed to peak leaf season. The pass combines the nostalgia of a vintage rail journey with the freedom of the valley's expanding trail network, and it comes with a beautifully designed foliage map, trailhead shuttle access, and partner discounts at cafes and outfitters along the route.

This is a genuinely special product. The rail line winds directly through the valley's most photogenic corridors, the trail junctions are positioned at the best overlooks, and the whole experience has been designed to be endlessly shareable. Our campaign, **"Golden Rails,"** will bring this one-of-a-kind autumn experience to young travelers everywhere and make Cascade Valley the fall trip people talk about this year.

## Target Audience

Our primary audience is travelers under 35 who love discovering trips through social content. Young people today are constantly scrolling for their next getaway, and travel video is hugely popular with this group. They want experiences that look great on camera, feel authentic, and don't require a plane ticket. Many of them live within driving distance of the valley and are looking for exactly this kind of easy, memorable weekend escape.

Secondary audiences include young couples planning a fall "leaf-peeping" date trip and small friend groups organizing group outings.

## The Big Idea

**"Golden Rails: Ride Into the Color."**

We position the Rail-and-Trail Day Pass as the ultimate cinematic autumn experience, a trip that practically films itself. The creative centers on the moment the train rounds a bend and the valley opens up in full color, then follows riders as they hop off at trailheads to hike, bike, and capture the season. Every asset ends on the same simple promise: one pass, one golden day.

The campaign hero is a series of short, atmospheric films shot at golden hour along the rail line, cut for vertical viewing, paired with a wave of creator-made content that shows real trips as they actually happen.

## Channel & Media Plan

1. **Short-form video (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)** — the core of the plan. Short video is where young travelers live, and this kind of scenic content performs extremely well on these platforms. We run a mix of polished hero films and raw, in-the-moment clips.

2. **Creator partnerships** — we partner with travel and outdoors creators to ride the rails and document their day. Creators are very influential with this audience and their content feels more trustworthy than traditional ads.

3. **Paid social amplification** — targeted paid support behind the best-performing organic content within our drive-market radius.

4. **Landing page & booking flow** — a dedicated "Golden Rails" page with a live foliage tracker, itinerary builder, and direct pass purchase.

5. **On-the-ground activation** — branded photo moments at two flagship overlooks and a "Golden Ticket" surprise-and-delight program on select departures.

## KPIs

- Rail-and-Trail Day Pass sales during the campaign window
- Video views and completion rates across platforms
- Engagement rate on creator content
- Landing page traffic and pass-purchase conversion
- Growth in Cascade Valley's social following
- Earned media pickups and user-generated content volume

## Why This Will Work

Fall foliage content is beloved on social media, and Cascade Valley's rail-and-trail combination is truly unique in the region. Young consumers love authentic, experience-driven travel, and short-form video is a very effective way to reach them. By pairing a stunning hero campaign with a strong creator program and smart paid support, "Golden Rails" will put the valley in front of the right people at exactly the right moment of the year, when everyone is dreaming about autumn.

The product is beautiful, the timing is perfect, and the story tells itself. We're confident this campaign will make the 2026 foliage season Cascade Valley's most visible yet.
