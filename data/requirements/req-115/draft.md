# Cascade Valley Spring Wellness Retreat Campaign — Proposal DRAFT

**Prepared by:** Marcus Bell, Junior Planner, Meridian & Craft
**Client:** Cascade Valley Tourism Board
**Campaign window:** February 15 – May 31, 2028
**Budget:** $320,000

---

## Campaign Title

**"The Great Exhale"**

---

## Opening

Introducing the Cascade Valley Spring Wellness Retreat, a curated weekend package around $450 per couple that bundles lodging, guided forest-bathing walks, riverside yoga, spa access, and a farm-to-table dinner. Wellness travel is one of the hottest trends in the industry right now — sleep tourism, digital detox, quiet luxury, cold plunges, all of it is having a moment. Cascade Valley can own this wave in the drive market with a retreat product that looks incredible on camera and feels even better in person.

---

## Tactic Stack

We hit this from every angle that is working right now:

1. **Short-form video (TikTok, Reels).** ASMR-style nature sound content, "silent walking" trend tie-ins, and POV retreat mornings. Calm content is huge.
2. **Hosted wellness creator stays.** We bring wellness and lifestyle creators in for comped retreat weekends and let them document the full experience — this is the playbook every trending destination is running.
3. **Podcast & audio sponsorships.** Mindfulness and wellness podcasts are booming; host-read spots put the retreat inside the exact headspace we want to own.
4. **Paid search & display.** Capture "wellness retreat near me" and spring getaway searches.
5. **Email & landing page.** A "Great Exhale" booking page plus sends to the tourism board's list with a calm, minimal aesthetic.

---

## Target Audience

Burned-out professionals aged 28-45 within drive-market range. They are stressed, over-scheduled, and increasingly spending on recovery experiences. Many follow wellness creators already, which is why the creator-stay tactic matters.

---

## The Big Idea

**"The Great Exhale"** frames Cascade Valley as the place where the drive market goes to breathe out. Every asset uses slow pacing, natural sound, and one repeated visual motif — a long exhale as the valley opens up through the windshield.

---

## More Tactics to Layer In

- **Spotify audio ads** with nature soundscapes and a whispered CTA
- **A "48-Hour Reset" itinerary carousel** creators and the board can both post
- **Micro-influencer seeding** of retreat gift boxes (candle, trail map, tea)
