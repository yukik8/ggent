# Campaign Strategy: Premium Bottled Matcha Launch

## Target Audience
Women in their 50s represent a health-conscious demographic with strong
purchasing power. They value quality, authenticity, and wellness benefits.

## Key Strategy

**1. Emphasize Health Benefits**
Highlight matcha's antioxidants, L-theanine, and metabolism support.
Position the product as a daily wellness ritual.

**2. Premium Positioning**
Use elegant packaging shots, traditional tea-ceremony imagery, and
craftsmanship storytelling to justify the premium price point.

**3. TV Creative Direction**
A serene 30-second spot: a sophisticated woman enjoying a calm morning
moment. Warm lighting, Japanese aesthetics, quality-of-life framing.

**4. Media Plan**
Prime-time and morning information programs index well against F50
viewers. Complement with YouTube pre-roll for reach extension.

**5. Launch Timing**
Align with new-year health resolutions or spring wellness season for
maximum receptivity.

## Success Metrics
Brand awareness lift, purchase intent, and trial rate among the target
demographic.
